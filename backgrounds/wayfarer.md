# Wayfarer

**Source:** Player's Handbook (2024)

## Overview

You grew up on the streets, surviving by your wits and learning to fend for yourself. You might have been an orphan, a runaway, or simply someone for whom circumstances never quite aligned. You wandered from place to place, picking up odd jobs, learning the ways of different communities, and discovering that luck — whether good or bad — has a way of finding you.

Choose A or B:
- **(A)** 2 Daggers, Thieves' Tools, Gaming Set (any), Bedroll, 2 Pouches, Traveler's Clothes, 16 GP
- **(B)** 50 GP

## Origin Feat: Lucky

**Origin Feat:** [Lucky](../feats/origin/lucky.md) — Gain Luck Points equal to your Proficiency Bonus (recharge on Long Rest). Spend to gain Advantage on your D20 Tests or impose Disadvantage on attacks against you.

New background in the 2024 PHB. No 2014 equivalent. Urchin was the closest thematic predecessor.
