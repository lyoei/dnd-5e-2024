# Wayfarer

**Source:** Player's Handbook (2024)

## Overview

You grew up on the streets, surviving by your wits and learning to fend for yourself. You might have been an orphan, a runaway, or simply someone for whom circumstances never quite aligned. You wandered from place to place, picking up odd jobs, learning the ways of different communities, and discovering that luck — whether good or bad — has a way of finding you.

## Mechanical Benefits

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Ability Scores** | Dexterity, Wisdom, Charisma (choose +2/+1 or +1/+1/+1) |
| **Skill Proficiencies** | Insight, Stealth |
| **Tool Proficiency** | Thieves' Tools |
| **Origin Feat** | Lucky |

## Equipment

Choose A or B:
- **(A)** 2 Daggers, Thieves' Tools, Gaming Set (any), Bedroll, 2 Pouches, Traveler's Clothes, 16 GP
- **(B)** 50 GP

## Origin Feat: Lucky

You gain the following benefits:

- **Luck Points.** You have a number of Luck Points equal to your Proficiency Bonus and can spend them on the benefits below. You regain your expended Luck Points when you finish a Long Rest.
- **Advantage.** When you roll a d20 for a D20 Test, you can spend 1 Luck Point to give yourself Advantage on the roll.
- **Disadvantage.** When a creature rolls a d20 for an attack roll against you, you can spend 1 Luck Point to impose Disadvantage on that roll.

## 2024 Changes

- **New Background:** Wayfarer is new to the 2024 PHB. It did not exist in the 2014 PHB (Urchin was the closest equivalent).
