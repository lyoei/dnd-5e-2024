# Guard

**Source:** Player's Handbook (2024)

## Overview

You served as a guard, protecting those around you from danger. Perhaps you watched over a noble estate, a busy marketplace, a quiet village, or the walls of a city. Whatever your post, you were there to maintain order and keep people safe.

## Mechanical Benefits

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Ability Scores** | Strength, Intelligence, Wisdom (choose +2/+1 or +1/+1/+1) |
| **Skill Proficiencies** | Athletics, Perception |
| **Tool Proficiency** | Choose one kind of Gaming Set |
| **Origin Feat** | [Alert](../feats/origin/alert.md) |

## Equipment

Choose A or B:
- **(A)** Spear, Light Crossbow, 20 Bolts, Gaming Set (same as above), Hooded Lantern, Manacles, Quiver, Traveler's Clothes, 12 GP
- **(B)** 50 GP

## Origin Feat: Alert

**Origin Feat:** [Alert](../feats/origin/alert.md) — Add Proficiency Bonus to Initiative rolls and swap Initiative with a willing ally.

## 2024 Changes

New background in the 2024 PHB. No 2014 equivalent. Soldier was the closest thematic predecessor for a martial/watchkeeping background.
