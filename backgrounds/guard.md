# Guard

**Source:** Player's Handbook (2024)

## Overview

You served as a guard, protecting those around you from danger. Perhaps you watched over a noble estate, a busy marketplace, a quiet village, or the walls of a city. Whatever your post, you were there to maintain order and keep people safe.

## Mechanical Benefits

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Ability Scores** | Strength, Intelligence, Wisdom (choose +2/+1 or +1/+1/+1) |
| **Skill Proficiencies** | Athletics, Perception |
| **Tool Proficiency** | Choose one kind of Gaming Set |
| **Origin Feat** | Alert |

## Equipment

Choose A or B:
- **(A)** Spear, Light Crossbow, 20 Bolts, Gaming Set (same as above), Hooded Lantern, Manacles, Quiver, Traveler's Clothes, 12 GP
- **(B)** 50 GP

## Origin Feat: Alert

You gain the following benefits:

- **Initiative Proficiency.** When you roll Initiative, you can add your Proficiency Bonus to the roll.
- **Initiative Swap.** Immediately after you roll Initiative, you can swap your Initiative with one willing ally in the same combat. You can't make this swap if you or the ally is Incapacitated.

## 2024 Changes

- **New Background:** Guard is new to the 2024 PHB. It did not exist in the 2014 PHB (Soldier was the closest equivalent for a martial/watchkeeping background).
