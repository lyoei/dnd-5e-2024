# Scribe

**Source:** Player's Handbook (2024)

## Overview

You spent formative years in a scriptorium, copying books and learning a great deal about the subjects you were transcribing. You might have worked in a monastery, a government office, a merchant house, or a library. The work was often tedious, but it sharpened your eye for detail and gave you a broad understanding of many topics.
