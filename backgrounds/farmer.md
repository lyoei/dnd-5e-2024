# Farmer

**Source:** Player's Handbook (2024)

## Overview

You grew up close to the land. Years of tending the fields, growing crops, minding livestock, and fixing fences built a strength and resilience in you. You might have worked a small family farm or labored on a vast estate.

## Mechanical Benefits

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Ability Scores** | Strength, Constitution, Wisdom (choose +2/+1 or +1/+1/+1) |
| **Skill Proficiencies** | Animal Handling, Nature |
| **Tool Proficiency** | Carpenter's Tools |
| **Origin Feat** | Tough |

## Equipment

Choose A or B:
- **(A)** Sickle, Carpenter's Tools, Healer's Kit, Iron Pot, Shovel, Traveler's Clothes, 30 GP
- **(B)** 50 GP

## Origin Feat: Tough

Your Hit Point maximum increases by an amount equal to twice your character level when you gain this feat. Whenever you gain a level thereafter, your Hit Point maximum increases by an additional 2 Hit Points.

## 2024 Changes

- **New Background:** Farmer is new to the 2024 PHB. It did not exist in the 2014 PHB (Folk Hero was the closest equivalent).
