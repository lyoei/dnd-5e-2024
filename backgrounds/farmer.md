# Farmer

**Source:** Player's Handbook (2024)

## Overview

You grew up close to the land. Years of tending the fields, growing crops, minding livestock, and fixing fences built a strength and resilience in you. You might have worked a small family farm or labored on a vast estate.

## Mechanical Benefits

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Ability Scores** | Strength, Constitution, Wisdom (choose +2/+1 or +1/+1/+1) |
| **Skill Proficiencies** | Animal Handling, Nature |
| **Tool Proficiency** | Carpenter's Tools |
| **Origin Feat** | [Tough](../feats/origin/tough.md) |
