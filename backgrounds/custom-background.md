# Custom Background

**Source:** Player's Handbook (2024)

## Overview

The 2024 PHB allows you to create a custom background if none of the 16 listed backgrounds fit your character concept. A custom background lets you mix and match background components to represent any origin story you can imagine.

## Rules

When creating a custom background, you choose each of the following components independently:

### Ability Scores

Choose **any three** ability scores. Increase one by **+2** and another by **+1**, or increase all three by **+1**. No score can exceed 20 from this increase.

Unlike the listed backgrounds (which lock you into three thematically chosen scores), a custom background lets you pick any three of the six ability scores.

### Skill Proficiencies

Choose **any two** skill proficiencies.

### Tool Proficiency

Choose **one** tool proficiency — either a specific tool or one from the Artisan's Tools category.

### Origin Feat

Choose **any one** Origin feat. The Origin feats available in the 2024 PHB are:

| Feat | Summary |
|---|---|
| [Alert](../feats/origin/alert.md) | Add Proficiency Bonus to Initiative; swap Initiative with an ally |
| [Crafter](../feats/origin/crafter.md) | Faster/cheaper crafting, 20% shop discount, 3 tool proficiencies |
| [Healer](../feats/origin/healer.md) | Battle Medic (2d6+2 HP), reroll 1s on healing dice |
| [Lucky](../feats/origin/lucky.md) | Luck Points for Advantage/Disadvantage manipulation |
| [Magic Initiate](../feats/origin/magic-initiate.md) | 2 cantrips + 1 first-level spell from Cleric, Druid, or Wizard list |
| [Musician](../feats/origin/musician.md) | 3 instrument proficiencies, give Heroic Inspiration after rests |
| [Savage Attacker](../feats/origin/savage-attacker.md) | Roll weapon damage twice, use either result (once per turn) |
| [Skilled](../feats/origin/skilled.md) | 3 additional skill or tool proficiencies |
| [Tavern Brawler](../feats/origin/tavern-brawler.md) | Enhanced unarmed strikes, shove, furniture weapons |
| [Tough](../feats/origin/tough.md) | +2 HP per level (retroactive) |

### Equipment

Choose one of:
- **(A)** 50 GP to buy your own starting equipment
- **(B)** A thematically appropriate equipment package (work with your DM)

## Using Older Backgrounds

The 2024 PHB includes a sidebar ("Backgrounds and Species from Older Books") confirming you can still use backgrounds from earlier 5e books. When doing so:

1. **Ability Scores:** Choose any three ability scores for your +2/+1 (or +1/+1/+1) — use the custom background rules above.
2. **Origin Feat:** If the old background doesn't provide a feat, choose any Origin feat from the list above.
3. **Tool Proficiency:** If the old background doesn't grant a tool proficiency, choose one.
4. Everything else (skills, flavor, etc.) works as written in the original source.

## Tips for Custom Backgrounds

- **Optimize freely:** Custom backgrounds let you put your ASIs wherever you want, making them mechanically superior for min-maxing. A custom background Wizard can take Int/Int/Con (putting +2 Int, +1 Con) regardless of narrative.
- **Talk to your DM:** Some tables prefer players use the listed 16 backgrounds for consistency. Check before assuming custom is allowed.
- **Write a narrative:** Even though you're picking mechanics à la carte, write a short backstory paragraph to ground your choices in fiction.

## 2024 Changes

Custom backgrounds are a formalized version of the variant rule that existed in the 2014 PHB, but significantly expanded. In 2014, custom backgrounds only let you swap skill proficiencies and tool/language proficiencies. The 2024 version adds full control over ability scores and Origin Feat selection, making it a true build-your-own system.
