# Merchant

**Source:** Player's Handbook (2024)

## Overview

You were involved in the world of commerce, whether as a traveling trader, a shopkeeper, or a member of a trading company. You know how to evaluate goods, negotiate deals, and manage logistics. You've traveled trade routes, dealt with suppliers and customers alike, and learned that fortune favors the bold — and the lucky.

## Origin Feat: Lucky

**Origin Feat:** [Lucky](../feats/origin/lucky.md) — Gain Luck Points equal to your Proficiency Bonus (recharge on Long Rest). Spend to gain Advantage on your D20 Tests or impose Disadvantage on attacks against you.

## 2024 Changes

New background in the 2024 PHB. No 2014 equivalent. Guild Merchant (a variant of Guild Artisan) was the closest thematic predecessor.
