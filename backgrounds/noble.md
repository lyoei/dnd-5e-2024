# Noble

**Source:** Player's Handbook (2024)

## Overview

You were raised in a castle or manor among the privileged elite. Your family had wealth, power, and influence, and you were educated among tutors and instructors who prepared you for a life of leadership and courtly intrigue. You learned etiquette, history, and the art of persuasion, acquiring a well-rounded set of skills.

## Mechanical Benefits

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Ability Scores** | Strength, Intelligence, Charisma (choose +2/+1 or +1/+1/+1) |
| **Skill Proficiencies** | History, Persuasion |
| **Tool Proficiency** | Choose one kind of Gaming Set |
| **Origin Feat** | [Skilled](../feats/origin/skilled.md) |

## Equipment

Choose A or B:
- **(A)** Gaming Set (same as above), Fine Clothes, Perfume, 29 GP
- **(B)** 50 GP

## Origin Feat: Skilled

**Origin Feat:** [Skilled](../feats/origin/skilled.md) — Gain proficiency in any combination of three skills or tools of your choice.

## 2024 Changes

- **Ability Scores:** Now grants ability score increases (Str/Int/Cha). Previously, backgrounds did not grant ASIs.
- **Origin Feat:** Gains the Skilled feat. Previously, backgrounds did not grant feats.
- **Skill Proficiencies:** History and Persuasion, unchanged from 2014.
- **Tool Proficiency:** One Gaming Set, unchanged from 2014.
- **Removed:** Position of Privilege feature, personality traits, ideals, bonds, and flaws tables, and language proficiencies are no longer part of backgrounds.
- **Equipment:** Updated equipment package and GP values.
