# Criminal

**Source:** Player's Handbook (2024)

## Overview

You eked out a living in dark alleys, cutting purses or burglarizing shops. Perhaps you were part of a small gang of like-minded criminals who looked out for each other. Or maybe you were a lone wolf, fending for yourself against the world's dangers and the law's long arm.

## Mechanical Benefits

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Ability Scores** | Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence (choose +2/+1 or +1/+1/+1) |
| **Skill Proficiencies** | Sleight of Hand, Stealth |
| **Tool Proficiency** | Thieves' Tools |
| **Origin Feat** | [Alert](../feats/origin/alert.md) |

## Equipment

Choose A or B:
- **(A)** 2 Daggers, Thieves' Tools, Crowbar, 2 Pouches, Traveler's Clothes, 16 GP
- **(B)** 50 GP

## Origin Feat: Alert

**Origin Feat:** [Alert](../feats/origin/alert.md) — Add Proficiency Bonus to Initiative rolls and swap Initiative with a willing ally.

## 2024 Changes

- **Ability Scores:** Now grants ability score increases (Dex/Con/Int). Previously, backgrounds did not grant ASIs.
- **Origin Feat:** Gains the Alert feat. Previously, backgrounds did not grant feats.
- **Skill Proficiencies:** Changed from Deception and Stealth (2014) to Sleight of Hand and Stealth.
- **Tool Proficiency:** Thieves' Tools, unchanged from 2014.
- **Removed:** Criminal Contact feature, personality traits, ideals, bonds, and flaws tables, and language proficiencies are no longer part of backgrounds.
- **Equipment:** Updated equipment package and GP values.
