# Weapons

The Weapons table shows the game's main weapons. Each weapon belongs to a category (Simple or Martial) and is classified as Melee or Ranged. Every weapon now also has a **Mastery** property — a new feature in the 2024 rules.

## Weapon Properties

| Property | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Ammunition** | You can make a ranged attack only if you have ammunition to fire. Each attack expends one piece. Drawing ammunition is part of the attack (free hand needed for one-handed weapons). After a fight, spend 1 minute to recover half the ammunition used. |
| **Finesse** | Use your choice of Strength or Dexterity modifier for attack and damage rolls. Must use the same modifier for both. |
| **Heavy** | Disadvantage on attack rolls if it's a Melee weapon and your Strength < 13, or a Ranged weapon and your Dexterity < 13. |
| **Light** | When you attack with a Light weapon using the Attack action, you can make one extra attack as a Bonus Action with a different Light weapon. Don't add your ability modifier to the Bonus Action's damage unless negative. |
| **Loading** | You can fire only one piece of ammunition per action, Bonus Action, or Reaction. |
| **Range** | Two numbers: normal range and long range (in feet). Attacks beyond normal range have Disadvantage. Can't attack beyond long range. |
| **Reach** | Adds 5 feet to your reach for attacks and Opportunity Attacks. |
| **Thrown** | You can throw the weapon for a ranged attack and draw it as part of the attack. Melee weapons use the same ability modifier as melee attacks. |
| **Two-Handed** | Requires two hands to attack. |
| **Versatile** | Can be used with one or two hands. The parenthetical damage value is used when wielded two-handed for melee attacks. |

## 2024 Changes

Key changes from the 2014 PHB:

- **Weapon Mastery (NEW):** Every weapon now has a Mastery property (Cleave, Graze, Nick, Push, Sap, Slow, Topple, Vex). This is the biggest addition — martial classes can unlock these for tactical combat bonuses. See [weapon-mastery.md](weapon-mastery.md) for full details.
- **Light property reworked:** The Light property now explicitly grants an extra Bonus Action attack with a different Light weapon (previously this was in the Two-Weapon Fighting rules rather than the property itself).
- **Heavy property reworked:** Now uses a Strength/Dexterity score threshold of 13 instead of the Small creature size restriction from 2014.
- **Firearms (Musket & Pistol):** Now included in the base PHB weapons table rather than being DMG-optional.
- **Ammunition recovery:** Explicitly stated as half (rounded down) after 1 minute post-fight.
- **Lance:** Clarified as Two-Handed unless mounted (previously had the Special property with different wording).
