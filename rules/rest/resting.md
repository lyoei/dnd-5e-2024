# Resting

> Source: 2024 Player's Handbook

## Short Rest

A Short Rest is a period of at least **1 hour**, during which a character does nothing more strenuous than eating, drinking, reading, and tending to wounds.

### What You Can Do During a Short Rest

- **Spend Hit Dice.** A character can spend one or more **Hit Dice** at the end of a Short Rest. For each Hit Die spent, the player rolls the die and adds the character's **Constitution modifier** to it. The character regains Hit Points equal to the total (minimum of 0). The player can decide to spend an additional Hit Die after each roll.

### What Recharges on a Short Rest

Many class features and some species traits recharge on a Short Rest:
- **Orc: Adrenaline Rush** uses
- **Warlock: Pact Magic** spell slots (Warlock-specific mechanic)
- **Fighter: Action Surge** and **Second Wind**
- **Monk: Focus Points** (formerly Ki Points)
- **Bard: Bardic Inspiration** (at certain levels)
- Various subclass features

**2024 Change:** Short Rest duration is unchanged at 1 hour. However, many abilities that previously recharged only on a Short Rest now also work on a "PB uses per Long Rest" model. The 2024 PHB shifts more abilities to Long Rest recharge with Proficiency Bonus scaling.

## Long Rest

A Long Rest is a period of extended downtime, at least **8 hours** long, during which a character sleeps for at least 6 hours and performs no more than 2 hours of light activity (reading, talking, eating, standing watch).

### Benefits of a Long Rest

When a character finishes a Long Rest, they gain the following benefits:

1. **Regain All Hit Points.** The character's current Hit Points are restored to their Hit Point maximum.

2. **Regain Hit Dice.** The character regains spent **Hit Dice**, up to a number equal to **half their total number of Hit Dice** (minimum of one die). For example, a level 8 character regains up to 4 spent Hit Dice.

3. **Regain All Spell Slots.** All expended spell slots are restored.

4. **Remove 1 Level of Exhaustion.** If the character has any Exhaustion levels, **one level** is removed (provided the character had food and drink during the day).

5. **Other Recharges.** Most class features, species traits, and item charges that specify "you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest" are restored.

### Long Rest Restrictions

- A character **can't benefit from more than one Long Rest in a 24-hour period**.
- A character must have **at least 1 Hit Point** at the start of the rest to gain its benefits.

### Interruptions

If a Long Rest is interrupted by a period of **strenuous activity** — at least 1 hour of walking, fighting, casting spells, or similar — the character must begin the rest again to gain any benefit from it.

**2024 Change:** A short combat encounter (less than 1 hour total of strenuous activity) no longer necessarily interrupts a Long Rest. The threshold for interruption is now 1 hour of strenuous activity, not any combat at all.

## Hit Dice Recovery

| Rest Type | Hit Dice Recovery |
|---|---|
| **Short Rest** | Spend Hit Dice to heal (they are not regained) |
| **Long Rest** | Regain spent Hit Dice equal to **half your total** (minimum 1) |

**2024 Change:** Hit Dice recovery on Long Rest is unchanged from 2014 (half total, minimum 1). However, the 2024 rules now **fully restore HP on a Long Rest** without requiring Hit Dice expenditure. In 2014, characters also fully healed on a Long Rest, so this is mechanically unchanged.

---

## Trance (Elves)

Elves don't need to sleep. They can finish a Long Rest in **4 hours** if they spend those hours in a trancelike meditation, during which they retain consciousness.

## 2024 Resting Changes Summary

| Change | 2014 | 2024 |
|---|---|---|
| Short Rest duration | 1 hour | 1 hour (unchanged) |
| Long Rest duration | 8 hours (6 sleep + 2 light) | 8 hours (unchanged) |
| HP recovery on Long Rest | Full HP | Full HP (unchanged) |
| Hit Dice recovery on Long Rest | Half total (min 1) | Half total (min 1; unchanged) |
| Long Rest interruption | Any combat interrupts | 1 hour of strenuous activity to interrupt |
| Exhaustion removal | 1 level per Long Rest | 1 level per Long Rest (unchanged) |
| Ability recharge trend | Mix of Short/Long Rest | More abilities → Long Rest + PB uses |
| Potion drinking | Action (or DM houserule) | Utilize action (formally defined) |
