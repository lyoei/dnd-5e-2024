# Action Economy

> Source: 2024 Player's Handbook

## Overview

On your turn in combat, you can:
- **Move** up to your Speed
- Take **one Action**
- Take **one Bonus Action** (if available)
- Take **one Reaction** (on your turn or someone else's)
- **Free interactions** (brief communication, interact with one object, draw/stow one weapon as part of Attack action)

## Actions

### Attack

Attack with a weapon or an **Unarmed Strike**. Some features, such as the fighter's **Extra Attack**, allow you to make more than one attack with this action.

When you take the Attack action, you can replace one of your attacks with an Unarmed Strike option (Grapple or Shove) or, if eligible, with a Breath Weapon or similar species/class feature.

**2024 Change:** Drawing or stowing one weapon can now be done as part of each attack within the Attack action.

### Cast a Spell → Magic

> Renamed to **Magic** in 2024.

Use the Magic action to cast a spell, use a magic item, or use a magical feature. This replaces the 2014 "Cast a Spell" action.

**2024 Change:** Any action described as a "Magic action" is inherently magical and doesn't function in an Antimagic Field.

### Dash

For the rest of the turn, give yourself **extra movement equal to your Speed** (after any modifiers). If you have multiple movement speeds (e.g., Fly, Burrow), you can choose which speed to use each time you Dash.

**2024 Change:** Clarified that you can choose which movement speed to use when Dashing.

### Disengage

Your movement doesn't provoke **Opportunity Attacks** for the rest of the turn.

### Dodge

Until the start of your next turn:
- Attack rolls against you have **Disadvantage** (if you can see the attacker)
- You make **Dexterity saving throws** with **Advantage**

You lose this benefit if you have the Incapacitated condition or if your Speed is 0.

### Help

Help another creature's ability check or attack roll, or administer first aid.

**2024 Changes:**
- To help with an **ability check**, you must be proficient in the skill or tool being used. The target must be able to hear or see you and must be within 10 feet of you. They gain Advantage on the next ability check they make with that skill or tool before the start of your next turn.
- To help with an **attack roll**, you feint, distract, or otherwise hamper a target within 5 feet of you. The next attack roll against that target by an ally has Advantage, provided the attack is made before the start of your next turn.
- **First Aid:** You can use the Help action to try to stabilize a creature with 0 HP, making a DC 10 Wisdom (Medicine) check.

### Hide

Make a **Dexterity (Stealth)** check against a DC of **15** (base DC) to try to become Hidden.

**While Hidden:**
- You are not visible to creatures you are hiding from
- You have Advantage on attack rolls against creatures that can't see you
- Attack rolls against you have Disadvantage

**2024 Change:** Base DC of 15 for hiding is new. In 2014, the DC was typically set by a creature's passive Perception.

### Influence (NEW in 2024)

Make a social ability check to alter a creature's attitude.

Use **Charisma** (Deception, Intimidation, Performance, or Persuasion) or **Wisdom** (Animal Handling) to influence a creature. The DM determines the creature's reaction:

| Reaction | Result |
|---|---|
| **Willing** | No check needed; creature complies (in its own way) |
| **Unwilling** | Automatic failure; creature refuses |
| **Hesitant** | DC = 15 or creature's Intelligence score (whichever is higher). Success = creature complies. Failure = can't try the same approach for 24 hours |

### Magic (NEW in 2024)

Cast a spell, use a magic item, or use a magical feature.

This replaces the 2014 "Cast a Spell" action and is broader — it covers any use of magic, whether from a spell, a magic item, or a class/species feature described as a "Magic action."

### Ready

Prepare to take an action in response to a trigger you define.

- You describe the trigger and the action you'll take in response.
- When the trigger occurs, you can use your **Reaction** to take the readied action, or ignore the trigger.
- If you ready a spell, you cast it on your turn (expending the spell slot) and hold its energy with **Concentration**. If your Concentration is broken, the spell is lost.

### Search

Make a **Wisdom** check using one of the following skills: **Insight**, **Medicine**, **Perception**, or **Survival**.

**2024 Change:** Search is now a formally defined action. In 2014 it was informal.

### Study (NEW in 2024)

Make an **Intelligence** check using one of the following skills: **Arcana**, **History**, **Investigation**, **Nature**, or **Religion**.

Use this to recall lore, examine clues, or deduce information.

### Utilize (NEW in 2024)

Use a **nonmagical object**.

This covers actions like drinking a potion (nonmagical), using a tool, interacting with an environmental object, etc. Drawing a weapon is NOT a Utilize action (it's part of the Attack action).

**2024 Change:** Replaces the 2014 "Use an Object" action with a clearer name and scope.

## Bonus Actions

You can take **one Bonus Action** per turn, but only if a special ability, spell, or feature says you can do something as a Bonus Action. You otherwise don't have a Bonus Action to take.

You choose when to take a Bonus Action during your turn unless its timing is specified. Anything that deprives you of your ability to take actions also prevents you from taking a Bonus Action.

**Common Bonus Actions include:**
- Offhand attack (Two-Weapon Fighting / Nick Weapon Mastery)
- Cunning Action (Rogue)
- Rage (Barbarian)
- Some spells (e.g., *Misty Step*, *Healing Word*, *Spiritual Weapon*)
- Species abilities (e.g., Dragonborn Draconic Flight, Orc Adrenaline Rush)

---

## Reactions

A Reaction is an instant response to a trigger. You can take **one Reaction** between each of your turns. When you take a Reaction, you can't take another until the start of your next turn.

If the Reaction interrupts another creature's turn, that creature can continue its turn right after the Reaction.

**Common Reactions:**
- **Opportunity Attack:** When a hostile creature you can see moves out of your reach, you can use your Reaction to make **one melee attack** against it. (2024 change: now only triggers on voluntary movement, not forced movement like Shove.)
- **Readied Action:** Take a previously readied action in response to its trigger.
- *Shield*, *Counterspell*, *Absorb Elements* (spells)
- **Stone's Endurance** (Goliath), **Storm's Thunder** (Goliath)

## 2024 Action Economy Changes Summary

| Change | 2014 | 2024 |
|---|---|---|
| "Cast a Spell" | Action name | Replaced by **Magic** |
| "Use an Object" | Action name | Replaced by **Utilize** |
| New actions | — | **Influence**, **Study**, **Magic**, **Utilize** |
| Hide DC | Passive Perception | Base DC 15 |
| Help (ability check) | Always works | Requires skill/tool proficiency |
| Weapon draw/stow | 1 free interaction | Part of each attack in Attack action |
| Opportunity Attacks | Any leaving reach | Only voluntary movement |
| Potions | Unclear | Utilize action (or Magic for magical potions) |
