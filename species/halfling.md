# Halfling

> Source: 2024 Player's Handbook

Cherished and guided by gods who value life, home, and hearth, halflings gravitate toward bucolic havens where family and community help shape their lives. Their size — similar to that of a human child — helps them pass through crowds unnoticed and slip through tight spaces. Halflings live about 150 years.

## Traits

| Trait | Value |
|---|---|
| **Creature Type** | Humanoid |
| **Size** | Small (about 2–3 feet tall) |
| **Speed** | 30 feet |
| **Life Span** | About 150 years |

### Brave

You have Advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the **Frightened** condition.

### Halfling Nimbleness

You can move through the space of any creature that is a size larger than you, but you can't stop in the same space.

### Luck

When you roll a **1** on the d20 of a D20 Test, you can reroll the die, and you must use the new roll.

### Naturally Stealthy

You can take the **Hide** action even when you are obscured only by a creature that is at least one size larger than you.

## 2024 Changes (vs. 2014)

- **No subraces.** Lightfoot and Stout halfling subraces are removed.
- **Speed increased** from 25 feet to **30 feet**.
- **Naturally Stealthy** (was Lightfoot only) now available to all halflings.
- **Brave** unchanged — Advantage vs. Frightened.
- **Luck** unchanged — reroll natural 1s on d20 tests.
- **Halfling Nimbleness** slightly changed — now works with any creature larger than you (was specifically "larger" size category), and clarified you can't stop in their space.
- **Stout Resilience** (poison resistance from Stout subrace) removed.
- **No fixed ability score increases.** ASIs come from backgrounds.
