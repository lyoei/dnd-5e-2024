# Dwarf

> Source: 2024 Player's Handbook

Dwarves were raised from the earth in the elder days by a deity of the forge (Moradin, Reorx, and others). That god gave dwarves an affinity for stone and metal and for living underground, making them resilient like the mountains, with a life span of about 350 years. Squat and often bearded, dwarves carved cities and strongholds into mountainsides and under the earth.

## Traits

| Trait | Value |
|---|---|
| **Creature Type** | Humanoid |
| **Size** | Medium (about 4–5 feet tall) |
| **Speed** | 30 feet |
| **Life Span** | About 350 years |

### Darkvision

You have Darkvision with a range of **120 feet**.

### Dwarven Resilience

You have Resistance to **Poison** damage. You also have Advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the **Poisoned** condition.

### Dwarven Toughness

Your Hit Point maximum increases by **1**, and it increases by **1** again whenever you gain a level.

### Stonecunning

As a **Bonus Action**, you gain **Tremorsense** with a range of 60 feet for 10 minutes. You must be on a stone surface or touching a stone surface to use this Tremorsense. The stone can be natural or worked.

You can use this Bonus Action a number of times equal to your **Proficiency Bonus**, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a **Long Rest**.

## 2024 Changes (vs. 2014)

- **No subraces.** Hill Dwarf and Mountain Dwarf subraces are removed. All dwarves get the same traits.
- **Speed increased** from 25 feet to **30 feet** (dwarves are no longer slower than other species).
- **Darkvision** increased from 60 feet to **120 feet**.
- **Dwarven Toughness** (was Hill Dwarf only) is now available to all dwarves. Grants +1 HP per level.
- **Stonecunning** completely reworked — was a History check bonus on stonework, now grants **Tremorsense** (60 ft, 10 min, bonus action, PB uses/long rest). A massive combat upgrade.
- **Dwarven Combat Training** (weapon proficiencies) removed — weapon proficiency now comes from classes/backgrounds.
- **Tool Proficiency** (smith's tools, brewer's supplies, etc.) removed.
- **No fixed ability score increases.** ASIs come from backgrounds.
- **Dwarven Resilience** unchanged (Poison resistance + advantage vs. Poisoned).
