# Dragonborn

> Source: 2024 Player's Handbook

The ancestors of dragonborn hatched from the eggs of chromatic and metallic dragons. Dragonborn look like wingless, bipedal dragons — scaly, bright-eyed, and thick-boned with horns on their heads — and their coloration and other features are reminiscent of their draconic ancestors.

## Traits

| Trait | Value |
|---|---|
| **Creature Type** | Humanoid |
| **Size** | Medium (about 5–7 feet tall) |
| **Speed** | 30 feet |

### Draconic Ancestry

Your lineage stems from a dragon progenitor. Choose the kind of dragon from the table below. Your choice affects your Breath Weapon and Damage Resistance traits as well as your appearance.

| Dragon | Damage Type |
|---|---|
| Black | Acid |
| Blue | Lightning |
| Brass | Fire |
| Bronze | Lightning |
| Copper | Acid |
| Gold | Fire |
| Green | Poison |
| Red | Fire |
| Silver | Cold |
| White | Cold |

### Breath Weapon

When you take the **Attack action** on your turn, you can replace one of your attacks with an exhalation of magical energy in either a **15-foot Cone** or a **30-foot Line** that is 5 feet wide (choose the shape each time).

Each creature in that area must make a **Dexterity saving throw** (DC = 8 + your Constitution modifier + your Proficiency Bonus). On a failed save, a creature takes **1d10** damage of the type determined by your Draconic Ancestry. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage.

**Damage scaling:** 1d10 → 2d10 (level 5) → 3d10 (level 11) → 4d10 (level 17).

You can use this Breath Weapon a number of times equal to your **Proficiency Bonus**, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a **Long Rest**.

### Damage Resistance

You have Resistance to the damage type determined by your Draconic Ancestry trait.

### Darkvision

You have Darkvision with a range of **60 feet**.

### Draconic Flight (Level 5)

When you reach character level 5, you can channel draconic magic to give yourself temporary flight. As a **Bonus Action**, you sprout spectral wings on your back that last for **10 minutes** or until you retract the wings (no action required) or have the Incapacitated condition. During that time, you have a **Fly Speed** equal to your Speed. Your wings appear to be made of the same energy as your Breath Weapon. Once you use this trait, you can't use it again until you finish a **Long Rest**.

## 2024 Changes (vs. 2014)

- **Breath Weapon massively improved.** Now replaces one attack within the Attack action (works with Extra Attack), uses per long rest equal to Proficiency Bonus (was 1/short rest), and scales with d10s instead of d6s. Can choose 15-foot Cone OR 30-foot Line each time (was locked to one shape by ancestry).
- **Draconic Flight** is brand new at level 5 — dragonborn never had flight before.
- **Darkvision** added (2014 dragonborn lacked it).
- **Damage Resistance** retained from 2014 but unchanged.
- **No subraces.** 2014 had no subraces either (Fizban's added Chromatic/Metallic/Gem, which are replaced by this unified version).
- **No fixed ability score increases.** ASIs come from backgrounds.
- **Breath Weapon save** changed from Dex or Con (depending on ancestry shape) to always Dexterity.
