# Elf

> Source: 2024 Player's Handbook

Created by the god Corellon, elves have pointed ears and lack facial and body hair. They live for around 750 years and don't sleep but instead enter a trance when they need to rest, remaining aware of their surroundings while immersing themselves in memories and meditations.

An environment subtly transforms elves after they inhabit it for a millennium or more, granting them certain kinds of magic. **Drow**, **High Elves**, and **Wood Elves** are examples of elves who have been transformed thus.

## Traits

| Trait | Value |
|---|---|
| **Creature Type** | Humanoid |
| **Size** | Medium (about 5–6 feet tall) |
| **Speed** | 30 feet |
| **Life Span** | About 750 years |

### Darkvision

You have Darkvision with a range of **60 feet**.

### Elven Lineage

You are part of an elven lineage that grants you supernatural abilities. Choose one of the following lineages. Whatever lineage you choose, Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spells you cast with this trait (choose the ability when you select the lineage):

#### Drow

The magic of the Underdark has transformed your ancestors. You know the *Dancing Lights* cantrip.

Starting at level 3, you always have *Faerie Fire* prepared. Starting at level 5, you always have *Darkness* prepared. You can cast each of these spells without a spell slot once per Long Rest. You can also cast them using any spell slots you have of the appropriate level.

#### High Elf

You have been infused with the magic of crossings between the Feywild and the Material Plane. You know the *Prestidigitation* cantrip.

Starting at level 3, you always have *Detect Magic* prepared. Starting at level 5, you always have *Misty Step* prepared. You can cast each of these spells without a spell slot once per Long Rest. You can also cast them using any spell slots you have of the appropriate level.

#### Wood Elf

You carry the magic of primeval forests within you. Your Speed increases to **35 feet**. You know the *Druidcraft* cantrip.

Starting at level 3, you always have *Longstrider* prepared. Starting at level 5, you always have *Pass without Trace* prepared. You can cast each of these spells without a spell slot once per Long Rest. You can also cast them using any spell slots you have of the appropriate level.

### Fey Ancestry

You have Advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the **Charmed** condition.

### Keen Senses

You have proficiency in the **Insight**, **Perception**, or **Survival** skill.

### Trance

You don't need to sleep, and magic can't put you to sleep. You can finish a **Long Rest** in 4 hours if you spend those hours in a trancelike meditation, during which you retain consciousness.

## 2024 Changes (vs. 2014)

- **Subraces → Lineages.** Drow, High Elf, and Wood Elf are now "Elven Lineages" rather than subraces. Each grants a cantrip plus scaling spells at levels 3 and 5.
- **Drow** lineage significantly improved — gets *Dancing Lights*, *Faerie Fire* (level 3), and *Darkness* (level 5), all castable once free + with spell slots. No longer has Sunlight Sensitivity.
- **High Elf** gets *Prestidigitation*, *Detect Magic* (level 3), *Misty Step* (level 5) — a massive upgrade from just one wizard cantrip.
- **Wood Elf** gets *Druidcraft*, *Longstrider* (level 3), *Pass without Trace* (level 5), plus 35 ft speed.
- **Keen Senses** changed from automatic Perception proficiency to a choice of Insight, Perception, or Survival.
- **Fey Ancestry** simplified — just Advantage vs. Charmed (no longer includes immunity to magical sleep, since Trance covers that separately).
- **Trance** now explicitly lets you finish a Long Rest in 4 hours.
- **Elf Weapon Training** (longsword, shortsword, etc.) removed.
- **No fixed ability score increases.** ASIs come from backgrounds.
