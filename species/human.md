# Human

> Source: 2024 Player's Handbook

Found throughout the multiverse, humans are as varied as they are numerous, and they endeavor to achieve as much as they can in the years they are given. Their ambition and resourcefulness are commended, respected, and feared on many worlds. Humans are as diverse in appearance as the people of Earth, and they have many gods.

## Traits

| Trait | Value |
|---|---|
| **Creature Type** | Humanoid |
| **Size** | Medium (about 4–7 feet tall) or Small (about 2–4 feet tall), chosen when you select this species |
| **Speed** | 30 feet |

### Resourceful

You gain **Heroic Inspiration** whenever you finish a **Long Rest**.

### Skillful

You gain proficiency in **one skill** of your choice.

### Versatile

You gain an **Origin Feat** of your choice. *Skilled* is recommended.

## 2024 Changes (vs. 2014)

- **Completely redesigned.** The 2014 Human had +1 to all ability scores (or the Variant Human with +1/+1, a skill, and a feat). Both versions are replaced.
- **Resourceful** is new — guarantees Heroic Inspiration (the 2024 replacement for the old Inspiration mechanic) after every Long Rest. This is unique and powerful.
- **Skillful** replaces the old +1 to all stats or the Variant Human's extra skill — now just one skill proficiency.
- **Versatile** grants a free **Origin Feat**, effectively making all humans "Variant Humans" but better — Origin Feats include Alert, Healer, Lucky, Magic Initiate, Musician, Savage Attacker, Skilled, Tavern Brawler, Tough, etc.
- **Size flexibility.** Can now choose Small or Medium.
- **No fixed ability score increases.** ASIs come from backgrounds.
- **No extra languages.** Languages come from backgrounds.
