# Warlock

> *Source: Player's Handbook (2024)*

Warlocks quest for knowledge that lies hidden in the fabric of the multiverse. Drawing on the ancient knowledge of beings such as angels, archfey, demons, devils, hags, and alien entities of the Far Realm, Warlocks piece together arcane secrets to bolster their own power.

## Class Feature Descriptions

### Level 1: Eldritch Invocations

You gain one Eldritch Invocation of your choice (such as Pact of the Tome). Invocations may have prerequisites. Whenever you gain a Warlock level, you can replace one invocation with another you qualify for. You gain more invocations at higher levels as shown in the table.

### Level 1: Pact Magic

You cast Warlock spells using Charisma as your spellcasting ability. You can use an Arcane Focus as a Spellcasting Focus.

- **Cantrips:** You know two Warlock cantrips (Eldritch Blast and Prestidigitation recommended). You learn another at levels 4 and 10.
- **Spell Slots:** Pact Magic slots are all the same level and recharge on a **Short or Long Rest**. See table for slot count and level.
- **Prepared Spells:** You prepare Warlock spells. You can replace one spell whenever you gain a Warlock level.

### Level 2: Magical Cunning

You can perform an esoteric rite for 1 minute. At the end, you regain expended Pact Magic spell slots but no more than half your maximum (round up). Once used, you can't do so again until you finish a Long Rest.

### Level 3: Warlock Subclass

You gain a Warlock subclass: Archfey Patron, Celestial Patron, Fiend Patron, or Great Old One Patron.

### Level 4: Ability Score Improvement

You gain the Ability Score Improvement feat or another feat of your choice. You gain this feature again at Warlock levels 8, 12, and 16.

### Level 9: Contact Patron

You always have Contact Other Plane prepared. You can cast it without expending a spell slot to contact your patron, and you automatically succeed on the spell's saving throw.

Once you cast it this way, you can't do so again until you finish a Long Rest.

### Level 11: Mystic Arcanum

Choose one level 6 Warlock spell as an arcanum. You can cast it once without expending a spell slot, regaining the ability on a Long Rest.

You gain additional arcanum spells at levels 13 (level 7), 15 (level 8), and 17 (level 9). Whenever you gain a Warlock level, you can replace one arcanum spell with another Warlock spell of the same level.

### Level 19: Epic Boon

You gain an Epic Boon feat or another feat of your choice. Boon of Fate is recommended.

### Level 20: Eldritch Master

When you use your Magical Cunning feature, you regain **all** your expended Pact Magic spell slots.

## Subclasses

### Archfey Patron

*Bargain with Whimsical Fey*

Your pact draws on the power of the Feywild. You might make a deal with an archfey such as the Prince of Frost, the Queen of Air and Darkness, Titania of the Summer Court, or an ancient hag.

#### Level 3: Archfey Spells

| Warlock Level | Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | Calm Emotions, Faerie Fire, Misty Step, Phantasmal Force, Sleep |
| 5 | Blink, Plant Growth |
| 7 | Dominate Beast, Greater Invisibility |
| 9 | Dominate Person, Seeming |

#### Level 3: Steps of the Fey

You can cast Misty Step without expending a spell slot a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum once), regaining all uses on a Long Rest.

Whenever you cast Misty Step, choose one additional effect:

- **Refreshing Step.** Immediately after you teleport, you or one creature you can see within 10 feet gains 1d10 Temporary Hit Points.
- **Taunting Step.** Creatures within 5 feet of the space you left must succeed on a Wisdom save or have Disadvantage on attack rolls against creatures other than you until the start of your next turn.

#### Level 6: Misty Escape

You can cast Misty Step as a Reaction in response to taking damage.

Additional Steps of the Fey options:

- **Disappearing Step.** You have the Invisible condition until the start of your next turn or until you attack, deal damage, or cast a spell.
- **Dreadful Step.** Creatures within 5 feet of the space you left or appeared in (your choice) must succeed on a Wisdom save or take 2d10 Psychic damage.

#### Level 10: Beguiling Defenses

You are immune to the Charmed condition.

When a creature you can see hits you with an attack roll, you can take a Reaction to reduce the damage by half (round down) and force the attacker to make a Wisdom save. On a failure, it takes Psychic damage equal to the damage you take. Once used, you can't use this Reaction again until you finish a Long Rest unless you expend a Pact Magic spell slot.

#### Level 14: Bewitching Magic

Immediately after you cast an Enchantment or Illusion spell using an action and a spell slot, you can cast Misty Step as part of the same action without expending a spell slot.

### Celestial Patron

*Call on the Power of the Heavens*

Your pact draws on the Upper Planes. You might enter an agreement with an empyrean, couatl, sphinx, unicorn, or another heavenly entity.

#### Level 3: Celestial Spells

| Warlock Level | Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | Aid, Cure Wounds, Guiding Bolt, Lesser Restoration, Light, Sacred Flame |
| 5 | Daylight, Revivify |
| 7 | Guardian of Faith, Wall of Fire |
| 9 | Greater Restoration, Summon Celestial |

#### Level 3: Healing Light

You have a pool of d6s equal to 1 + your Warlock level. As a Bonus Action, heal yourself or one creature you can see within 60 feet by expending dice from the pool (max dice per use = your Charisma modifier, minimum 1). Roll the expended dice and restore HP equal to the total. The pool regains all dice on a Long Rest.

#### Level 6: Radiant Soul

You have Resistance to Radiant damage. Once per turn, when a spell you cast deals Radiant or Fire damage, you can add your Charisma modifier to that spell's damage against one target.

#### Level 10: Celestial Resilience

You gain Temporary Hit Points whenever you use your Magical Cunning feature or finish a Short or Long Rest. The Temporary HP equal your Warlock level + your Charisma modifier. Additionally, choose up to five creatures you can see; they each gain Temporary HP equal to half your Warlock level + your Charisma modifier.

#### Level 14: Searing Vengeance

When you or an ally within 60 feet is about to make a Death Saving Throw, you can unleash radiant energy. The creature regains HP equal to half its HP maximum and can end the Prone condition on itself. Each creature of your choice within 30 feet of that creature takes 2d8 + your Charisma modifier Radiant damage and has the Blinded condition until the end of the current turn.

Once used, you can't use it again until you finish a Long Rest.

### Fiend Patron

*Make a Deal with the Lower Planes*

Your pact draws on the Lower Planes. You might forge a bargain with a demon lord, archdevil, pit fiend, balor, yugoloth, or night hag.

#### Level 3: Dark One's Blessing

When you reduce an enemy to 0 HP, you gain Temporary HP equal to your Charisma modifier + your Warlock level (minimum 1). You also gain this benefit if someone else reduces an enemy within 10 feet of you to 0 HP.

#### Level 3: Fiend Spells

| Warlock Level | Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | Burning Hands, Command, Scorching Ray, Suggestion |
| 5 | Fireball, Stinking Cloud |
| 7 | Fire Shield, Wall of Fire |
| 9 | Geas, Insect Plague |

#### Level 6: Dark One's Own Luck

When you make an ability check or a saving throw, you can add 1d10 to your roll (after seeing the roll but before effects occur). You can use this a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum once), no more than once per roll. All uses regain on a Long Rest.

#### Level 10: Fiendish Resilience

Choose one damage type (other than Force) whenever you finish a Short or Long Rest. You have Resistance to that damage type until you choose a different one.

#### Level 14: Hurl Through Hell

Once per turn when you hit a creature with an attack roll, the target must succeed on a Charisma save against your spell save DC, or it disappears and hurtles through the Lower Planes. The target takes 8d10 Psychic damage (if not a Fiend) and has the Incapacitated condition until the end of your next turn, when it returns.

Once used, you can't use it again until you finish a Long Rest unless you expend a Pact Magic spell slot.

### Great Old One Patron

*Unearth Forbidden Lore of Ineffable Beings*

You might bind yourself to Tharizdun, Zargon, Hadar, Great Cthulhu, or invoke several entities without yoking yourself to one.

#### Level 3: Awakened Mind

As a Bonus Action, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet. You and the chosen creature can communicate telepathically while within a number of miles equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum 1 mile). To understand each other, you each must mentally use a language the other knows. The connection lasts for a number of minutes equal to your Warlock level. It ends early if you connect with a different creature.

#### Level 3: Great Old One Spells

| Warlock Level | Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | Detect Thoughts, Dissonant Whispers, Phantasmal Force, Tasha's Hideous Laughter |
| 5 | Clairvoyance, Hunger of Hadar |
| 7 | Confusion, Summon Aberration |
| 9 | Modify Memory, Telekinesis |

#### Level 3: Psychic Spells

When you cast a Warlock spell that deals damage, you can change its damage type to Psychic. When you cast a Warlock Enchantment or Illusion spell, you can do so without Verbal or Somatic components.

#### Level 6: Clairvoyant Combatant

When you form a telepathic bond with a creature using Awakened Mind, you can force it to make a Wisdom save against your spell save DC. On a failure, the creature has Disadvantage on attack rolls against you, and you have Advantage on attack rolls against it for the duration of the bond.

Once used, you can't use it again until you finish a Short or Long Rest unless you expend a Pact Magic spell slot.

#### Level 10: Eldritch Hex

You always have the Hex spell prepared. When you cast Hex and choose an ability, the target also has Disadvantage on saving throws of the chosen ability for the duration.

#### Level 10: Thought Shield

Your thoughts can't be read by telepathy or other means unless you allow it. You have Resistance to Psychic damage, and whenever a creature deals Psychic damage to you, that creature takes the same amount of damage.

#### Level 14: Create Thrall

When you cast Summon Aberration, you can modify it so that it doesn't require Concentration. If you do so, the spell's duration becomes 1 minute, and when summoned, the Aberration has Temporary HP equal to your Warlock level + your Charisma modifier.

The first time each turn the Aberration hits a creature under your Hex, the Aberration deals extra Psychic damage equal to Hex's bonus damage.

## 2024 Changes (vs. 2014 PHB)

- **Eldritch Invocations** start at level 1 (previously level 2). Invocation progression is more generous (up to 10 at level 18+).
- **Pact Boons** (Pact of the Chain, Blade, Tome) are now Eldritch Invocations rather than a separate class feature. This means you choose a Pact Boon as your first invocation at level 1.
- **Pact Magic** now uses **prepared spells** instead of "spells known."
- **Magical Cunning** (level 2) is a new feature allowing Warlocks to recover half their Pact Magic slots once per Long Rest via a 1-minute rite.
- **Contact Patron** (level 9) is a new feature granting free casting of Contact Other Plane.
- **Spell Slots:** Warlocks gain a 3rd Pact Magic slot at level 11 (previously stayed at 2 until level 11, then jumped to 3). They gain a 4th slot at level 17.
- **Mystic Arcanum** is retained but now allows replacing arcanum spells on level-up.
- **Eldritch Master** (level 20) now upgrades Magical Cunning to restore all Pact Magic slots (previously was a 1-minute rite to regain all slots once per Long Rest).
- **Subclasses** now start at level 3 (previously level 1). Subclass features at levels 3, 6, 10, 14.
- **Archfey Patron:** Steps of the Fey is entirely new (free Misty Step with rider effects). Misty Escape redesigned. Beguiling Defenses reworked. Bewitching Magic is new.
- **Celestial Patron:** Previously from Xanathar's Guide; now core PHB. Healing Light, Radiant Soul, Celestial Resilience, and Searing Vengeance all updated.
- **Fiend Patron:** Dark One's Blessing updated (now also triggers when an ally kills within 10 ft). Dark One's Own Luck uses Charisma modifier for number of uses. Hurl Through Hell now uses Charisma save and can be recharged with a Pact Magic slot.
- **Great Old One:** Awakened Mind redesigned. Clairvoyant Combatant, Psychic Spells, Eldritch Hex, and Create Thrall are all new or heavily redesigned features.
- Four ASIs (levels 4, 8, 12, 16) plus Epic Boon at 19.
