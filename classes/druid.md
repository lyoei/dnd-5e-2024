# Druid

> *Source: 2024 Player's Handbook*

Druids belong to ancient orders that call on the forces of nature. Harnessing the magic of animals, plants, and the four elements, Druids heal, transform into animals, and wield elemental destruction.

## Class Overview

| Trait | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Wisdom |
| **Hit Point Die** | d8 per Druid level |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Intelligence and Wisdom |
| **Skill Proficiencies** | Choose 2: Arcana, Animal Handling, Insight, Medicine, Nature, Perception, Religion, or Survival |
| **Weapon Proficiencies** | Simple weapons |
| **Tool Proficiencies** | Herbalism Kit |
| **Armor Training** | Light armor and Shields |
| **Starting Equipment** | (A) Leather Armor, Shield, Sickle, Druidic Focus (Quarterstaff), Explorer's Pack, Herbalism Kit, and 9 GP; or (B) 50 GP |

## Class Features

### Level 1: Spellcasting

Full caster using the Druid spell list. Wisdom is the spellcasting ability. Druidic Focus as focus.

- **Cantrips.** Start with 2. Gain extras at levels 4 and 10.
- **Prepared Spells.** Choose from full Druid spell list. Can change entire prepared list on Long Rest.

### Level 1: Druidic

You know Druidic (secret language of Druids). You always have **Speak with Animals** prepared.

Can leave hidden messages in Druidic; others need DC 15 Intelligence (Investigation) to detect (can't decipher without magic).

### Level 1: Primal Order

Choose one sacred role:

- **Magician.** One extra Druid cantrip. Bonus to Intelligence (Arcana or Nature) checks equal to Wisdom modifier (minimum +1).
- **Warden.** Proficiency with Martial weapons and training with Medium armor.

### Level 2: Wild Shape

As a **Bonus Action**, shape-shift into a known Beast form. Duration: hours equal to half Druid level. Can end early as a Bonus Action.

- **Uses.** Start with 2. Regain 1 on Short Rest, all on Long Rest. Increases per table.
- **Known Forms.** Start with 4 forms (max CR 1/4, no Fly Speed). Increases:

| Druid Level | Known Forms | Max CR | Fly Speed |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2 | 4 | 1/4 | No |
| 4 | 6 | 1/2 | No |
| 8 | 8 | 1 | Yes |

- Can swap one known form on each Long Rest.

**Rules While Shape-Shifted:**
- **Temporary HP.** Gain Temp HP equal to your Druid level.
- **Game Statistics.** Replaced by Beast stat block, but retain: creature type, HP, Hit Dice, Int/Wis/Cha, class features, languages, feats, skill/save proficiencies (use higher modifier).
- **No Spellcasting.** Can't cast spells (doesn't break existing Concentration).
- **Equipment.** Choose: falls, merges, or is worn (DM decides practicality).

### Level 2: Wild Companion

As a Magic action, expend a spell slot or Wild Shape use to cast **Find Familiar** without Material components. The familiar is Fey and disappears on Long Rest.

### Level 3: Druid Subclass

Choose a subclass (see below).

### Level 4: Ability Score Improvement

ASI feat or another qualifying feat. Repeats at levels 8, 12, and 16.

### Level 5: Wild Resurgence

- Once per turn, if you have no Wild Shape uses left, expend a spell slot to regain one use (no action).
- Can expend one Wild Shape use (no action) to regain a **level 1 spell slot** (once per Long Rest).

### Level 7: Elemental Fury

Choose one:

- **Potent Spellcasting.** Add Wisdom modifier to Druid cantrip damage.
- **Primal Strike.** Once per turn, when hitting with a weapon or Beast form attack, deal extra **1d8** Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder damage (choose each hit).

### Level 15: Improved Elemental Fury

Your chosen option improves:

- **Potent Spellcasting.** Druid cantrips with range 10+ feet gain **+300 feet** range.
- **Primal Strike.** Extra damage increases to **2d8**.

### Level 18: Beast Spells

While in Wild Shape, you can cast spells in Beast form — except spells with a Material component that has a specified cost or is consumed.

### Level 19: Epic Boon

Gain an Epic Boon feat or another qualifying feat. **Boon of Dimensional Travel** recommended.

### Level 20: Archdruid

- **Evergreen Wild Shape.** When rolling Initiative with no Wild Shape uses, regain one.
- **Nature Magician.** Convert unexpended Wild Shape uses into a spell slot (each use = 2 spell levels). E.g., 2 uses = level 4 slot. Once per Long Rest.
- **Longevity.** Age 1 year for every 10 that pass.

## Subclasses

Druid subclasses grant features at levels **3, 6, 10, and 14**.

### Circle of the Land

*Celebrate Connection to the Natural World*

#### Level 3: Circle of the Land Spells

On each Long Rest, choose a land type. Gain associated spells:

**Arid Land:**
| Druid Level | Circle Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | Blur, Burning Hands, Fire Bolt |
| 5 | Fireball |
| 7 | Blight |
| 9 | Wall of Stone |

**Polar Land:**
| Druid Level | Circle Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | Fog Cloud, Hold Person, Ray of Frost |
| 5 | Sleet Storm |
| 7 | Ice Storm |
| 9 | Cone of Cold |

**Temperate Land:**
| Druid Level | Circle Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | Misty Step, Shocking Grasp, Sleep |
| 5 | Lightning Bolt |
| 7 | Freedom of Movement |
| 9 | Tree Stride |

**Tropical Land:**
| Druid Level | Circle Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | Acid Splash, Ray of Sickness, Web |
| 5 | Stinking Cloud |
| 7 | Polymorph |
| 9 | Insect Plague |

#### Level 3: Land's Aid

Magic action + Wild Shape use: 10-foot-radius Sphere within 60 feet. Creatures of your choice make Constitution save: **2d6 Necrotic** on failure, half on success. One creature of your choice in the area regains **2d6 HP**. Scales to 3d6 (level 10) and 4d6 (level 14).

#### Level 6: Natural Recovery

Cast one level 1+ Circle Spell without a spell slot (once per Long Rest). Also, on Short Rest, recover spell slots with combined level ≤ half Druid level (round up), none level 6+. Once per Long Rest.

#### Level 10: Nature's Ward

Immune to Poisoned condition. Resistance based on current land:
- Arid → Fire
- Polar → Cold
- Temperate → Lightning
- Tropical → Poison

#### Level 14: Nature's Sanctuary

Magic action + Wild Shape use: create a 15-foot Cube of spectral trees/vines within 120 feet. Lasts 1 minute. You and allies have Half Cover in the area. Allies also gain your Nature's Ward resistance. Can move the Cube 60 feet as a Bonus Action.

### Circle of the Moon

*Adopt Animal Forms to Guard the Wilds*

#### Level 3: Circle Forms

Enhanced Wild Shape:
- **Challenge Rating.** Max CR = Druid level ÷ 3 (round down).
- **Armor Class.** AC = 13 + Wisdom modifier (if higher than Beast's AC).
- **Temporary HP.** Gain **3 × Druid level** Temp HP (instead of normal Druid level amount).

#### Level 3: Circle of the Moon Spells

| Druid Level | Always Prepared |
|---|---|
| 3 | Cure Wounds, Moonbeam, Starry Wisp |
| 5 | Conjure Animals |
| 7 | Fount of Moonlight |
| 9 | Mass Cure Wounds |

Can cast these spells while in Wild Shape form.

#### Level 6: Improved Circle Forms

While in Wild Shape:
- **Lunar Radiance.** Attacks can deal normal damage type or Radiant damage (choose per hit).
- **Increased Toughness.** Add Wisdom modifier to Constitution saving throws.

#### Level 10: Moonlight Step

Bonus Action: teleport up to 30 feet. Advantage on next attack roll before end of turn. Uses equal to Wisdom modifier (minimum 1). Regain all on Long Rest, or spend a level 2+ spell slot per use to restore.

#### Level 14: Lunar Form

- **Improved Lunar Radiance.** Once per turn, deal extra **2d10 Radiant** damage with a Wild Shape form attack.
- **Shared Moonlight.** When using Moonlight Step, teleport one willing creature within 10 feet to within 10 feet of your destination.

### Circle of the Sea

*Become One with Tides and Storms*

#### Level 3: Circle of the Sea Spells

| Druid Level | Always Prepared |
|---|---|
| 3 | Fog Cloud, Gust of Wind, Ray of Frost, Thunderwave |
| 5 | Lightning Bolt, Water Breathing |
| 7 | Control Water, Ice Storm |
| 9 | Conjure Elemental, Hold Monster |

#### Level 3: Wrath of the Sea

Bonus Action + Wild Shape use: manifest a 5-foot Emanation (ocean spray) for 10 minutes. On manifest and as a Bonus Action on subsequent turns, choose a creature in the Emanation: Constitution save or take Cold damage (**d6s equal to Wisdom modifier**, minimum 1d6) and, if Large or smaller, be pushed up to 15 feet away.

#### Level 6: Aquatic Affinity

Wrath of the Sea Emanation increases to **10 feet**. Gain Swim Speed equal to your Speed.

#### Level 10: Stormborn

While Wrath of the Sea is active:
- **Flight.** Fly Speed equal to your Speed.
- **Resistance.** Resistance to Cold, Lightning, and Thunder damage.

#### Level 14: Oceanic Gift

Can manifest Wrath of the Sea around a willing creature within 60 feet instead of yourself (they gain all benefits, using your spell DC and Wisdom modifier). Can manifest around both yourself and another creature by expending **2 Wild Shape uses**.

### Circle of the Stars

*Harness Secrets Hidden in Constellations*

#### Level 3: Star Map

Create a Tiny star chart (Spellcasting Focus). While holding it:
- Always have **Guidance** and **Guiding Bolt** prepared.
- Cast **Guiding Bolt** without a spell slot, uses equal to Wisdom modifier (minimum 1). Regain all on Long Rest.
- If lost, recreate in a 1-hour ceremony (Short or Long Rest).

#### Level 3: Starry Form

Bonus Action + Wild Shape use: take a luminous starry form (retain game statistics) for 10 minutes. Sheds Bright Light 10 feet, Dim Light 10 feet more. Choose a constellation:

- **Archer.** On activation and as Bonus Action each turn: ranged spell attack, 60 feet, dealing **1d8 + Wisdom modifier** Radiant damage.
- **Chalice.** When casting a spell with a slot that restores HP, you or a creature within 30 feet regains **1d8 + Wisdom modifier** HP.
- **Dragon.** On Intelligence/Wisdom checks or Constitution saves for Concentration, treat a d20 roll of 9 or lower as 10.

#### Level 6: Cosmic Omen

On each Long Rest, roll a die (even or odd determines effect until next Long Rest):

- **Weal (Even).** When a creature within 30 feet makes a D20 Test, use Reaction to add 1d6.
- **Woe (Odd).** When a creature within 30 feet makes a D20 Test, use Reaction to subtract 1d6.

Uses equal to Wisdom modifier (minimum 1). Regain all on Long Rest.

#### Level 10: Twinkling Constellations

Archer and Chalice improve to **2d8**. Dragon grants **Fly Speed 20 feet** (can hover). Can change constellation at the start of each turn while in Starry Form.

#### Level 14: Full of Stars

While in Starry Form, gain Resistance to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage.

## 2024 Changes from 2014

### Core Class Changes
- **Subclass choice** moved to **level 3** (was level 2 in 2014).
- **Primal Order** (level 1) is new — similar to Cleric's Divine Order, offering Magician (extra cantrip + skill bonus) or Warden (martial weapons + medium armor).
- **Metal armor restriction removed.** No longer prohibited from wearing metal armor or shields.
- **Wild Shape** completely overhauled:
  - Now grants **Temporary HP** equal to Druid level (instead of using the Beast's HP pool).
  - You keep your own HP, Hit Dice, and class features.
  - Can't cast spells in Wild Shape (unchanged), but doesn't break Concentration.
  - Known Forms system replaces the old CR table approach.
  - Moved to level 2 (was level 2 — unchanged).
- **Wild Companion** (level 2) is now a core feature (was optional from *Tasha's*).
- **Wild Resurgence** (level 5) is new — spell slot ↔ Wild Shape use conversion.
- **Elemental Fury** (level 7) is new — replaces no 2014 equivalent; offers Potent Spellcasting or Primal Strike.
- **Improved Elemental Fury** (level 15) is new.
- **Beast Spells** (level 18) — unchanged conceptually but now allows most spellcasting in Wild Shape.
- **Timeless Body** removed.
- **Archdruid** (level 20) reworked — Evergreen Wild Shape, Nature Magician (convert uses to spell slots), and Longevity (was unlimited Wild Shape uses).
- **Epic Boon** at level 19 (new).
- **ASI** at levels 4, 8, 12, 16 (was also at 19).
- **Druidic** now always prepares Speak with Animals.

### Subclass Changes
- All subclasses now grant features at levels **3, 6, 10, and 14** (was 2, 6, 10, 14).
- **Circle of the Land** significantly reworked:
  - Land types changed to Arid, Polar, Temperate, Tropical (was Arctic, Coast, Desert, Forest, Grassland, Mountain, Swamp, Underdark).
  - Land's Aid is new (an offensive + healing ability using Wild Shape).
  - Natural Recovery moved to level 6 and includes free Circle Spell casting.
  - Nature's Ward (level 10) is new (resistance + poison immunity).
  - Nature's Sanctuary (level 14) is new.
- **Circle of the Moon** significantly reworked:
  - Wild Shape CR cap simplified (Druid level ÷ 3).
  - AC uses 13 + Wisdom modifier (new).
  - Temp HP = 3 × Druid level (much more than standard Wild Shape).
  - Circle of the Moon Spells added (can be cast in Wild Shape).
  - Combat Wild Shape and Elemental Wild Shape removed.
  - Moonlight Step (level 10) and Lunar Form (level 14) are new.
- **Circle of the Sea** is entirely new (replaces no specific 2014 subclass).
- **Circle of the Stars** moved from *Tasha's Cauldron* to core PHB with updates:
  - Starry Form largely similar but moved to Wild Shape expenditure.
  - Cosmic Omen, Twinkling Constellations, Full of Stars retained with minor adjustments.
- **Circle of the Shepherd** and **Circle of Spores** removed from core PHB.
