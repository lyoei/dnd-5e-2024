# Paladin

> *Source: 2024 Player's Handbook*

Paladins are united by their oaths to stand against the forces of annihilation and corruption. Whether sworn before a god's altar, in a sacred glade before nature spirits, or in a moment of desperation and grief with the dead as the only witness, a Paladin's oath is a powerful bond — a source of power that turns a devout warrior into a blessed champion.

## Class Feature Descriptions

### Level 1: Lay On Hands
Pool of healing power = **5 × Paladin level** HP. Replenishes on Long Rest.
- **Bonus Action:** Touch a creature and restore HP from the pool.
- Spend 5 HP from pool to remove the **Poisoned** condition (doesn't restore HP).

### Level 1: Spellcasting
- **Half-caster** with Paladin spell list. Spellcasting ability: **Charisma**.
- **Prepared spells** system (choose from Paladin spell list). Can swap one spell per Long Rest.
- Spellcasting Focus: **Holy Symbol**.

### Level 1: Weapon Mastery
Use mastery properties of **2** kinds of weapons. Can change choices on Long Rest.

### Level 2: Fighting Style
Gain a Fighting Style feat. Alternatively, choose **Blessed Warrior**: learn 2 Cleric cantrips (count as Paladin spells, use CHA). Can replace one cantrip per Paladin level.

### Level 2: Paladin's Smite
Always have *Divine Smite* prepared. Can cast it **once without a spell slot** per Long Rest.

### Level 3: Channel Divinity
Channel divine energy for magical effects. Starts with **2 uses** (gain 3rd at level 11). Regain 1 on Short Rest, all on Long Rest. **Save DC = spell save DC.**

Built-in option:
- **Divine Sense (Bonus Action):** Detect Celestials, Fiends, and Undead within 60 ft for 10 minutes (or until Incapacitated). Also detects consecrated/desecrated areas.

### Level 3: Paladin Subclass
Choose a subclass (see below). Subclass features at levels 3, 7, 15, and 20.

### Level 4: Ability Score Improvement
Gain ASI feat or another feat. Also at levels 8, 12, and 16.

### Level 5: Extra Attack
Attack twice when you take the Attack action.

### Level 5: Faithful Steed
Always have *Find Steed* prepared. Can cast it **once without a spell slot** per Long Rest.

### Level 6: Aura of Protection
**10-ft Emanation** (inactive while Incapacitated). You and allies in the aura gain a **bonus to saving throws equal to your CHA modifier** (minimum +1). Only one Aura of Protection benefits a creature at a time.

### Level 9: Abjure Foes
**Magic Action:** Expend one Channel Divinity use. Target up to CHA modifier creatures you can see within 60 ft. Each must make a **WIS save** or be **Frightened** for 1 minute (or until taking damage). While Frightened this way, target can only do one thing on its turn: move, take an action, OR take a Bonus Action.

### Level 10: Aura of Courage
You and allies have **Immunity to the Frightened condition** while in your Aura of Protection. **If a Frightened ally enters the aura, that condition has no effect on that ally while there.**

### Level 11: Radiant Strikes
When you hit with a melee weapon or Unarmed Strike, deal an extra **1d8 Radiant damage**.

### Level 14: Restoring Touch
When using Lay On Hands, spend 5 HP per condition to remove: **Blinded, Charmed, Deafened, Frightened, Paralyzed, or Stunned** (doesn't restore HP for those points).

### Level 18: Aura Expansion
Aura of Protection becomes a **30-ft Emanation**.

### Level 19: Epic Boon
Gain an Epic Boon feat (Boon of Truesight recommended) or another feat.

## Subclasses

The 2024 PHB includes four Paladin subclasses: **Oath of Devotion**, **Oath of Glory**, **Oath of the Ancients**, and **Oath of Vengeance**.

### Oath of Devotion

*Uphold the Ideals of Justice and Order*

**Tenets:** Let your word be your promise. Protect the weak and never fear to act. Let your honorable deeds be an example.

#### Oath of Devotion Spells

| Paladin Level | Spells |
|:---:|---|
| 3 | Protection from Evil and Good, Shield of Faith |
| 5 | Aid, Zone of Truth |
| 9 | Beacon of Hope, Dispel Magic |
| 13 | Freedom of Movement, Guardian of Faith |
| 17 | Commune, Flame Strike |

#### Level 3: Sacred Weapon
When you take the Attack action, expend one Channel Divinity use to imbue a melee weapon you're holding for 10 minutes **or until you use this feature again**:
- Add **CHA modifier** to attack rolls (minimum +1).
- Choose to deal normal damage type or **Radiant damage** on each hit.
- Weapon emits **Bright Light 20 ft** / Dim Light 20 ft.

You can end this effect early (no action required). This effect also ends if you aren't carrying the weapon.

#### Level 7: Aura of Devotion
You and allies have **Immunity to the Charmed condition** while in your Aura of Protection. **If a Charmed ally enters the aura, that condition has no effect on that ally while there.**

#### Level 15: Smite of Protection
Whenever you cast *Divine Smite*, you and allies gain **Half Cover** while in your Aura of Protection until the start of your next turn.

#### Level 20: Holy Nimbus
**Bonus Action:** Imbue Aura of Protection with holy power for 10 minutes. Once per Long Rest (or expend a level 5 spell slot).
- **Holy Ward:** Advantage on saves forced by Fiends or Undead.
- **Radiant Damage:** Enemies starting their turn in aura take CHA modifier + Proficiency Bonus Radiant damage.
- **Sunlight:** Aura filled with Bright Light that counts as sunlight.

### Oath of Glory

*Strive for the Heights of Heroism*

**Tenets:** Endeavor to be known by your deeds. Face hardships with courage. Inspire others to strive for glory.

#### Oath of Glory Spells

| Paladin Level | Spells |
|:---:|---|
| 3 | Guiding Bolt, Heroism |
| 5 | Enhance Ability, Magic Weapon |
| 9 | Haste, Protection from Energy |
| 13 | Compulsion, Freedom of Movement |
| 17 | Legend Lore, Yolande's Regal Presence |

#### Level 3: Inspiring Smite
After casting *Divine Smite*, expend one Channel Divinity use: distribute **2d8 + Paladin level** Temporary HP among creatures of your choice within 30 ft.

#### Level 3: Peerless Athlete
**Bonus Action**, expend one Channel Divinity use: for 1 hour, Advantage on STR (Athletics) and DEX (Acrobatics) checks. Long and High Jump distances increase by 10 ft **(this extra distance costs movement as normal)**.

#### Level 7: Aura of Alacrity
Your Speed increases by **10 ft**. Allies entering your Aura of Protection **for the first time on a turn** or starting their turn there gain **+10 ft Speed** until end of their next turn.

#### Level 15: Glorious Defense
**Reaction** when you or a creature within 10 ft is hit by an attack: grant **+CHA modifier to AC** against that attack (minimum +1). If the attack misses, make one weapon attack against the attacker (if in range). Uses equal to CHA modifier per Long Rest.

#### Level 20: Living Legend
**Bonus Action:** Gain benefits for 10 minutes. Once per Long Rest (or expend a level 5 slot).
- **Charismatic:** Advantage on all Charisma checks.
- **Saving Throw Reroll:** Reaction to reroll a failed save (must use new roll).
- **Unerring Strike:** Once per turn, a missed weapon attack hits instead.

### Oath of the Ancients

*Preserve Life and Light in the World*

**Tenets:** Kindle the light of hope. Shelter life. Delight in art and laughter.

#### Oath of the Ancients Spells

| Paladin Level | Spells |
|:---:|---|
| 3 | Ensnaring Strike, Speak with Animals |
| 5 | Misty Step, Moonbeam |
| 9 | Plant Growth, Protection from Energy |
| 13 | Ice Storm, Stoneskin |
| 17 | Commune with Nature, Tree Stride |

#### Level 3: Nature's Wrath
**Magic Action**, expend one Channel Divinity use: each creature of your choice within **15 ft** must make a **STR save** or be **Restrained** for 1 minute (repeats save at end of each turn).

#### Level 7: Aura of Warding
You and allies have **Resistance to Necrotic, Psychic, and Radiant damage** while in your Aura of Protection.

#### Level 15: Undying Sentinel
When reduced to 0 HP (not killed outright), drop to **1 HP** instead and regain **3 × Paladin level** HP. Once per Long Rest. Also: can't be aged magically; cease visibly aging.

#### Level 20: Elder Champion
**Bonus Action:** Imbue Aura with primal power for 1 minute. Once per Long Rest (or expend a level 5 slot).
- **Diminish Defiance:** Enemies in aura have Disadvantage on saves vs your spells and Channel Divinity.
- **Regeneration:** Regain 10 HP at start of each of your turns.
- **Swift Spells:** Spells with an action casting time can be cast as a Bonus Action instead.

### Oath of Vengeance

*Punish Evildoers at Any Cost*

**Tenets:** Show the wicked no mercy. Fight injustice and its causes. Aid those harmed by injustice.

#### Oath of Vengeance Spells

| Paladin Level | Spells |
|:---:|---|
| 3 | Bane, Hunter's Mark |
| 5 | Hold Person, Misty Step |
| 9 | Haste, Protection from Energy |
| 13 | Banishment, Dimension Door |
| 17 | Hold Monster, Scrying |

#### Level 3: Vow of Enmity
When you take the Attack action, expend one Channel Divinity use: choose a creature within 30 ft. **Advantage on attack rolls** against it for 1 minute (or until you use this feature again). If the creature drops to 0 HP, transfer the vow to a different creature within 30 ft (no action).

#### Level 7: Relentless Avenger
When you hit a creature with an **Opportunity Attack**, reduce its Speed to **0** until end of current turn. You can then move up to half your Speed as part of the same Reaction (no OAs provoked).

#### Level 15: Soul of Vengeance
**Reaction** immediately after a creature under your Vow of Enmity hits or misses with an attack: make a melee attack against it (if in range).

#### Level 20: Avenging Angel
**Bonus Action:** Gain benefits for 10 minutes. Once per Long Rest (or expend a level 5 slot).
- **Flight:** Fly Speed 60 ft, can hover.
- **Frightful Aura:** Enemies starting their turn in your Aura of Protection must make WIS save or be Frightened for 1 minute (or until taking damage). Attack rolls against Frightened creatures have Advantage.

## 2024 Changes (vs. 2014 PHB)

- **Divine Smite is now a spell** (*Paladin's Smite* / *Divine Smite*), not a class feature. It is a Bonus Action spell, meaning it can no longer be used on critical hits retroactively (must declare before the attack). Can be used once per Long Rest without a spell slot.
- **Spellcasting at level 1** (was level 2 in 2014). Paladin is now a caster from the start.
- **Weapon Mastery** added (2 weapons).
- **Lay On Hands** changed to a **Bonus Action** (was an Action in 2014). Pool is still 5 × Paladin level.
- **Channel Divinity** is now a unified resource (2 uses, 3rd at level 11) with Divine Sense as a Channel Divinity option (was a separate resource in 2014).
- **Aura of Protection** moved to level 6 (unchanged) but now explicitly an "Emanation."
- **Abjure Foes (new, level 9):** Channel Divinity for mass Frighten — replaces the old Divine Sense as a combat tool.
- **Aura of Courage** moved to level 10 (was level 10 in 2014, unchanged).
- **Radiant Strikes (new, level 11):** Passive +1d8 Radiant damage on melee hits — replaces Improved Divine Smite.
- **Restoring Touch (level 14):** Expanded condition removal through Lay On Hands.
- **Aura Expansion** moved to level 18 (was level 18 in 2014, 30 ft).
- **Faithful Steed (new, level 5):** Always have *Find Steed* prepared with a free casting.
- **Fighting Style** moved to level 2 (was level 2 in 2014). Now includes full Fighting Style feat list plus Blessed Warrior.
- **Subclass at level 3** (was level 3 in 2014, unchanged).
- **Saving throw proficiencies** changed to **Wisdom and Charisma** (was Wisdom and Charisma in 2014 — unchanged).
- **Oath of Glory** is new to the PHB (was in Mythic Odysseys of Theros/Tasha's).
- **Level 20 capstone** features now have consistent formatting: Bonus Action for 10 minutes, 1/Long Rest or expend a level 5 slot to restore.
- **Oath of Devotion** reworked: Sacred Weapon changed; Smite of Protection is new; Holy Nimbus redesigned.
- **Oath of the Ancients** reworked: Undying Sentinel redesigned (heals 3× Paladin level); Elder Champion features changed.
- **Oath of Vengeance** refined: Vow of Enmity can transfer on kill; Relentless Avenger reduces Speed to 0; Avenging Angel updated.
