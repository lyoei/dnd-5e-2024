# Bard

> *Source: 2024 Player's Handbook*

Invoking magic through music, dance, and verse, Bards are expert at inspiring others, soothing hurts, disheartening foes, and creating illusions. Bards believe the multiverse was spoken into existence and that remnants of its Words of Creation still resound and glimmer on every plane of existence.

## Class Features

### Level 1: Bardic Inspiration

As a **Bonus Action**, inspire a creature within 60 feet who can see or hear you. They gain one Bardic Inspiration die (starts as d6).

- **Usage.** When the creature fails a D20 Test within the next **hour**, they can roll and add the Bardic Inspiration die to the d20, potentially turning failure into success. The die is expended when rolled.
- **Number of Uses.** Equal to Charisma modifier (minimum 1). All uses regain on Long Rest.
- **Die Progression.** d6 → d8 (level 5) → d10 (level 10) → d12 (level 15).
- Only one Bardic Inspiration die per creature at a time.

### Level 1: Spellcasting

Full caster using the Bard spell list. Charisma is the spellcasting ability. Musical Instrument as focus.

- **Cantrips.** Start with 2; gain extras at levels 4 and 10.
- **Prepared Spells.** Choose spells from the Bard spell list (replaces "spells known" from 2014). Can replace one spell when gaining a Bard level.

### Level 2: Expertise

Gain Expertise in 2 skill proficiencies. Gain 2 more at level 9.

### Level 2: Jack of All Trades

Add half your Proficiency Bonus (round down) to ability checks using skills you lack proficiency in (that don't already use your Proficiency Bonus).

### Level 3: Bard Subclass

Choose a subclass (see below).

### Level 4: Ability Score Improvement

Gain the ASI feat or another qualifying feat. Repeats at levels 8, 12, and 16.

### Level 5: Font of Inspiration

Bardic Inspiration uses now regain on **Short or Long Rest**. Additionally, you can expend a spell slot (no action required) to regain one use of Bardic Inspiration.

### Level 7: Countercharm

When you or a creature within 30 feet fails a saving throw against Charmed or Frightened, you can use a **Reaction** to cause the save to be rerolled with Advantage.

### Level 10: Magical Secrets

Whenever the Prepared Spells number increases (including at this level), you can choose new prepared spells from the **Bard, Cleric, Druid, and Wizard** spell lists. Replacement spells can also come from those lists.

### Level 18: Superior Inspiration

When you roll Initiative, regain expended Bardic Inspiration uses until you have 2 (if you had fewer).

### Level 19: Epic Boon

Gain an Epic Boon feat or another qualifying feat. **Boon of Spell Recall** recommended.

### Level 20: Words of Creation

You always have **Power Word: Heal** and **Power Word: Kill** prepared. When you cast either, you can target a second creature within 10 feet of the first.

## Subclasses

Bard subclasses grant features at levels **3, 6, and 14**.

### College of Dance

*Move in Harmony with the Cosmos*

#### Level 3: Dazzling Footwork

While not wearing armor or wielding a Shield:

- **Dance Virtuoso.** Advantage on Charisma (Performance) checks involving dancing.
- **Unarmored Defense.** AC = 10 + Dexterity modifier + Charisma modifier.
- **Agile Strikes.** When you expend Bardic Inspiration as part of an action/Bonus Action/Reaction, make one Unarmed Strike as part of it.
- **Bardic Damage.** Use Dexterity for Unarmed Strike attack rolls. Damage = Bardic Inspiration die + Dexterity modifier (Bludgeoning). This roll doesn't expend the die.

#### Level 6: Inspiring Movement

When an enemy ends its turn within 5 feet of you, use **Reaction** + expend Bardic Inspiration to move up to half Speed. One ally within 30 feet can also move up to half their Speed as a Reaction. No Opportunity Attacks from this movement.

#### Level 6: Tandem Footwork

When rolling Initiative (not Incapacitated), expend Bardic Inspiration: roll the die and add the result as a bonus to Initiative for you and all allies within 30 feet who can see/hear you.

#### Level 14: Leading Evasion

Evasion for Dexterity saves (no damage on success, half on failure). Creatures within 5 feet making the same save share this benefit. Can't use while Incapacitated.

### College of Glamour

*Weave Beguiling Fey Magic*

#### Level 3: Beguiling Magic

Always have **Charm Person** and **Mirror Image** prepared.

After casting an Enchantment or Illusion spell with a spell slot, you can force a creature within 60 feet to make a Wisdom save vs. your spell DC. Failed = Charmed or Frightened (your choice) for 1 minute (save at end of each turn). Once per Long Rest, or restore by expending one Bardic Inspiration use.

#### Level 3: Mantle of Inspiration

As a Bonus Action, expend Bardic Inspiration. Choose up to Charisma modifier creatures within 60 feet. Each gains **2 × Bardic Inspiration die roll** Temporary HP and can use Reaction to move up to their Speed without provoking Opportunity Attacks.

#### Level 6: Mantle of Majesty

Always have **Command** prepared.

As a Bonus Action, cast Command without a spell slot and take on unearthly appearance for 1 minute (Concentration). During this time, cast Command as Bonus Action without spell slots. Charmed creatures auto-fail the save. Once per Long Rest, or restore with a level 3+ spell slot.

#### Level 14: Unbreakable Majesty

As a Bonus Action, assume majestic presence for 1 minute (or until Incapacitated). When any creature hits you with an attack for the first time on a turn, attacker must make a Charisma save vs. your spell DC or the attack misses. Once per Short or Long Rest.

### College of Lore

*Plumb the Depths of Magical Knowledge*

#### Level 3: Bonus Proficiencies

Gain proficiency with 3 skills of your choice.

#### Level 3: Cutting Words

When a creature you can see within 60 feet makes a damage roll or succeeds on an ability check or attack roll, use **Reaction** + expend Bardic Inspiration: roll the die and subtract it from the creature's roll.

#### Level 6: Magical Discoveries

Learn 2 spells from Cleric, Druid, or Wizard spell lists (cantrips or spells you have slots for). Always prepared. Can replace one on each Bard level-up.

#### Level 14: Peerless Skill

When you fail an ability check or attack roll, expend Bardic Inspiration: add the die roll to the d20. On failure, the Bardic Inspiration is **not** expended.

### College of Valor

*Sing the Deeds of Ancient Heroes*

#### Level 3: Combat Inspiration

Creatures with your Bardic Inspiration die gain options:

- **Defense.** When hit by an attack, use Reaction to roll the die and add to AC against that attack.
- **Offense.** After hitting with an attack, roll the die and add to damage.

#### Level 3: Martial Training

Proficiency with Martial weapons, training with Medium armor and Shields. Can use a Simple or Martial weapon as a Spellcasting Focus for Bard spells.

#### Level 6: Extra Attack

Attack twice when taking the Attack action. Can replace one attack with a cantrip (action casting time).

#### Level 14: Battle Magic

After casting a spell with an action casting time, make one weapon attack as a Bonus Action.

### College of the Moon

*Inspire Allies with Primal Tales*

> *Source: Forgotten Realms — Heroes of Faerûn*

#### Level 3: Moon's Inspiration

- **Inspired Eclipse.** When giving Bardic Inspiration, you can become Invisible and teleport up to 30 feet (as part of Bonus Action). Invisibility lasts until start of your next turn; ends early if you attack, deal damage, or cast a spell.
- **Lunar Vitality.** Once per turn when restoring HP with a spell, expend Bardic Inspiration die and add the roll to HP restored. Target's Speed also increases by 10 feet until end of their next turn.

#### Level 3: Primal Lore

Learn Druidic and one Druid cantrip (counts as Bard spell, doesn't count against cantrips known). Can swap on level-up. Gain proficiency in one skill: Animal Handling, Insight, Medicine, Nature, Perception, or Survival.

#### Level 6: Blessing of Moonlight

Always have **Moonbeam** prepared. When casting Moonbeam, can modify it: you glow (Dim Light 5 feet), and when a creature fails its Moonbeam save, another creature of your choice within 60 feet regains **2d4 HP**. Once per Long Rest.

#### Level 14: Eventide's Splendor

- **Shadow of the New Moon.** When using Inspired Eclipse, the Bardic Inspiration recipient also becomes Invisible and can immediately use Reaction to teleport up to 30 feet. Their invisibility lasts until start of their next turn.
- **Vibrance of the Full Moon.** Lunar Vitality can roll 1d6 instead of expending a Bardic Inspiration die.

## Spell List

The Bard uses the **Bard spell list** from the 2024 PHB. Key spells include:

## 2024 Changes from 2014

### Core Class Changes
- **Bardic Inspiration** now lasts **1 hour** (was 10 minutes). Uses regain on Long Rest at level 1 (was Short Rest via Font of Inspiration at level 5).
- **Spells Known → Prepared Spells.** Bards now "prepare" spells like other casters, though they can only replace one spell per level-up (unlike Clerics/Druids who swap on Long Rest).
- **Expertise** moved to level 2 (was level 3). Second set at level 9 (was level 10).
- **Font of Inspiration** (level 5) now also lets you expend a spell slot to regain a Bardic Inspiration use.
- **Jack of All Trades** moved to level 2 (was level 2, unchanged).
- **Countercharm** (level 7) completely reworked — now a Reaction to force a reroll with Advantage (was an action to grant advantage on saves for a round).
- **Magical Secrets** moved to level 10 (was level 10 for Lore, level 14/18 for others). Now integrated into spell preparation — can choose from Bard/Cleric/Druid/Wizard lists.
- **Song of Rest** removed entirely.
- **Superior Inspiration** (level 18) reworked — now restores up to 2 uses on Initiative (was restore 1 use if you had 0).
- **Words of Creation** at level 20 is new (was no capstone in 2014 beyond ASI).
- **Epic Boon** at level 19 is new.
- **ASI** at levels 4, 8, 12, 16 (was also at 19).

### Subclass Changes
- **Subclass choice** at level 3 (unchanged). Features at 3, 6, 14 (was 3, 6, 14).
- **College of Dance** is new (replaces College of Swords in PHB).
- **College of Glamour** moved to core PHB from *Xanathar's Guide* with updates.
- **College of Lore** retained with adjustments (Magical Discoveries replaces Additional Magical Secrets at level 6).
- **College of Valor** retained with minor updates (Extra Attack now lets you swap one attack for a cantrip).
- **College of the Moon** is new (from *Forgotten Realms — Heroes of Faerûn* supplement).
- **College of Whispers** and **College of Swords** removed from core PHB.
