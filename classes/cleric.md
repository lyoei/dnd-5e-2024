# Cleric

> *Source: 2024 Player's Handbook*

Clerics draw power from the realms of the gods and harness it to work miracles. Blessed by a deity, a pantheon, or another immortal entity, a Cleric can reach out to the divine magic of the Outer Planes—where gods dwell—and channel it to bolster people and battle foes.

## Class Overview

| Trait | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Wisdom |
| **Hit Point Die** | d8 per Cleric level |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Wisdom and Charisma |
| **Skill Proficiencies** | Choose 2: History, Insight, Medicine, Persuasion, or Religion |
| **Weapon Proficiencies** | Simple weapons |
| **Armor Training** | Light and Medium armor and Shields |
| **Starting Equipment** | (A) Chain Shirt, Shield, Mace, Holy Symbol, Priest's Pack, and 7 GP; or (B) 110 GP |

## Class Features

### Level 1: Spellcasting

Full caster using the Cleric spell list. Wisdom is the spellcasting ability. Holy Symbol as focus.

- **Cantrips.** Start with 3. Gain extras at levels 4 and 10.
- **Prepared Spells.** Choose from full Cleric spell list. Can change entire prepared list on Long Rest.

### Level 1: Divine Order

Choose one sacred role:

- **Protector.** Proficiency with Martial weapons and training with Heavy armor.
- **Thaumaturge.** One extra Cleric cantrip. Bonus to Intelligence (Arcana or Religion) checks equal to Wisdom modifier (minimum +1).

### Level 2: Channel Divinity

Channel divine energy. Start with 2 uses; regain one on Short Rest, all on Long Rest. Uses increase per the table.

Two base options:

- **Divine Spark.** Magic action. Target a creature within 30 feet. Roll 1d8 + Wisdom modifier. Either restore that many HP **or** force a Constitution save — on failure, deal Necrotic or Radiant damage (your choice) equal to the total (half on success). Additional d8s at levels 7 (2d8), 13 (3d8), and 18 (4d8).
- **Turn Undead.** Magic action. Each Undead of your choice within 30 feet makes a Wisdom save or is Frightened and Incapacitated for 1 minute (tries to flee). Ends on damage, your Incapacitation, or your death.

### Level 3: Cleric Subclass

Choose a subclass (see below).

### Level 4: Ability Score Improvement

ASI feat or another qualifying feat. Repeats at levels 8, 12, and 16.

### Level 5: Sear Undead

When using Turn Undead, roll d8s equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum 1d8). Each Undead that fails its save takes Radiant damage equal to the total. Doesn't end the Turn effect.

### Level 7: Blessed Strikes

Choose one:

- **Divine Strike.** Once per turn, when hitting with a weapon attack, deal extra 1d8 Necrotic or Radiant damage (your choice).
- **Potent Spellcasting.** Add Wisdom modifier to Cleric cantrip damage.

### Level 10: Divine Intervention

As a Magic action, cast any Cleric spell of level 5 or lower without a spell slot or Material components. Once per Long Rest.

### Level 14: Improved Blessed Strikes

Your chosen option improves:

- **Divine Strike.** Extra damage increases to 2d8.
- **Potent Spellcasting.** When dealing cantrip damage, grant a creature within 60 feet Temporary HP equal to 2 × Wisdom modifier.

### Level 19: Epic Boon

Gain an Epic Boon feat or another qualifying feat. **Boon of Fate** recommended.

### Level 20: Greater Divine Intervention

When using Divine Intervention, you can choose **Wish**. If you do, you can't use Divine Intervention again until you finish **2d4 Long Rests**.

## Subclasses

Cleric subclasses grant features at levels **3, 6, and 17**.

### Life Domain

*Soothe the Hurts of the World*

#### Level 3: Disciple of Life

When a spell you cast with a spell slot restores HP, the creature regains additional HP equal to **2 + spell slot level** (on the turn you cast it).

#### Level 3: Life Domain Spells

| Cleric Level | Always Prepared |
|---|---|
| 3 | Aid, Bless, Cure Wounds, Lesser Restoration |
| 5 | Mass Healing Word, Revivify |
| 7 | Aura of Life, Death Ward |
| 9 | Greater Restoration, Mass Cure Wounds |

#### Level 3: Preserve Life

Magic action + Channel Divinity use: restore HP equal to **5 × Cleric level**, divided among Bloodied creatures within 30 feet (including yourself). Can't restore a creature above half its HP max.

#### Level 6: Blessed Healer

After casting a spell with a spell slot that restores HP to others (not just yourself), you regain HP equal to **2 + spell slot level**.

#### Level 17: Supreme Healing

When rolling dice to restore HP with a spell or Channel Divinity, use the maximum value for each die instead of rolling.

### Light Domain

*Bring Light to Banish Darkness*

#### Level 3: Light Domain Spells

| Cleric Level | Always Prepared |
|---|---|
| 3 | Burning Hands, Faerie Fire, Scorching Ray, See Invisibility |
| 5 | Daylight, Fireball |
| 7 | Arcane Eye, Wall of Fire |
| 9 | Flame Strike, Scrying |

#### Level 3: Radiance of the Dawn

Magic action + Channel Divinity: 30-foot Emanation. Dispels magical Darkness. Each creature of your choice makes a Constitution save — **2d10 + Cleric level** Radiant damage on failure, half on success.

#### Level 3: Warding Flare

When a creature within 30 feet makes an attack roll, use **Reaction** to impose Disadvantage. Uses equal to Wisdom modifier (minimum 1). Regain all on Long Rest.

#### Level 6: Improved Warding Flare

Warding Flare uses regain on **Short or Long Rest**. When used, grant the attack target **2d6 + Wisdom modifier** Temporary HP.

#### Level 17: Corona of Light

Magic action: emit sunlight aura for 1 minute (or dismiss). Bright Light 60 feet, Dim Light 30 feet more. Enemies in Bright Light have Disadvantage on saves against Radiance of the Dawn and Fire/Radiant damage spells. Uses equal to Wisdom modifier; regain all on Long Rest.

---

### Trickery Domain

*Make Mischief and Challenge Authority*

#### Level 3: Blessing of the Trickster

Magic action: give yourself or a willing creature within 30 feet Advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks until your next Long Rest or you use this again.

#### Level 3: Trickery Domain Spells

| Cleric Level | Always Prepared |
|---|---|
| 3 | Charm Person, Disguise Self, Invisibility, Pass without Trace |
| 5 | Hypnotic Pattern, Nondetection |
| 7 | Confusion, Dimension Door |
| 9 | Dominate Person, Modify Memory |

#### Level 3: Invoke Duplicity

Bonus Action + Channel Divinity: create a visual illusion of yourself within 30 feet. Lasts 1 minute. Benefits:

- **Cast Spells.** Cast spells as if in the illusion's space (use your own senses).
- **Distract.** Advantage on attacks when you and illusion are both within 5 feet of a target.
- **Move.** Bonus Action to move illusion up to 30 feet (within 120 feet of you).

#### Level 6: Trickster's Transposition

When creating or moving the illusion, you can teleport and swap places with it.

#### Level 17: Improved Duplicity

- **Shared Distraction.** Your allies also gain Advantage on attacks against creatures within 5 feet of the illusion.
- **Healing Illusion.** When the illusion ends, you or a creature within 5 feet regains HP equal to your Cleric level.

### War Domain

*Inspire Valor and Smite Foes*

#### Level 3: Guided Strike

When you or a creature within 30 feet misses with an attack roll, expend Channel Divinity to give **+10 bonus** to the roll. If used for another creature, requires your Reaction.

#### Level 3: War Domain Spells

| Cleric Level | Always Prepared |
|---|---|
| 3 | Guiding Bolt, Magic Weapon, Shield of Faith, Spiritual Weapon |
| 5 | Crusader's Mantle, Spirit Guardians |
| 7 | Fire Shield, Freedom of Movement |
| 9 | Hold Monster, Steel Wind Strike |

#### Level 3: War Priest

As a **Bonus Action**, make one weapon attack or Unarmed Strike. Uses equal to Wisdom modifier (minimum 1). Regain all on Short or Long Rest.

#### Level 6: War God's Blessing

Expend Channel Divinity to cast **Shield of Faith** or **Spiritual Weapon** without a spell slot. The spell doesn't require Concentration; lasts 1 minute (ends early if you recast, become Incapacitated, or die).

#### Level 17: Avatar of Battle

Resistance to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage.

*Unearth Secrets and Master the Mind*

> *Source: Forgotten Realms — Heroes of Faerûn*

#### Level 3: Blessings of Knowledge

Proficiency with one Artisan's Tools. Proficiency + Expertise in 2 skills from: Arcana, History, Nature, or Religion.

#### Level 3: Knowledge Domain Spells

| Cleric Level | Always Prepared |
|---|---|
| 3 | Command, Comprehend Languages*, Detect Magic*, Detect Thoughts*, Identify*, Mind Spike* |
| 5 | Dispel Magic, Nondetection, Tongues* |
| 7 | Arcane Eye*, Banishment, Confusion |
| 9 | Legend Lore*, Scrying*, Synaptic Static |

*\* Divination school spell*

#### Level 3: Mind Magic

Magic action + Channel Divinity: cast one prepared Divination spell from the Knowledge Domain Spells table without a spell slot or Material components.

#### Level 6: Unfettered Mind

Gain telepathy 60 feet (simultaneous contacts = Wisdom modifier, minimum 1). Gain proficiency in Intelligence saving throws (or another save if you already have Int saves).

#### Level 17: Divine Foreknowledge

Bonus Action: for 1 hour, Advantage on all D20 Tests. Once per Long Rest, or restore with a level 6+ spell slot.

## 2024 Changes from 2014

### Core Class Changes
- **Subclass choice** moved to **level 3** (was level 1 in 2014). This is one of the biggest structural changes.
- **Divine Order** (level 1) is new — replaces the immediate subclass choice, offering Protector (heavy armor + martial weapons) or Thaumaturge (extra cantrip + skill bonus).
- **Channel Divinity** reworked:
  - **Divine Spark** is new (combines healing and damage in one feature, replaces some subclass Channel Divinity options).
  - **Turn Undead** now applies Frightened + Incapacitated (was just Frightened; Destroy Undead removed as a separate feature).
  - Uses: regain 1 on Short Rest (was all on Short Rest in 2014). Total uses scale per table.
- **Sear Undead** (level 5) is new — adds Radiant damage to Turn Undead.
- **Blessed Strikes** (level 7) is now a core class feature offering Divine Strike OR Potent Spellcasting (previously these were scattered across subclasses at level 8).
- **Improved Blessed Strikes** (level 14) is new, upgrading the chosen option.
- **Divine Intervention** (level 10) completely reworked — now lets you cast a level 5 or lower Cleric spell for free (was a percentile-based prayer with DM discretion).
- **Greater Divine Intervention** (level 20) is new — can use Wish.
- **Destroy Undead** removed (partially replaced by Sear Undead).
- **Epic Boon** at level 19 (new).
- **ASI** at levels 4, 8, 12, 16 (was also at 19).
- **Saving throws** changed to Wisdom and Charisma (was Wisdom and Charisma — unchanged).

### Subclass Changes
- All subclasses now grant features at levels **3, 6, and 17** (was 1, 2, 6, 8, 17 in 2014).
- Subclass domain spells start at level 3 (was level 1).
- **Life Domain** retained with updates (Preserve Life uses "Bloodied" condition terminology; scaling unchanged).
- **Light Domain** retained with significant updates (Warding Flare improved, Corona of Light reworked).
- **Trickery Domain** retained with major rework (Invoke Duplicity greatly improved, Trickster's Transposition is new).
- **War Domain** retained with updates (Guided Strike can now help allies, War God's Blessing reworked).
- **Knowledge Domain** is new to 2024 PHB supplements (from *Forgotten Realms — Heroes of Faerûn*).
- **Nature Domain** and **Tempest Domain** removed from core PHB.
