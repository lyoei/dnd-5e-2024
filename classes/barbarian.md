# Barbarian

> *Source: 2024 Player's Handbook*

Barbarians are mighty warriors powered by primal forces of the multiverse that manifest as a Rage. More than a mere emotion—and not limited to anger—this Rage is an incarnation of a predator's ferocity, a storm's fury, and a sea's turmoil.

## Class Overview

| Trait | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Strength |
| **Hit Point Die** | d12 per Barbarian level |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Strength and Constitution |
| **Skill Proficiencies** | Choose 2: Animal Handling, Athletics, Intimidation, Nature, Perception, or Survival |
| **Weapon Proficiencies** | Simple and Martial weapons |
| **Armor Training** | Light and Medium armor and Shields |
| **Starting Equipment** | (A) Greataxe, 4 Handaxes, Explorer's Pack, and 15 GP; or (B) 75 GP |

## Class Features

### Level 1: Rage

You can imbue yourself with a primal power called Rage as a **Bonus Action** (can't wear Heavy armor).

- **Damage Resistance.** Resistance to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage.
- **Rage Damage.** Bonus damage on Strength-based attacks (weapon or Unarmed Strike), as shown in the table.
- **Strength Advantage.** Advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws.
- **No Concentration or Spells.** Can't maintain Concentration or cast spells.
- **Duration.** Lasts until end of your next turn; extend each round by: making an attack roll against an enemy, forcing an enemy to make a saving throw, or taking a Bonus Action to extend. Maximum 10 minutes.
- **Uses.** Regain one use on Short Rest, all uses on Long Rest.

### Level 1: Unarmored Defense

While not wearing armor: AC = 10 + Dexterity modifier + Constitution modifier. Can use a Shield.

### Level 1: Weapon Mastery

Use mastery properties of 2 kinds of Simple or Martial Melee weapons. Can swap one choice after each Long Rest. Number increases at higher levels (see table).

### Level 2: Danger Sense

Advantage on Dexterity saving throws unless Incapacitated.

### Level 2: Reckless Attack

On your first attack roll of your turn, you can attack recklessly: Advantage on Strength-based attack rolls until start of your next turn, but attack rolls against you also have Advantage.

### Level 3: Barbarian Subclass

Choose a subclass (see below).

### Level 3: Primal Knowledge

Gain proficiency in one additional Barbarian skill. While Raging, you can make Acrobatics, Intimidation, Perception, Stealth, or Survival checks as Strength checks.

### Level 4: Ability Score Improvement

Gain the Ability Score Improvement feat or another feat. Repeats at levels 8, 12, and 16.

### Level 5: Extra Attack

Attack twice when you take the Attack action.

### Level 5: Fast Movement

Speed increases by 10 feet while not wearing Heavy armor.

### Level 7: Feral Instinct

Advantage on Initiative rolls.

### Level 7: Instinctive Pounce

When you enter Rage (as part of the Bonus Action), you can move up to half your Speed.

### Level 9: Brutal Strike

When using Reckless Attack, you can forgo Advantage on one Strength-based attack. If it hits, deal an extra **1d10** damage and choose one effect:

- **Forceful Blow.** Push target 15 feet away; you can then move up to half your Speed toward the target without provoking Opportunity Attacks.
- **Hamstring Blow.** Target's Speed reduced by 15 feet until start of your next turn.

### Level 11: Relentless Rage

If you drop to 0 HP while Raging (and don't die outright), make a DC 10 Constitution save. On success, HP changes to 2 × your Barbarian level. DC increases by 5 each subsequent use; resets on Short or Long Rest.

### Level 13: Improved Brutal Strike

New Brutal Strike options:

- **Staggering Blow.** Target has Disadvantage on next saving throw; can't make Opportunity Attacks until start of your next turn.
- **Sundering Blow.** Next attack roll by another creature against the target gains +5 bonus before start of your next turn.

### Level 15: Persistent Rage

When you roll Initiative, you can regain all expended Rage uses (once per Long Rest). Rage now lasts 10 minutes without needing to extend. Ends early only on Unconscious condition (not just Incapacitated) or donning Heavy armor.

### Level 17: Improved Brutal Strike (2d10)

Brutal Strike extra damage increases to **2d10**. You can use **two** different Brutal Strike effects per use.

### Level 18: Indomitable Might

If your total for a Strength check or Strength save is less than your Strength score, use the score instead.

### Level 19: Epic Boon

Gain an Epic Boon feat or another qualifying feat. **Boon of Irresistible Offense** recommended.

### Level 20: Primal Champion

Strength and Constitution scores increase by 4, to a maximum of 25.

## Subclasses

All Barbarian subclasses grant features at levels **3, 6, 10, and 14**.

### Path of the Berserker

*Channel Rage into Violent Fury*

#### Level 3: Frenzy

When using Reckless Attack while Raging, deal extra damage to the first target hit on your turn with a Strength-based attack. Roll a number of **d6s equal to your Rage Damage bonus** and add them together. Same damage type as the weapon/Unarmed Strike.

#### Level 6: Mindless Rage

Immunity to Charmed and Frightened conditions while Raging. If already affected when entering Rage, the condition ends.

#### Level 10: Retaliation

When you take damage from a creature within 5 feet, you can use a **Reaction** to make one melee attack against it.

#### Level 14: Intimidating Presence

As a Bonus Action, each creature of your choice in a **30-foot Emanation** must make a Wisdom save (DC 8 + Strength modifier + Proficiency Bonus). Failed save = Frightened for 1 minute (repeats save at end of each turn). Once used, requires Long Rest or expending a Rage use to restore.

---

### Path of the Wild Heart

*Walk in Community with the Animal World*

#### Level 3: Animal Speaker

Can cast **Beast Sense** and **Speak with Animals** as Rituals only. Wisdom is the spellcasting ability.

#### Level 3: Rage of the Wilds

When activating Rage, choose one:

- **Bear.** Resistance to every damage type except Force, Necrotic, Psychic, and Radiant.
- **Eagle.** Disengage and Dash as part of Rage Bonus Action; while Raging, can take Bonus Action for both.
- **Wolf.** Allies have Advantage on attacks against enemies within 5 feet of you.

#### Level 6: Aspect of the Wilds

Choose one (can change on Long Rest):

- **Owl.** Darkvision 60 feet (or extend existing by 60 feet).
- **Panther.** Climb Speed equal to your Speed.
- **Salmon.** Swim Speed equal to your Speed.

#### Level 10: Nature Speaker

Can cast **Commune with Nature** as a Ritual only. Wisdom is the spellcasting ability.

#### Level 14: Power of the Wilds

When activating Rage, choose one:

- **Falcon.** Fly Speed equal to Speed (no armor).
- **Lion.** Enemies within 5 feet have Disadvantage on attacks against targets other than you (or another Barbarian with this feature).
- **Ram.** Can knock Large or smaller creatures Prone on melee hit.

---

### Path of the World Tree

*Trace the Roots and Branches of the Multiverse*

#### Level 3: Vitality of the Tree

- **Vitality Surge.** When activating Rage, gain Temporary HP equal to your Barbarian level.
- **Life-Giving Force.** Start of each turn while Raging, choose a creature within 10 feet to gain Temporary HP (roll d6s equal to Rage Damage bonus). Temp HP vanish when Rage ends.

#### Level 6: Branches of the Tree

When a creature starts its turn within 30 feet while you're Raging, use a **Reaction**: target makes Strength save (DC 8 + Strength mod + Prof Bonus) or is teleported to within 5 feet of you. Can reduce its Speed to 0 until end of current turn.

#### Level 10: Battering Roots

During your turn, reach is 10 feet greater with Heavy or Versatile melee weapons. On a hit, can activate Push or Topple mastery property in addition to another mastery property.

#### Level 14: Travel Along the Tree

When activating Rage and as a Bonus Action while Raging: teleport up to 60 feet. Once per Rage, can increase to 150 feet and bring up to 6 willing creatures within 10 feet (they appear within 10 feet of your destination).

---

### Path of the Zealot

*Rage in Ecstatic Union with a God*

#### Level 3: Divine Fury

On each turn while Raging, first creature hit with a weapon/Unarmed Strike takes extra damage equal to **1d6 + half your Barbarian level** (round down). Damage is Necrotic or Radiant (your choice each time).

#### Level 3: Warrior of the Gods

Pool of **4 d12s** for self-healing. As a Bonus Action, expend dice from pool, roll, and regain HP equal to total. All dice regain on Long Rest. Pool maximum increases: 5 dice at level 6, 6 at level 12, 7 at level 17.

#### Level 6: Fanatical Focus

Once per active Rage, if you fail a saving throw, reroll with a bonus equal to your Rage Damage bonus. Must use new roll.

#### Level 10: Zealous Presence

As a Bonus Action, up to 10 creatures of your choice within 60 feet gain Advantage on attack rolls and saving throws until start of your next turn. Once per Long Rest, or expend a Rage use to restore.

#### Level 14: Rage of the Gods

When activating Rage, can assume divine warrior form (1 minute or until 0 HP). Once per Long Rest.

- **Flight.** Fly Speed equal to Speed; can hover.
- **Resistance.** Resistance to Necrotic, Psychic, and Radiant damage.
- **Revivification.** When a creature within 30 feet would drop to 0 HP, use Reaction + expend a Rage use to set their HP to your Barbarian level instead.

## 2024 Changes from 2014

### Core Class Changes
- **Weapon Mastery** added at level 1 (new system for weapon properties).
- **Rage** now extends by making an attack, forcing a save, or using a Bonus Action (previously required attacking or taking damage). Duration clarified as extending round-by-round, up to 10 minutes.
- **Rage uses** regain one on Short Rest (previously all on Long Rest only).
- **Primal Knowledge** (level 3) is new — allows using Strength for certain skill checks while Raging.
- **Brutal Strike** (level 9) is new — replaces Brutal Critical. Provides tactical effects (push, slow) instead of extra critical damage dice.
- **Improved Brutal Strike** (levels 13 and 17) adds new options and increases damage.
- **Instinctive Pounce** moved from optional feature to core (level 7).
- **Persistent Rage** (level 15) reworked — now regains all Rage uses on Initiative and Rage doesn't need extending.
- **Indomitable Might** moved from level 18 (unchanged).
- **Feral Instinct** now grants Advantage on Initiative (previously couldn't be surprised + Initiative bonus).
- **Brutal Critical** removed entirely (replaced by Brutal Strike).
- **Epic Boon** at level 19 (new).
- **Primal Champion** unchanged at level 20.
- **ASI** at levels 4, 8, 12, 16 (was also 19; now 19 is Epic Boon).

### Subclass Changes
- **Subclass choice** moved to level 3 (was level 3 already, unchanged).
- **Path of the Totem Warrior** replaced by **Path of the Wild Heart** (similar concept, updated mechanics).
- **Path of the World Tree** is entirely new (replaces no specific 2014 subclass in PHB).
- **Path of the Zealot** moved from *Xanathar's Guide* to core PHB with significant rework:
  - Divine Fury is similar but damage formula changed.
  - Warrior of the Gods completely reworked (now a self-healing pool; was free resurrection).
  - Zealous Presence reworked.
  - Rage Beyond Death (level 14) replaced by Rage of the Gods (divine form with flight).
- **Path of the Berserker** significantly improved:
  - Frenzy no longer causes Exhaustion; now deals extra d6s based on Rage Damage bonus.
  - Intimidating Presence moved to level 14, now an Emanation AoE.
  - Retaliation moved to level 10.
  - Mindless Rage unchanged.
