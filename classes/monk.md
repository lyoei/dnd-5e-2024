# Monk

> *Source: 2024 Player's Handbook*

Monks use rigorous combat training and mental discipline to align themselves with the multiverse and focus their internal reservoirs of power. Whether channeled as a striking display of martial prowess or as a subtler manifestation of defense and speed, this power infuses all that a Monk does.

## Class Overview

| Trait | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Dexterity and Wisdom |
| **Hit Point Die** | d8 per Monk level |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Strength and Dexterity |
| **Skill Proficiencies** | Choose 2: Acrobatics, Athletics, History, Insight, Religion, or Stealth |
| **Tool Proficiencies** | Choose one type of Artisan's Tools or Musical Instrument |
| **Weapon Proficiencies** | Simple weapons and Martial weapons that have the Light property |
| **Armor Training** | None |
| **Starting Equipment** | (A) Spear, 5 Daggers, Artisan's Tools or Musical Instrument, Explorer's Pack, 11 GP; or (B) 50 GP |

## Class Feature Descriptions

### Level 1: Martial Arts
While unarmed or wielding only Monk weapons (Simple Melee + Light Martial Melee) and not wearing armor or wielding a Shield:
- **Bonus Unarmed Strike:** Make an Unarmed Strike as a Bonus Action.
- **Martial Arts Die:** Roll your Martial Arts die (starts at 1d6, scales per table) in place of normal Unarmed Strike or Monk weapon damage.
- **Dexterous Attacks:** Use DEX instead of STR for attack and damage rolls of Unarmed Strikes and Monk weapons. Also use DEX for Grapple/Shove save DCs.

### Level 1: Unarmored Defense
While not wearing armor or wielding a Shield: **AC = 10 + DEX modifier + WIS modifier**.

### Level 2: Monk's Focus
Gain **Focus Points** (equal to Monk level, starting at 2). Regain all on Short or Long Rest. **Focus save DC = 8 + WIS modifier + Proficiency Bonus.**

Three options fueled by Focus Points:
- **Flurry of Blows (1 FP):** Make two Unarmed Strikes as a Bonus Action.
- **Patient Defense:** Disengage as Bonus Action for free. Spend 1 FP to also Dodge as part of the same Bonus Action.
- **Step of the Wind:** Dash as Bonus Action for free. Spend 1 FP to also Disengage + double jump distance for the turn.

### Level 2: Unarmored Movement
Speed increases by +10 ft (scaling to +30 ft at level 18) while not wearing armor or wielding a Shield.

### Level 2: Uncanny Metabolism
When you roll Initiative, regain all expended Focus Points. Also roll your Martial Arts die and regain HP equal to Monk level + the number rolled. Once per Long Rest.

### Level 3: Deflect Attacks
**Reaction** when hit by an attack that deals Bludgeoning, Piercing, or Slashing damage: reduce total damage by **1d10 + DEX modifier + Monk level**. If reduced to 0, you can expend 1 FP to redirect: choose a creature within 5 ft (melee) or 60 ft (ranged, no Total Cover). Target makes DEX save or takes **2 × Martial Arts die + DEX modifier** damage of the same type.

### Level 3: Monk Subclass
Choose a subclass (see below). Subclass features at levels 3, 6, 11, and 17.

### Level 4: Ability Score Improvement
Gain the ASI feat or another feat. Also at levels 8, 12, and 16.

### Level 4: Slow Fall
**Reaction** when falling: reduce fall damage by **5 × Monk level**.

### Level 5: Extra Attack
Attack twice when you take the Attack action.

### Level 5: Stunning Strike
Once per turn when you hit with a Monk weapon or Unarmed Strike, expend 1 FP. Target makes **CON save**. Failed: Stunned until start of your next turn. Success: target's Speed halved until start of your next turn, and next attack against it has Advantage.

### Level 6: Empowered Strikes
Unarmed Strike damage can be **Force** damage (your choice) instead of its normal type.

### Level 7: Evasion
DEX save for half damage → take no damage on success, half on failure. Doesn't work if Incapacitated.

### Level 9: Acrobatic Movement
While not wearing armor or wielding a Shield: move along vertical surfaces and across liquids on your turn without falling during the movement.

### Level 10: Heightened Focus
Enhanced versions of core Focus abilities:
- **Flurry of Blows (1 FP):** Make **three** Unarmed Strikes instead of two.
- **Patient Defense (1 FP):** Also gain Temporary HP equal to **2 × Martial Arts die**.
- **Step of the Wind (1 FP):** Bring one willing Large-or-smaller creature within 5 ft with you; their movement doesn't provoke OAs.

### Level 10: Self-Restoration
At the end of each of your turns, remove one condition: Charmed, Frightened, or Poisoned. Also: no Exhaustion from forgoing food/drink.

### Level 13: Deflect Energy
Deflect Attacks now works against **all damage types**, not just Bludgeoning/Piercing/Slashing.

### Level 14: Disciplined Survivor
Proficiency in **all saving throws**. When you fail a save, expend 1 FP to reroll (must use new roll).

### Level 15: Perfect Focus
When you roll Initiative and don't use Uncanny Metabolism, regain Focus Points until you have **4** (if you have 3 or fewer).

### Level 18: Superior Defense
Start of your turn: expend 3 FP for **Resistance to all damage except Force** for 1 minute or until Incapacitated.

### Level 19: Epic Boon
Gain an Epic Boon feat (Boon of Irresistible Offense recommended) or another feat.

### Level 20: Body and Mind
DEX and WIS scores increase by **4**, to a maximum of **25**.

## Subclasses

The 2024 PHB includes four Monk subclasses: **Warrior of Mercy**, **Warrior of Shadow**, **Warrior of the Elements**, and **Warrior of the Open Hand**.

### Warrior of Mercy

*Manipulate Forces of Life and Death*

Wandering physicians who bring healing and swift death in equal measure.

#### Level 3: Hand of Harm
Once per turn when you hit a creature with an Unarmed Strike and deal damage, expend 1 FP to deal extra **Necrotic damage** equal to 1 Martial Arts die + WIS modifier.

#### Level 3: Hand of Healing
**Magic Action**, expend 1 FP: touch a creature, restore HP equal to 1 Martial Arts die + WIS modifier. When using Flurry of Blows, replace one Unarmed Strike with Hand of Healing **without** expending a Focus Point.

#### Level 3: Implements of Mercy
Gain proficiency in Insight, Medicine, and Herbalism Kit.

#### Level 6: Physician's Touch
- **Hand of Harm:** Can also give the target the **Poisoned** condition until end of your next turn.
- **Hand of Healing:** Can also end one condition: Blinded, Deafened, Paralyzed, Poisoned, or Stunned.

#### Level 11: Flurry of Healing and Harm
- Replace **each** Flurry of Blows Unarmed Strike with Hand of Healing (no FP cost).
- Use Hand of Harm on a Flurry of Blows Unarmed Strike **without** expending a FP (still once per turn).
- Total uses of these benefits = WIS modifier per Long Rest.

#### Level 17: Hand of Ultimate Mercy
**Magic Action:** Touch a corpse that died within 24 hours, expend 5 FP. Creature returns to life with **4d10 + WIS modifier** HP. Removes: Blinded, Deafened, Paralyzed, Poisoned, Stunned. Once per Long Rest.

### Warrior of Shadow

*Harness Shadow Power for Stealth and Subterfuge*

Shadow monks draw on the power of the Shadowfell for stealth, teleportation, and darkness manipulation.

#### Level 3: Shadow Arts
- **Darkness (1 FP):** Cast *Darkness* without components. You can see within the spell's area. Can move the darkness area to a space within 60 ft at the start of each turn.
- **Darkvision:** Gain Darkvision 60 ft (or extend existing by 60 ft).
- **Shadowy Figments:** Learn *Minor Illusion* (WIS as spellcasting ability).

#### Level 6: Shadow Step
While entirely in Dim Light or Darkness, **Bonus Action** to teleport up to 60 ft to an unoccupied space also in Dim Light/Darkness. Advantage on next melee attack before end of turn.

#### Level 11: Improved Shadow Step
When using Shadow Step, expend 1 FP to remove the Dim Light/Darkness requirement. Can make an Unarmed Strike immediately after teleporting as part of the Bonus Action.

#### Level 17: Cloak of Shadows
**Magic Action** while in Dim Light/Darkness, expend 3 FP: shroud yourself in shadows for 1 minute (or until Incapacitated, or you end your turn in Bright Light).
- **Invisible** condition.
- **Partially Incorporeal:** Move through occupied spaces (Difficult Terrain).
- **Shadow Flurry:** Use Flurry of Blows without spending Focus Points.

### Warrior of the Elements

*Wield Strikes and Bursts of Elemental Power*

Elemental monks tap into the power of the Elemental Planes to empower their combat.

#### Level 3: Elemental Attunement
Start of your turn, expend 1 FP: imbue yourself with elemental energy for 10 minutes (or until Incapacitated).
- **Reach:** Unarmed Strike reach is **10 ft greater** than normal.
- **Elemental Strikes:** Unarmed Strike damage can be Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder. When dealing one of these types, force target to make STR save; on fail, move target 10 ft toward or away from you.

#### Level 3: Manipulate Elements
Know the *Elementalism* cantrip (WIS as spellcasting ability).

#### Level 6: Elemental Burst
**Magic Action**, expend 2 FP: 20-ft-radius Sphere within 120 ft. Choose Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder. Each creature makes DEX save — failed: **3 × Martial Arts die** damage; success: half.

#### Level 11: Stride of the Elements
While Elemental Attunement is active, gain **Fly Speed and Swim Speed** equal to your Speed.

#### Level 17: Elemental Epitome
While Elemental Attunement is active:
- **Damage Resistance:** Choose Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder (can change at start of each turn).
- **Destructive Stride:** When using Step of the Wind, Speed +20 ft; creatures of your choice within 5 ft take 1 Martial Arts die of chosen elemental damage (once per turn per creature).
- **Empowered Strikes:** Once per turn, deal extra damage equal to 1 Martial Arts die on an Unarmed Strike hit.

---

### Warrior of the Open Hand

*Master Unarmed Combat Techniques*

The classic martial artist, focused on grappling, pushing, and lethal unarmed strikes.

#### Level 3: Open Hand Technique
When you hit with a Flurry of Blows attack, impose one effect:
- **Addle:** Target can't make Opportunity Attacks until start of its next turn.
- **Push:** Target must make STR save or be pushed up to 15 ft away.
- **Topple:** Target must make DEX save or gain the Prone condition.

#### Level 6: Wholeness of Body
**Bonus Action:** Roll Martial Arts die; regain HP equal to die roll + WIS modifier (min 1). Uses equal to WIS modifier per Long Rest.

#### Level 11: Fleet Step
When you take a Bonus Action other than Step of the Wind, you can also use Step of the Wind immediately after.

#### Level 17: Quivering Palm
When you hit with an Unarmed Strike, expend 4 FP to start imperceptible vibrations lasting a number of days equal to Monk level. As an action (or forgoing one Attack action attack), end the vibrations. Target makes CON save: failed = **10d12 Force damage**; success = half. Only one target at a time.

## 2024 Changes (vs. 2014 PHB)

- **Ki → Focus Points:** "Ki Points" renamed to "Focus Points" throughout. Functionally similar but with new features.
- **Martial Arts Die** scales higher: reaches 1d12 at level 17 (was 1d10 max in 2014).
- **Monk weapons** expanded: now includes Martial Melee weapons with the Light property.
- **Patient Defense and Step of the Wind** now have free baseline versions (Disengage or Dash for free), with enhanced Focus Point versions.
- **Uncanny Metabolism (new, level 2):** Regain all Focus Points and some HP when rolling Initiative. Huge sustainability buff.
- **Deflect Attacks (replaces Deflect Missiles):** Now works on all melee and ranged attacks with B/P/S damage (not just ranged). The redirect costs 1 FP.
- **Stunning Strike** reworked: Now once per turn (not per hit). On a successful save, target still gets Speed halved and next attack has Advantage (partial failure).
- **Empowered Strikes (level 6):** Unarmed Strikes can deal Force damage (replaces the 2014 "counts as magical").
- **Heightened Focus (new, level 10):** Upgraded versions of Flurry/Patient Defense/Step of the Wind.
- **Self-Restoration (level 10):** Removes conditions at end of each turn (replaces Purity of Body).
- **Deflect Energy (level 13):** Deflect Attacks works on all damage types.
- **Disciplined Survivor (level 14):** Proficiency in all saves + reroll on Focus Point spend (replaces Diamond Soul, now stronger).
- **Perfect Focus (new, level 15):** Baseline Focus Point recovery on Initiative.
- **Superior Defense (level 18):** Resistance to all damage except Force (replaces Empty Body's invisibility).
- **Body and Mind (new, level 20):** +4 to DEX and WIS (max 25).
- **Subclass names changed:** Way of Mercy → Warrior of Mercy; Way of Shadow → Warrior of Shadow; Way of the Four Elements → Warrior of the Elements (completely reworked); Way of the Open Hand → Warrior of the Open Hand.
- **Warrior of the Elements** is completely redesigned — no longer a clunky spell-like system; now a smooth elemental attunement with reach, element swapping, and bursts.
- **Subclass feature levels changed:** Now at 3/6/11/17 (was 3/6/11/17 in some, 3/6/11/17 for others — now standardized).
