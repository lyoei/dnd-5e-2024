# Fighter

> *Source: 2024 Player's Handbook*

Fighters rule many battlefields. Questing knights, royal champions, elite soldiers, and hardened mercenaries — as Fighters, they all share an unparalleled prowess with weapons and armor. They master various weapon techniques, and a well-equipped Fighter always has the right tool at hand for any combat situation.

## Class Overview

| Trait | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Strength or Dexterity |
| **Hit Point Die** | d10 per Fighter level |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Strength and Constitution |
| **Skill Proficiencies** | Choose 2: Acrobatics, Animal Handling, Athletics, History, Insight, Intimidation, Persuasion, Perception, or Survival |
| **Weapon Proficiencies** | Simple and Martial weapons |
| **Armor Training** | Light, Medium, and Heavy armor and Shields |
| **Starting Equipment** | (A) Chain Mail, Greatsword, Flail, 8 Javelins, Dungeoneer's Pack, 4 GP; (B) Studded Leather Armor, Scimitar, Shortsword, Longbow, 20 Arrows, Quiver, Dungeoneer's Pack, 11 GP; or (C) 155 GP |

## Class Feature Descriptions

### Level 1: Fighting Style
You gain a Fighting Style feat of your choice (Defense is recommended). Whenever you gain a Fighter level, you can replace the feat with a different Fighting Style feat.

### Level 1: Second Wind
As a Bonus Action, you regain Hit Points equal to **1d10 + your Fighter level**. You can use this feature twice, regaining one use on a Short Rest and all uses on a Long Rest. Uses increase at higher levels (see table).

### Level 1: Weapon Mastery
You can use the mastery properties of **3** kinds of Simple or Martial weapons of your choice. You can change one weapon choice whenever you finish a Long Rest. The number of mastery weapons increases at higher levels (see table).

### Level 2: Action Surge
On your turn, you can take one additional action (except the Magic action). Once used, you must finish a Short or Long Rest to use it again. At level 17, you can use it twice before a rest but only once per turn.

### Level 2: Tactical Mind
When you fail an ability check, you can expend a use of Second Wind to roll 1d10 and add it to the check (instead of regaining HP). If the check still fails, the Second Wind use isn't expended.

### Level 3: Fighter Subclass
Choose a subclass (see below). Subclass features are gained at levels 3, 7, 10, 15, and 18.

### Level 4: Ability Score Improvement
Gain the Ability Score Improvement feat or another feat. Also gained at levels 6, 8, 12, 14, and 16.

### Level 5: Extra Attack
Attack twice when you take the Attack action.

### Level 5: Tactical Shift
When you activate Second Wind as a Bonus Action, you can move up to half your Speed without provoking Opportunity Attacks.

### Level 9: Indomitable
When you fail a saving throw, you can reroll it with a bonus equal to your Fighter level. You must use the new roll. Resets on a Long Rest. Gains additional uses at level 13 (two) and level 17 (three).

### Level 9: Tactical Master
When you attack with a weapon whose mastery property you can use, you can replace that property with **Push**, **Sap**, or **Slow** for that attack.

### Level 11: Two Extra Attacks
Attack three times when you take the Attack action.

### Level 13: Studied Attacks
If you miss an attack roll against a creature, you have Advantage on your next attack roll against that creature before the end of your next turn.

### Level 19: Epic Boon
Gain an Epic Boon feat (Boon of Combat Prowess recommended) or another feat.

### Level 20: Three Extra Attacks
Attack four times when you take the Attack action.

## Subclasses

The 2024 PHB includes four Fighter subclasses: **Battle Master**, **Champion**, **Eldritch Knight**, and **Psi Warrior**. The **Banneret** is available from *Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn* (supplement).

### Battle Master

*Master Sophisticated Battle Maneuvers*

Battle Masters are students of the art of battle, learning martial techniques passed down through generations, fueled by Superiority Dice.

#### Level 3: Combat Superiority
- Learn **3 maneuvers** (from the list below). Learn 2 more at levels 7, 10, and 15. Can replace one known maneuver each time you learn new ones.
- Start with **4 Superiority Dice (d8s)**. Gain one more at level 7 (5 total) and level 15 (6 total). All expended dice regain on Short or Long Rest.
- **Maneuver save DC** = 8 + Proficiency Bonus + Strength or Dexterity modifier (your choice).
- Only one maneuver per attack.

#### Level 3: Student of War
Gain proficiency with one Artisan's Tools and one skill from the Fighter skill list.

#### Level 7: Know Your Enemy
As a Bonus Action, discern a creature's Immunities, Resistances, and Vulnerabilities (within 30 ft). Once per Long Rest, or restore a use by expending one Superiority Die.

#### Level 10: Improved Combat Superiority
Superiority Die becomes a **d10**.

#### Level 15: Relentless
Once per turn, when you use a maneuver, you can roll **1d8** instead of expending a Superiority Die.

#### Level 18: Ultimate Combat Superiority
Superiority Die becomes a **d12**.

#### Maneuver Options

| Maneuver | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Ambush** | Add Superiority Die to Stealth checks or Initiative rolls. |
| **Bait and Switch** | Switch places with willing creature within 5 ft; one of you gains AC bonus equal to die roll until start of your next turn. |
| **Commander's Strike** | Replace one attack; a willing ally uses Reaction to attack, adding Superiority Die to damage. |
| **Commanding Presence** | Add Superiority Die to Intimidation, Performance, or Persuasion checks. |
| **Disarming Attack** | Add die to damage; target must make STR save or drop one held object. |
| **Distracting Strike** | Add die to damage; next attack roll against target by another attacker has Advantage. |
| **Evasive Footwork** | Bonus Action to Disengage + add die roll to AC until start of next turn. |
| **Feinting Attack** | Bonus Action; Advantage on next attack against a creature within 5 ft + add die to damage on hit. |
| **Goading Attack** | Add die to damage; target must make WIS save or have Disadvantage on attacks against others. |
| **Lunging Attack** | Bonus Action Dash; if you move 5+ ft straight and hit with melee, add die to damage. |
| **Maneuvering Attack** | Add die to damage; a willing ally can use Reaction to move half Speed without provoking OAs from target. |
| **Menacing Attack** | Add die to damage; target must make WIS save or be Frightened until end of your next turn. |
| **Parry** | Reaction when hit by melee attack; reduce damage by die roll + STR or DEX modifier. |
| **Precision Attack** | On a miss, add die roll to the attack roll (potentially turning it into a hit). |
| **Pushing Attack** | Add die to damage; Large or smaller target must make STR save or be pushed up to 15 ft. |
| **Rally** | Bonus Action; an ally within 30 ft gains Temporary HP equal to die roll + half Fighter level. |
| **Riposte** | Reaction when creature misses you with melee; make a melee attack, adding die to damage on hit. |
| **Sweeping Attack** | When you hit a creature with melee, choose another creature within 5 ft and your reach; if original roll would hit, it takes Superiority Die damage. |
| **Tactical Assessment** | Add Superiority Die to History, Investigation, or Insight checks. |
| **Trip Attack** | Add die to damage; Large or smaller target must make STR save or be knocked Prone. |

### Champion

*Pursue Physical Excellence in Combat*

Champions focus on relentless pursuit of martial prowess and physical excellence.

#### Level 3: Improved Critical
Attack rolls with weapons and Unarmed Strikes crit on **19 or 20**.

#### Level 3: Remarkable Athlete
Advantage on Initiative rolls and Strength (Athletics) checks. Immediately after scoring a Critical Hit, move up to half your Speed without provoking Opportunity Attacks.

#### Level 7: Additional Fighting Style
Gain another Fighting Style feat of your choice.

#### Level 10: Heroic Warrior
During combat, you can give yourself **Heroic Inspiration** at the start of your turn if you don't already have it.

#### Level 15: Superior Critical
Attack rolls with weapons and Unarmed Strikes crit on **18–20**.

#### Level 18: Survivor
- **Defy Death:** Advantage on Death Saving Throws. Rolls of 18–20 on Death Saves gain the benefit of rolling a 20.
- **Heroic Rally:** At the start of each of your turns, regain HP equal to 5 + CON modifier if you are Bloodied and have at least 1 HP.

### Eldritch Knight

*Support Combat Skills with Arcane Magic*

Eldritch Knights combine martial mastery with careful study of Wizard magic.

#### Level 3: Spellcasting
- Learn **2 Wizard cantrips** (Ray of Frost and Shocking Grasp recommended). Gain a 3rd cantrip at level 10.
- Prepare **3 Wizard spells** at level 3 (increases per table below). Uses **Intelligence** as spellcasting ability.
- Spellcasting Focus: Arcane Focus.

#### Level 3: War Bond
Ritual (1 hour, can be done during Short Rest) to bond with a weapon. Can't be disarmed of it; can summon it as a Bonus Action from same plane. Up to 2 bonded weapons (summon 1 at a time).

#### Level 7: War Magic
When you take the Attack action, you can replace one attack with a Wizard cantrip (casting time: action).

#### Level 10: Eldritch Strike
When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, it has Disadvantage on the next saving throw it makes against a spell you cast before the end of your next turn.

#### Level 15: Arcane Charge
When you use Action Surge, you can teleport up to 30 ft to an unoccupied space you can see (before or after the additional action).

#### Level 18: Improved War Magic
When you take the Attack action, you can replace **two** attacks with a casting of a level 1 or 2 Wizard spell (casting time: action).

### Psi Warrior

*Augment Physical Might with Psionic Power*

Psi Warriors awaken the power of their minds to infuse weapon strikes, lash out telekinetically, and create barriers of mental force.

#### Level 3: Psionic Power
Gain **Psionic Energy Dice** that fuel subclass powers:

| Fighter Level | Die Size | Number |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 3 | d6 | 4 |
| 5 | d8 | 6 |
| 9 | d8 | 8 |
| 11 | d10 | 8 |
| 13 | d10 | 10 |
| 17 | d12 | 12 |

Regain 1 die on Short Rest, all on Long Rest.

- **Protective Field (Reaction):** When you or a creature within 30 ft takes damage, expend 1 die; reduce damage by die roll + Intelligence modifier (min 1).
- **Psionic Strike:** Once per turn after hitting a target within 30 ft with a weapon, expend 1 die; deal extra Force damage equal to die roll + Intelligence modifier.
- **Telekinetic Movement (Magic Action):** Move a loose Large-or-smaller object or willing creature (not yourself) within 30 ft up to 30 ft. Once per Short/Long Rest, or expend a die to restore use.

#### Level 7: Telekinetic Adept
- **Psi-Powered Leap (Bonus Action):** Gain Fly Speed equal to twice your Speed until end of turn. Once per Short/Long Rest or expend a die to restore.
- **Telekinetic Thrust:** When you deal damage with Psionic Strike, force target to make STR save (DC 8 + INT mod + Prof). On fail, knock Prone or push 10 ft horizontally.

#### Level 10: Guarded Mind
Resistance to Psychic damage. At start of your turn, expend a die (no action) to end Charmed or Frightened on yourself.

#### Level 15: Bulwark of Force
Bonus Action: choose creatures (including yourself) within 30 ft, up to INT modifier. Each gains **Half Cover** for 1 minute or until you're Incapacitated. Once per Long Rest or expend a die to restore.

#### Level 18: Telekinetic Master
Always have *Telekinesis* prepared. Cast it without a spell slot or components (INT as ability). While concentrating on it, make one weapon attack as a Bonus Action each turn. Once per Long Rest or expend a die to restore.

## 2024 Changes (vs. 2014 PHB)

- **Weapon Mastery** is entirely new — Fighters get the most mastery weapons of any class (3 at level 1, up to 6 at level 16).
- **Second Wind** now scales with more uses (up to 4) and fuels **Tactical Mind** (level 2) and **Tactical Shift** (level 5).
- **Tactical Mind** (new): Spend Second Wind on ability checks instead of healing.
- **Tactical Shift** (new): Move half speed without OAs when using Second Wind.
- **Tactical Master** (new, level 9): Swap weapon mastery properties to Push/Sap/Slow.
- **Studied Attacks** (new, level 13): Advantage on next attack after a miss.
- **Action Surge** now explicitly excludes the Magic action.
- **Indomitable** now adds Fighter level as a bonus to the reroll (much stronger than 2014's flat reroll).
- **Extra Attacks** progression unchanged (2 at 5, 3 at 11, 4 at 20).
- **Psi Warrior** is new to the PHB (was in Tasha's Cauldron of Everything in 2014).
- **Champion** reworked: Remarkable Athlete grants Advantage on Initiative and Athletics; Heroic Warrior gives free Heroic Inspiration; Survivor reworked with Defy Death and Heroic Rally (Bloodied condition trigger).
- **Battle Master** maneuver list expanded/revised (e.g., Ambush, Bait and Switch, Tactical Assessment added; some maneuvers reworked). Know Your Enemy is now a Bonus Action with Superiority Die recharge. Relentless gives a free d8 instead of regaining a die.
- **Eldritch Knight** now uses prepared spells (not known spells) from the full Wizard list. War Bond revised. School restrictions removed.
- **ASI** at levels 4, 6, 8, 12, 14, 16 (6 total — most of any class).
- **Epic Boon** at level 19 (new).
