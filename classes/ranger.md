# Ranger

> *Source: 2024 Player's Handbook*

Far from bustling cities, amid the trees of trackless forests and across wide plains, Rangers keep their unending watch in the wilderness. Thanks to their connection with nature, Rangers can also cast spells that harness primal powers of the wilderness. A Ranger's talents and magic are honed with deadly focus to protect the world from the ravages of monsters and tyrants.

## Class Feature Descriptions

### Level 1: Spellcasting
- **Half-caster** with Ranger spell list. Spellcasting ability: **Wisdom**.
- **Prepared spells** system. Can swap one spell per Long Rest.
- Spellcasting Focus: **Druidic Focus**.

### Level 1: Favored Enemy
Always have *Hunter's Mark* prepared. Can cast it **without a spell slot** twice at level 1, scaling up to 6 free casts at level 17 (see table). Regain all free uses on Long Rest.

### Level 1: Weapon Mastery
Use mastery properties of **2** kinds of weapons. Can change choices on Long Rest.

### Level 2: Deft Explorer
- **Expertise:** Choose one skill proficiency you lack Expertise in; gain Expertise.
- **Languages:** Learn 2 additional languages.

### Level 2: Fighting Style
Gain a Fighting Style feat. Alternatively, choose **Druidic Warrior**: learn 2 Druid cantrips (count as Ranger spells, use WIS). Can replace one cantrip per Ranger level.

### Level 3: Ranger Subclass
Choose a subclass (see below). Subclass features at levels 3, 7, 11, and 15.

### Level 4: Ability Score Improvement
Gain ASI feat or another feat. Also at levels 8, 12, and 16.

### Level 5: Extra Attack
Attack twice when you take the Attack action.

### Level 6: Roving
Speed increases by **10 ft** while not wearing Heavy Armor. Gain **Climb Speed** and **Swim Speed** equal to your Speed.

### Level 9: Expertise
Choose 2 skill proficiencies you lack Expertise in; gain Expertise in both.

### Level 10: Tireless
- **Temporary HP (Action):** As an action, gain **1d8 + WIS modifier** (minimum of 1) Temporary HP. Uses equal to WIS modifier (minimum of once) per Long Rest.
- **Decrease Exhaustion:** Whenever you finish a Short Rest, your Exhaustion level decreases by 1.

### Level 13: Relentless Hunter
Taking damage **cannot break your Concentration** on *Hunter's Mark*.

### Level 14: Nature's Veil
**Bonus Action:** Give yourself the **Invisible** condition until end of your next turn. Uses equal to WIS modifier per Long Rest.

### Level 17: Precise Hunter
You have **Advantage on attack rolls** against the creature currently marked by your *Hunter's Mark*.

### Level 18: Feral Senses
Gain **Blindsight 30 ft**.

### Level 19: Epic Boon
Gain an Epic Boon feat (Boon of Dimensional Travel recommended) or another feat.

### Level 20: Foe Slayer
*Hunter's Mark* damage die becomes **d10** instead of d6.

## Subclasses

The 2024 PHB includes four Ranger subclasses: **Beast Master**, **Fey Wanderer**, **Gloom Stalker**, and **Hunter**.

### Beast Master

*Bond with a Primal Beast*

Beast Masters form a mystical bond with a primal beast companion.

#### Level 3: Primal Companion
Summon a primal beast — choose one stat block:

| Stat Block | Size | HP | Speed | Key Trait |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Beast of the Land** | Medium | 5 + 5 × Ranger level | 40 ft, Climb 40 ft | Charge: +1d6 damage + Prone (Large or smaller) on 20 ft straight move |
| **Beast of the Sky** | Small | 4 + 4 × Ranger level | 10 ft, Fly 60 ft | Flyby: No OAs when flying out of reach |
| **Beast of the Sea** | Medium | 5 + 5 × Ranger level | 5 ft, Swim 60 ft | Amphibious; Grapple on hit |

All beasts share:
- **AC:** 13 + your WIS modifier
- **Attack bonus:** Your spell attack modifier
- **Primal Bond:** Add your Proficiency Bonus to beast's ability checks and saves
- **Understands** your known languages
- Acts on your turn; needs your **Bonus Action** to take actions beyond Dodge. You can also sacrifice one of your attacks to command Beast's Strike.
- Revive with a **Magic Action + spell slot** if died within 1 hour (returns in 1 minute with full HP).
- Summon a different beast on Long Rest.

#### Level 7: Exceptional Training
When you command the beast as a Bonus Action, it can also use its Bonus Action for Dash, Disengage, Dodge, or Help. Beast attacks can deal **Force damage** instead of normal type.

#### Level 11: Bestial Fury
Beast's Strike can be used **twice** when commanded. First time each turn the beast hits a *Hunter's Mark* target, it deals the spell's extra **Force damage**.

#### Level 15: Share Spells
When you cast a spell targeting yourself, also affect your beast if it's within 30 ft.

### Fey Wanderer

*Wield Fey Mirth and Fury*

A Ranger touched by the Feywild, blending charm and martial prowess.

#### Fey Wanderer Spells

| Ranger Level | Spell |
|:---:|---|
| 3 | Charm Person |
| 5 | Misty Step |
| 9 | Summon Fey |
| 13 | Dimension Door |
| 17 | Mislead |

#### Level 3: Dreadful Strikes
When you hit with a weapon, deal extra **1d4 Psychic damage** (once per turn). Increases to **1d6** at Ranger level 11.

#### Level 3: Otherworldly Glamour
Add **WIS modifier** (minimum +1) to all Charisma checks. Gain proficiency in one of: Deception, Performance, or Persuasion.

#### Level 7: Beguiling Twist
Advantage on saves to avoid/end Charmed or Frightened. When you or a creature within 120 ft succeeds on such a save, **Reaction:** force a different creature within 120 ft to make WIS save or be Charmed or Frightened (your choice) for 1 minute (repeats save each turn).

#### Level 11: Fey Reinforcements
Cast *Summon Fey* without a Material component. **Once per Long Rest** without a spell slot. Can modify it to not require Concentration (duration becomes 1 minute).

#### Level 15: Misty Wanderer
Cast *Misty Step* **without a spell slot** (WIS modifier times per Long Rest). When you cast it, bring one willing creature within 5 ft along (teleports to within 5 ft of your destination).

### Gloom Stalker

*Draw on Shadow Magic to Fight Your Foes*

At home in the darkest places, wielding Shadowfell magic for ambushes and fear.

#### Gloom Stalker Spells

| Ranger Level | Spell |
|:---:|---|
| 3 | Disguise Self |
| 5 | Rope Trick |
| 9 | Fear |
| 13 | Greater Invisibility |
| 17 | Seeming |

#### Level 3: Dread Ambusher
- **Ambusher's Leap:** First turn of combat, Speed +10 ft.
- **Dreadful Strike:** When you hit with a weapon, deal extra **2d6 Psychic damage**. Once per turn, uses equal to WIS modifier per Long Rest.
- **Initiative Bonus:** Add WIS modifier to Initiative rolls.

#### Level 3: Umbral Sight
Gain **Darkvision 60 ft** (or extend existing by 60 ft). While entirely in Darkness, you have the **Invisible** condition to any creature relying on Darkvision to see you.

#### Level 7: Iron Mind
Gain proficiency in **Wisdom saving throws**. If already proficient, choose Intelligence or Charisma saves instead.

#### Level 11: Stalker's Flurry
Dreadful Strike damage increases to **2d8**. When using Dreadful Strike, choose one additional effect:
- **Sudden Strike:** Make another attack with the same weapon against a **different creature** within 5 ft of the original target and in weapon range.
- **Mass Fear:** Target and creatures within 10 ft make WIS save or be **Frightened** until start of your next turn.

#### Level 15: Shadowy Dodge
**Reaction** when targeted by an attack: impose **Disadvantage** on the attack roll. Hit or miss, teleport up to **30 ft** to an unoccupied space you can see.

### Hunter

*Protect Nature and People from Destruction*

The versatile monster hunter, specializing in adaptable tactics.

#### Level 3: Hunter's Lore
While a creature is marked by *Hunter's Mark*, you know its **Immunities, Resistances, and Vulnerabilities**.

#### Level 3: Hunter's Prey
Choose one (swappable on Short or Long Rest):
- **Colossus Slayer:** When you hit a creature missing any HP, deal extra **1d8 damage** (once per turn).
- **Horde Breaker:** Once per turn when you make a weapon attack, make another attack with the same weapon against a **different creature** within 5 ft of the original target and in weapon range (haven't attacked this turn).

#### Level 7: Defensive Tactics
Choose one (swappable on Short or Long Rest):
- **Escape the Horde:** Opportunity Attacks have **Disadvantage** against you.
- **Multiattack Defense:** When a creature hits you, it has **Disadvantage** on all other attack rolls against you for the rest of the turn.

#### Level 11: Superior Hunter's Prey
Once per turn when you deal damage to a *Hunter's Mark* target, also deal the spell's extra damage to **another creature** you can see within 30 ft.

#### Level 15: Superior Hunter's Defense
**Reaction** when you take damage: gain **Resistance** to that damage type (and all other damage of the same type) until end of the current turn.

## 2024 Changes (vs. 2014 PHB)

- **Ranger is massively reworked** — widely considered the most improved class in the 2024 PHB.
- **Spellcasting at level 1** (was level 2 in 2014). Rangers are now half-casters from the start.
- **Favored Enemy completely redesigned:** No longer a ribbon feature about creature types. Now grants free *Hunter's Mark* castings (2–6 per Long Rest without spell slots). This is the Ranger's signature damage feature.
- **Natural Explorer removed entirely.** No more terrain-based ribbons.
- **Weapon Mastery** added (2 weapons).
- **Deft Explorer (level 2):** Grants Expertise and 2 languages. Cleaner than 2014's Favored Terrain.
- **Fighting Style** moved to level 2 (was level 2 in 2014). Now includes Druidic Warrior option.
- **Prepared spells** instead of known spells — much more flexible.
- **Spellcasting Focus:** Can now use a Druidic Focus (didn't have one in 2014).
- **Roving (level 6):** +10 ft Speed, plus Climb and Swim Speed. Replaces various terrain features.
- **Expertise (level 9):** Gain Expertise in 2 skills. Rangers now get 3 total Expertise skills (1 at level 2, 2 at level 9).
- **Tireless (level 10):** Temp HP and Exhaustion recovery — replaces Hide in Plain Sight.
- **Relentless Hunter (new, level 13):** Unbreakable *Hunter's Mark* Concentration from damage. Huge for the class identity.
- **Nature's Veil (level 14):** Multiple uses of free Invisibility. Replaces Vanish.
- **Precise Hunter (new, level 17):** Advantage on attacks against *Hunter's Mark* target. Excellent capstone progression.
- **Feral Senses (level 18):** Now grants **Blindsight 30 ft** (was a vague "sense invisible creatures" in 2014).
- **Foe Slayer (level 20):** *Hunter's Mark* die becomes d10. Simpler and more impactful than 2014's once-per-turn WIS bonus.
- **Subclass changes:**
  - **Beast Master** completely reworked with Primal Companion stat blocks (Beast of the Land/Sea/Sky). Beast scales with Ranger level and uses spell attack modifier. Much more viable than 2014.
  - **Fey Wanderer** is new to the PHB (was in Tasha's Cauldron of Everything).
  - **Gloom Stalker** is new to the PHB (was in Xanathar's Guide to Everything). Dread Ambusher reworked with Dreadful Strike (WIS mod uses).
  - **Hunter** streamlined: options are now swappable on Short/Long Rest. Superior Hunter's Prey and Defense are new.
- **3 skill proficiencies** at level 1 (was 3 in 2014 — unchanged, still the most of any class).
- **Subclass feature levels:** 3/7/11/15 (consistent across all subclasses).
- **Epic Boon** at level 19 (new).
