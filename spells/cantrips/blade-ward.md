# Blade Ward

*Cantrip, Abjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

Whenever a creature makes an attack roll against you before the spell ends, the attacker subtracts 1d4 from the attack roll.

## Spell Lists

Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

## 2024 Changes

Completely redesigned. No longer grants resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage for 1 round. Now subtracts 1d4 from attack rolls against you, requires concentration, and lasts up to 1 minute. Added to Bard spell list.
