# Chill Touch

*Cantrip, Necromancy*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

Channeling the chill of the grave, make a melee spell attack against a target within reach. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 Necrotic damage, and it can't regain Hit Points until the end of your next turn.

## At Higher Levels

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The damage increases by 1d10 when you reach levels 5 (2d10), 11 (3d10), and 17 (4d10).

## Spell Lists

Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

## 2024 Changes

Major rework. Range changed from 120 feet (ranged spell attack) to Touch (melee spell attack). Damage increased from 1d8 to 1d10. Removed the anti-undead secondary effect (disadvantage on attacks). Now actually a touch spell matching its name.
