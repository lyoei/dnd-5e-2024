# Poison Spray

*Cantrip, Necromancy*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You spray toxic mist at a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d12 Poison damage.

## At Higher Levels

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The damage increases by 1d12 when you reach levels 5 (2d12), 11 (3d12), and 17 (4d12).

## Spell Lists

Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

## 2024 Changes

Major improvements. Range increased from 10 feet to 30 feet. Changed from a Constitution saving throw to a ranged spell attack. School changed from Conjuration to Necromancy. Added Verbal component.
