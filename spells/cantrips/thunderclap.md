# Thunderclap

*Cantrip, Evocation*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

Each creature in a 5-foot Emanation originating from you must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d6 Thunder damage. The spell's thunderous sound can be heard up to 100 feet away.

## At Higher Levels

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The damage increases by 1d6 when you reach levels 5 (2d6), 11 (3d6), and 17 (4d6).

## Spell Lists

Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

## 2024 Changes

Previously from supplements (Elemental Evil/Xanathar's), now in the core PHB. Area changed from "5-foot radius" to "5-foot Emanation" (using 2024 area terminology). Removed the Verbal component.
