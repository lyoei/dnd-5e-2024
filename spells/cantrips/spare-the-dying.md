# Spare the Dying

*Cantrip, Necromancy*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 15 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

Choose a creature within range that has 0 Hit Points and isn't dead. The creature becomes Stable.

## At Higher Levels

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The range doubles when you reach levels 5 (30 feet), 11 (60 feet), and 17 (120 feet).

## Spell Lists

Cleric, Druid

## 2024 Changes

Range increased from Touch to 15 feet. Added cantrip scaling that doubles the range at higher levels. Added to the Druid spell list. No longer requires touch, making it safer to use in combat.
