# True Strike

*Cantrip, Divination*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** S, M (a weapon with which you have proficiency and that is worth 1+ CP)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

Guided by a flash of magical insight, you make one attack with the weapon used in the spell's casting. The attack uses your spellcasting ability for the attack and damage rolls instead of using Strength or Dexterity. If the attack deals damage, it can be Radiant damage or the weapon's normal damage type (your choice).

## At Higher Levels

**Cantrip Upgrade.** Whether you deal Radiant damage or the weapon's normal damage type, the attack deals extra Radiant damage when you reach levels 5 (1d6), 11 (2d6), and 17 (3d6).

## Spell Lists

Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

## 2024 Changes

Completely redesigned from the ground up. No longer grants advantage on your next attack (widely considered the worst cantrip in 2014). Now makes an actual weapon attack using spellcasting ability, with optional Radiant damage and cantrip scaling. Duration changed from Concentration/1 round to Instantaneous. A massive improvement.
