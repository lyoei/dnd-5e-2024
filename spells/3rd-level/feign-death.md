# Feign Death

*3rd-Level Necromancy*

- **Casting Time:** Action or Ritual
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of graveyard dirt)
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** Yes

## Description

You touch a willing creature and put it into a cataleptic state that is indistinguishable from death.

For the spell's duration, or until you use an action to touch the target and dismiss the spell, the target appears dead to all outward inspection and to spells used to determine the target's status. The target has the Blinded and Incapacitated conditions, and its Speed drops to 0. The target has Resistance to all damage except Psychic damage, and it has Immunity to the Poisoned condition and to disease.
