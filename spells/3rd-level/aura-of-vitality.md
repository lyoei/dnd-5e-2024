# Aura of Vitality

*3rd-Level Abjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Self (30-foot emanation)
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

An aura of healing energy radiates from you in a 30-foot Emanation for the duration. When you create this aura and at the start of each of your turns while it lasts, you can restore 2d6 Hit Points to one creature in it (no action required).

## At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the healing increases by 2d6 for each slot level above 3rd.

## Spell Lists

Cleric, Paladin, Druid

## 2024 Changes

Moved from Evocation to Abjuration. Healing is now automatic (no action required) instead of requiring a Bonus Action, making it significantly more powerful. Originally from Paladin-only, now available to Cleric and Druid as well.
