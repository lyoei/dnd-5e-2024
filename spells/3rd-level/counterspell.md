# Counterspell

*3rd-Level Abjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Reaction, which you take when you see a creature within 60 feet of yourself casting a spell with a Verbal, Somatic, or Material component
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You attempt to interrupt a creature in the process of casting a spell. The creature must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the spell fails and has no effect. On a successful save, the spell isn't interrupted, and you expend no spell slot for casting Counterspell.

## 2024 Changes

Major rework in 2024. Now requires a Constitution saving throw from the target instead of an ability check by the caster. The caster retains the spell slot if the Counterspell fails. No longer auto-succeeds against same-level or lower spells. Very controversial change.
