# Bestow Curse

*3rd-Level Necromancy*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You touch a creature, and that creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or become cursed for the duration. When you cast this spell, choose the nature of the curse from the following options:

- Choose one ability. The target has Disadvantage on ability checks and saving throws made with that ability.
- While the target is cursed, your attacks and spells deal an extra 1d8 Necrotic damage to it.
- While cursed, the target must make a Wisdom saving throw at the start of each of its turns. If it fails, it wastes its action that turn doing nothing.
- While the target is cursed, it can't regain Hit Points.

A Remove Curse spell ends this effect. At the DM's option, you may choose an alternative curse effect, but it should be no more powerful than those described above.

## At Higher Levels

If you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the duration is Concentration, up to 10 minutes. If you use a 5th-level or higher spell slot, the duration is 8 hours and doesn't require Concentration. If you use a 7th-level or higher spell slot, the duration is until dispelled and doesn't require Concentration.
