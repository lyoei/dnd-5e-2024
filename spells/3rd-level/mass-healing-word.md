# Mass Healing Word

*3rd-Level Evocation*

- **Casting Time:** Bonus Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

As you call out words of restoration, up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within range regain Hit Points equal to 2d4 + your spellcasting ability modifier.

## At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the healing increases by 1d4 for each slot level above 3rd.

## Spell Lists

Cleric, Bard

## 2024 Changes

Healing die changed from 1d4 to 2d4 base.
