# Crusader's Mantle

*3rd-Level Evocation*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Self (30-foot emanation)
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

Holy power radiates from you in a 30-foot Emanation, awakening boldness in your allies. Until the spell ends, each nonhostile creature in the Emanation (including you) deals an extra 1d4 Radiant damage when it hits with a weapon or an Unarmed Strike.
