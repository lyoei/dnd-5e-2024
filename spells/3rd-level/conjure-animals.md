# Conjure Animals

*3rd-Level Conjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You conjure nature spirits that take the form of spectral animals in unoccupied spaces that you can see within range. Choose one of the following options for what appears:

The spirits take whatever animal forms you choose. Each spirit has the statistics of the Conjure Animals stat block, which uses your spell attack modifier and spell save DC.

On each of your turns, you can take a Bonus Action to move each of the spirits up to 30 feet to a space you can see. Each spirit's space is Difficult Terrain for your enemies.

## At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you conjure additional spirits and their damage increases, as noted in the stat block.

## 2024 Changes

Completely reworked in 2024. No longer summons actual animals with independent stat blocks. Instead creates spectral spirits with a standardized stat block. Much simpler to run but loses utility aspects of summoning specific animals.
