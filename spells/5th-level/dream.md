# Dream

*5th-level Illusion*

- **Casting Time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** Special
- **Components:** V, S, M (a handful of sand, a dab of ink, and a writing quill plucked from a sleeping bird)
- **Duration:** 8 hours
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

This spell shapes a creature's dreams. Choose a creature known to you as the target of this spell. The target must be on the same plane of existence as you. You, or a willing creature you touch, enters a trance state, acting as a messenger. While in the trance, the messenger is aware of their surroundings but can't take actions or move. If the target is asleep, the messenger appears in the target's dreams and can converse with the target. The messenger can also shape the environment of the dream. If the target is awake, the messenger knows it and can either end the trance or wait for the target to fall asleep.

## At Higher Levels

No additional effect at higher levels.
