# Hallow

*5th-level Abjuration*

- **Casting Time:** 24 hours
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (herbs, oils, and incense worth at least 1,000 GP, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Until dispelled
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You touch a point and infuse an area around it with holy or unholy power. The area can have a radius up to 60 feet. Celestials, Elementals, Fey, Fiends, and Undead can't enter the area, nor can such creatures charm, frighten, or possess creatures within it. You can designate additional effects within the area from a list including Courage, Darkness, Daylight, Energy Protection, Energy Vulnerability, Everlasting Rest, Extradimensional Interference, Fear, Silence, and Tongues.

## At Higher Levels

No additional effect at higher levels.
