# Telekinesis

*5th-level Transmutation*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You gain the ability to move or manipulate creatures or objects by thought. When you cast the spell, and as a Magic action on each of your turns for the duration, you can exert your will on one creature or object that you can see within range: Creature (Strength check contested by your spellcasting; on failure, you move it up to 30 feet and it has the Restrained condition until your next turn) or Object (you can move an object that weighs up to 1,000 pounds up to 30 feet; you can also exert fine control on objects).

## At Higher Levels

None.
