# Conjure Elemental

*5th-level Conjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You conjure a spirit from the Elemental Planes that assumes a form you choose: Air Elemental, Earth Elemental, Fire Elemental, or Water Elemental. The creature appears in an unoccupied space you can see within range and uses the stat block for the chosen elemental. The creature is friendly to you and your companions for the duration. In combat, the creature acts on your initiative count. It obeys your verbal commands (no action required). If you don't issue any commands, the creature defends itself but otherwise takes no action.

## At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the elemental's HP increases by 10 for each slot level above 5th.
