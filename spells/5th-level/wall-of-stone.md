# Wall of Stone

*5th-level Evocation*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a small block of granite)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

A nonmagical wall of solid stone springs into existence at a point you choose within range. The wall is 6 inches thick and is composed of ten 10-foot-by-10-foot panels. Each panel must be contiguous with at least one other panel. If the wall cuts through a creature's space when it appears, the creature is pushed to one side of the wall (your choice which side). If a creature would be surrounded on all sides by the wall, the creature can make a Dexterity saving throw. On a success, it can use its Reaction to move up to its Speed. Each panel has AC 15, 30 HP per inch of thickness, and Immunity to Poison and Psychic damage. If the wall is maintained for the full duration, it becomes permanent and can't be dispelled.
