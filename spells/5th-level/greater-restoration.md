# Greater Restoration

*5th-level Abjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (diamond dust worth at least 100 GP, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You touch a creature and end one of the following effects on it: one effect that has the Charmed or Petrified condition on the creature, one curse (including the target's attunement to a cursed magic item), any reduction to one of the target's ability scores, or one effect reducing the target's Hit Point maximum.

## At Higher Levels

None.

## Spell Lists

Artificer, Bard, Cleric, Druid

## 2024 Changes

Petrified condition added. Simplified list of removable effects.
