# Yolande's Regal Presence

*5th-level Enchantment*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (a miniature tiara)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You surround yourself with unearthly majesty in a 10-foot Emanation. Whenever an enemy starts its turn in the Emanation, it must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the Charmed condition until the start of its next turn. While Charmed by this spell, the creature has the Incapacitated condition. A creature that succeeds on this save is immune to your use of this spell for 24 hours.

## At Higher Levels

No additional effect at higher levels.
