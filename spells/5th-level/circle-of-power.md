# Circle of Power

*5th-level Abjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

Divine energy radiates from you in a 30-foot-radius Sphere, empowering your allies. Each friendly creature in the sphere (including you) has Advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. Additionally, when an affected creature succeeds on a saving throw against a spell or magical effect that allows a save for half damage, the creature takes no damage from it.
