# Awaken

*5th-level Transmutation*

- **Casting Time:** 8 hours
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (an agate worth at least 1,000 GP, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You spend the casting time tracing magical pathways within a precious gemstone, and then touch a Huge or smaller Beast or Plant. The target must have an Intelligence of 3 or less or be a natural plant. The target gains an Intelligence of 10 and the ability to speak one language you know. If the target is a Plant, it gains the ability to move its limbs, roots, vines, creepers, and so forth, and it gains senses similar to a human's. The awakened creature is charmed by you for 30 days or until you or your companions do anything harmful to it. When the charmed condition ends, the creature chooses whether to remain friendly to you.

## At Higher Levels

No additional effect at higher levels.
