# Banishing Smite

*5th-level Conjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

The next time you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack before this spell ends, your attack deals an extra 5d10 Force damage. If this attack reduces the target to 50 Hit Points or fewer, you banish it. If the target is native to a different plane of existence, the target disappears, returning to its home plane. If the target is native to the plane you're on, the creature vanishes into a harmless demiplane and is Incapacitated there. The spell ends on the target when it returns after 1 minute.
