# Dispel Evil and Good

*5th-level Abjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (holy water or powdered silver and iron)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

Shimmering energy surrounds and protects you from Aberrations, Celestials, Elementals, Fey, Fiends, and Undead. For the duration, those creature types have Disadvantage on attack rolls against you. You can end the spell early by using either of the following special functions: Break Enchantment (as an action, touch a creature charmed, frightened, or possessed by one of the listed creature types to end the condition) or Dismissal (as an action, make a melee spell attack against an Aberration, Celestial, Elemental, Fey, Fiend, or Undead; on a hit, you try to send it back to its home plane; it must succeed on a Charisma save or be sent back).

## At Higher Levels

No additional effect at higher levels.

## Spell Lists

Cleric, Paladin

## 2024 Changes

Uses melee spell attack for Dismissal function. Modernized conditions.
