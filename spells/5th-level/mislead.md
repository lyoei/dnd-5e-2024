# Mislead

*5th-level Illusion*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You become Invisible at the same time that an illusory double of you appears where you are standing. The double lasts for the duration. You can use a Bonus Action to move the illusory double up to twice your Speed and make it gesture, speak, and behave in whatever way you choose. You can see through its eyes and hear through its ears as if you were located where it is. On each of your turns, you can switch from using its senses to using your own, or vice versa. While you are using its senses, you have the Blinded and Deafened conditions regarding your own surroundings. The Invisible condition ends if you attack, cast a spell, or enter the double's space.

## At Higher Levels

None.
