# Jallarzi's Storm of Radiance

*5th-level Evocation*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of phosphorus)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You unleash a storm of flashing light and thunder in a 10-foot-radius, 40-foot-high Cylinder centered on a point you can see within range. Each creature in the Cylinder when it appears must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 2d10 Radiant damage and 2d10 Thunder damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature also makes this save when it enters the Cylinder for the first time on a turn or ends its turn there. The Cylinder is Heavily Obscured for creatures outside it.

## At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the Radiant damage and Thunder damage each increase by 1d10 for each slot level above 5th.
