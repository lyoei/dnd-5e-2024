# Antilife Shell

*5th-level Abjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

A shimmering barrier extends from you in a 10-foot-radius Sphere and moves with you, remaining centered on you. The barrier prevents creatures other than Undead and Constructs from passing or reaching through it. An affected creature can cast spells or make attacks with Ranged or Reach weapons through the barrier. If you move so that an affected creature is forced through the barrier, the spell ends.
