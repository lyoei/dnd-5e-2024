# Contagion

*5th-level Necromancy*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 7 days
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

Your touch inflicts a disease. Make a melee spell attack against a creature within your reach. On a hit, the creature has the Poisoned condition. At the end of each of the Poisoned creature's turns, the creature makes a Constitution saving throw. After three failed saves, the creature is no longer Poisoned but is subjected to a disease of your choice for the remaining duration. After three successful saves, the creature recovers from the spell. Available diseases include Blinding Sickness, Filth Fever, Flesh Rot, Mindfire, Seizure, and Slimy Doom.

## At Higher Levels

No additional effect at higher levels.

## Spell Lists

Cleric, Druid

## 2024 Changes

Disease takes effect after three failed saves. Creature is Poisoned initially instead of disease taking immediate effect.
