# Cone of Cold

*5th-level Evocation*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Self (60-foot cone)
- **Components:** V, S, M (a small crystal or glass cone)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

A blast of cold air erupts from your hands. Each creature in a 60-foot Cone originating from you must make a Constitution saving throw. A creature takes 8d8 Cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature killed by this spell becomes a frozen statue until it thaws.

## At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 5th.
