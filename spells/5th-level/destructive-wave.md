# Destructive Wave

*5th-level Evocation*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Self (30-foot radius)
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You strike the ground, creating a burst of divine energy that ripples outward from you. Each creature you choose within 30 feet of you must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 5d6 Thunder damage plus 5d6 Radiant or Necrotic damage (your choice), and the creature has the Prone condition. On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage only and isn't knocked Prone.

## At Higher Levels

No additional effect at higher levels.
