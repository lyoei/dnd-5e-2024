# Reincarnate

*5th-level Necromancy*

- **Casting Time:** 1 hour
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (rare oils and unguents worth at least 1,000 GP, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You touch a dead Humanoid or a piece of a dead Humanoid. Provided that the creature has been dead no longer than 10 days, the spell forms a new adult body for it and then calls the soul to enter that body. If the target's soul isn't free or willing, the spell fails. The DM rolls on the Reincarnate table to determine what form the creature takes when restored to life, or the DM chooses a form.

## At Higher Levels

No additional effect at higher levels.

## 2024 Changes

School changed from Transmutation to Necromancy. DM can choose the form instead of random roll.
