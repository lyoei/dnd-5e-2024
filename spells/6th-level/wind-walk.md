# Wind Walk

*6th-level Transmutation*

- **Casting Time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (fire and holy water)
- **Duration:** 8 hours
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You and up to ten willing creatures you can see within range assume a gaseous form for the duration, appearing as wisps of cloud. While in this cloud form, a creature has a Fly Speed of 300 feet and has Resistance to damage from nonmagical weapons. A creature in cloud form can't take any actions other than the Dash action or to revert to its normal form. Reverting takes 1 minute, during which the creature is Incapacitated and can't move. A creature can revert to cloud form, which also takes 1 minute.

## At Higher Levels

No additional effect at higher levels.
