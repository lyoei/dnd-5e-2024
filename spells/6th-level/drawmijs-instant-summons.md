# Drawmij's Instant Summons

*6th-level Conjuration*

- **Casting Time:** 1 minute (Ritual)
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a sapphire worth 1,000 GP)
- **Duration:** Until dispelled
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** Yes

## Description

You touch an object weighing 10 pounds or less whose longest dimension is 6 feet or less. The spell leaves an invisible mark on its surface and invisibly inscribes the name of the item on the sapphire you use as the material component. Each time you cast this spell, you must use a different sapphire. At any time thereafter, you can use your action to speak the item's name and crush the sapphire. The item instantly appears in your hand regardless of physical or planar distances.

## At Higher Levels

No additional effect at higher levels.
