# Disintegrate

*6th-level Transmutation*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a lodestone and a pinch of dust)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

A thin green ray springs from your pointing finger to a target that you can see within range. The target can be a creature, an object, or a creation of magical force, such as the wall created by Wall of Force. A creature targeted by this spell must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, the target takes 10d6 + 40 Force damage. The target is disintegrated if this damage reduces it to 0 Hit Points. A disintegrated creature and everything it is wearing and carrying are reduced to a pile of fine gray dust. A Large or smaller nonmagical object or a creation of magical force is automatically disintegrated.

## At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases by 3d6 for each slot level above 6th.

## 2024 Changes

No longer auto-kills on 0 HP (reduces to 0 HP then disintegrates). Minor wording updates.
