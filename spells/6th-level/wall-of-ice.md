# Wall of Ice

*6th-level Evocation*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a small piece of quartz)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You create a wall of ice on a solid surface within range. You can form it into a hemispherical dome or a sphere with a radius of up to 10 feet, or you can shape a flat surface made up of ten 10-foot-square panels. Each panel must be contiguous with another panel. The wall is 1 foot thick and lasts for the duration. If the wall cuts through a creature's space when it appears, the creature within its area is pushed to one side and must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 10d6 Cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Each 10-foot section has AC 12, 30 HP, and Vulnerability to Fire damage.

## At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases by 2d6 for each slot level above 6th.
