# Tasha's Bubbling Cauldron

*6th-level Conjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 5 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a cauldron worth at least 500 GP that the spell consumes if not already used for the spell)
- **Duration:** 10 minutes
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You conjure a claw-footed cauldron filled with bubbling liquid. The cauldron appears in an unoccupied space on the ground within 5 feet of you and is 3 feet in diameter. You and your allies can dip a vial into the cauldron to draw out a potion that lasts for the spell's duration. The cauldron can produce a number of potions equal to your spellcasting ability modifier (minimum 1). You choose the potion from the following options for each vial drawn: Potion of Healing (Greater), Potion of Fire Breath, Potion of Resistance, or Potion of Speed.
