# Heroes' Feast

*6th-level Conjuration*

- **Casting Time:** 10 minutes
- **Range:** Self (10-foot radius)
- **Components:** V, S, M (a gem-encrusted bowl worth at least 1,000 GP, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You conjure a magnificent feast that takes 1 hour to consume and disappears at the end of that time. Up to twelve creatures can partake of the feast. A creature that partakes gains several benefits: it is cured of all diseases and poisons, becomes immune to poison and the Frightened condition, makes all Wisdom saving throws with Advantage, and its Hit Point maximum increases by 2d10, and it gains the same number of Hit Points. These benefits last for 24 hours.

## At Higher Levels

No additional effect at higher levels.
