# Heal

*6th-level Abjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

Choose a creature that you can see within range. A surge of positive energy washes through the creature, causing it to regain 70 Hit Points. This spell also ends the Blinded, Deafened, and Poisoned conditions on the target.

## At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the amount of healing increases by 10 for each slot level above 6th.

School changed from Evocation to Abjuration. Removes specific conditions listed.
