# Create Undead

*6th-level Necromancy*

- **Casting Time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** 10 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (one clay pot filled with grave dirt, one clay pot filled with brackish water, and one 150 GP black onyx stone for each corpse)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You can cast this spell only at night. Choose up to three dead Humanoids within range. Each target is raised as a Ghoul under your control. On each of your turns, you can use a Bonus Action to mentally command your undead. You can reassert control by casting this spell again. If you cast Animate Dead, you can command undead from both spells. After 24 hours, the undead are no longer under your control.

## At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a 7th-level slot, you can create or reassert control over four Ghouls. Using an 8th-level slot, you can create Ghasts or Wights. Using a 9th-level slot, you can create Mummies.
