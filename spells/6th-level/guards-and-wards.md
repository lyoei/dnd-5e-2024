# Guards and Wards

*6th-level Abjuration*

- **Casting Time:** 10 minutes
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (burning incense, a small measure of brimstone and oil, a knotted string, a small amount of umber hulk blood, and a small silver rod worth at least 10 GP)
- **Duration:** 24 hours
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You create a ward that protects up to 2,500 square feet of floor space. The warded area can be up to 20 feet tall and shaped as you desire. You can ward several stories of a stronghold by dividing the area among them. When you cast this spell, you can specify individuals that are unaffected by the spell's effects. You can also choose one or more of the following effects: Corridors (fog fills corridors, making them Heavily Obscured), Doors (all doors are magically locked), Stairs (webs fill stairways), Other Effect (place one of Dancing Lights, Magic Mouth, Stinking Cloud, Gust of Wind, or Suggestion in a chosen area).
