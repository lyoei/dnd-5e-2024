# Arcane Gate

*6th-level Conjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 500 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You create linked teleportation portals that remain open for the duration. Choose two points on the ground that you can see, one point within 10 feet of you and one point within 500 feet of you. A circular portal, 10 feet in diameter, opens over each point. A creature that enters one portal exits from the other portal as if the two were adjacent to each other. You can change the destination portal as a Bonus Action.

## At Higher Levels

No additional effect at higher levels.
