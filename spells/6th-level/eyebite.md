# Eyebite

*6th-level Necromancy*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

For the spell's duration, your eyes become an inky void imbued with dread power. One creature of your choice within 60 feet of you that you can see must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be affected by one of the following effects of your choice: Asleep (falls Unconscious), Panicked (has the Frightened condition, must Dash away), or Sickened (has Disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks). Until the spell ends, you can use a Magic action on each of your turns to target another creature.

## At Higher Levels

No additional effect at higher levels.

Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
