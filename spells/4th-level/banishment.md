# Banishment

*4th-Level Abjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (an item distasteful to the target)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Concentration:** yes
- **Ritual:** no

## Description

You attempt to send one creature that you can see within range to another plane of existence. The target must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or be transported to a harmless demiplane for the duration. While there, the target has the Incapacitated condition.

When the spell ends, the target reappears in the space it left or in the nearest unoccupied space if that space is occupied. If the target is an Aberration, a Celestial, an Elemental, a Fey, or a Fiend, the target doesn't return if the spell lasts for its full duration. The target is instead transported to a random location on a plane associated with its creature type.

## At Higher Levels

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 4.

## Spell Lists

Cleric, Paladin, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

## 2024 Changes

Target now has the Incapacitated condition while banished. Creature types that can be permanently banished are now explicitly listed (Aberration, Celestial, Elemental, Fey, Fiend). They go to a random location on an associated plane rather than their 'home plane.'
