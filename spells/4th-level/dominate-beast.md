# Dominate Beast

*4th-Level Enchantment*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No
## Description

You attempt to beguile a Beast you can see within range. The Beast must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the Charmed condition for the duration. The Beast has Advantage on the save if you or your allies are fighting it.

While the Beast is Charmed, you have a telepathic link with it as long as you and it are on the same plane of existence. You can use this telepathic link to issue commands to the creature while you are conscious (no action required), which it does its best to obey. You can specify a simple and general course of action. If the creature completes the order and doesn't receive further direction from you, it defends and preserves itself to the best of its ability.

Each time the target takes damage, it makes a new Wisdom saving throw against the spell. If the saving throw succeeds, the spell ends.

## At Higher Levels

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The duration is Concentration, up to 10 minutes with a 5th-level slot, up to 1 hour with a 6th-level slot, or up to 8 hours with a 7th-level or higher slot.
