# Compulsion

*4th-Level Enchantment*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No
## Description

Each creature of your choice that you can see within range must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the Charmed condition until the spell ends. A Charmed target moves in a direction of your choice at the start of each of its turns, taking the Dash action to do so. The target can take its action before it moves.

The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the spell on itself on a success.

None.
