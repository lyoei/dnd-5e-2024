# Stoneskin

*4th-Level Transmutation*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (diamond dust worth 100+ GP, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour
- **Concentration:** yes
- **Ritual:** no

## Description

This spell turns the flesh of a willing creature you touch as hard as stone. Until the spell ends, the target has Resistance to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage.

## At Higher Levels

None specified.

## Spell Lists

Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard

## 2024 Changes

No longer specifies 'nonmagical' damage—now grants Resistance to all Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage. School changed from Abjuration to Transmutation.
