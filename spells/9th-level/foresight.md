# Foresight

- **Level:** 9th
- **School:** Divination
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a hummingbird feather)
- **Duration:** 8 hours
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You touch a willing creature and bestow a limited ability to see into the immediate future. For the duration, the target has Advantage on D20 Tests, and other creatures have Disadvantage on attack rolls against it. The spell ends early if you cast it again.

## At Higher Levels

None.

## Spell Lists

Wizard, Druid, Bard, Warlock

## 2024 Changes

- Now grants Advantage on all D20 Tests (not just attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws — same effect, new terminology)
- Druid and Warlock added to spell list
- Spell ends early if you cast it again (explicit re-cast clause)
