# Holy Aura

**Level:** 8th
**School:** Abjuration
**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V, S, M (a tiny reliquary worth 1,000+ GP)
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
**Concentration:** Yes
**Ritual:** No

## Description

Shimmering light surrounds you in a 30-foot Emanation for the duration. Creatures of your choice in the Emanation when you cast this spell shed Dim Light in a 5-foot radius and have Advantage on all saving throws, and other creatures have Disadvantage on attack rolls against them. In addition, when a Fiend or an Undead hits an affected creature with a melee attack roll, the Emanation flashes with blinding light, forcing the attacker to succeed on a Constitution saving throw or have the Blinded condition until the end of its next turn.
