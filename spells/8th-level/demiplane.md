# Demiplane

- **Level:** 8th
- **School:** Conjuration
- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** S
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You create a shadowy door on a flat solid surface that you can see within range. You can open the door to reveal a demiplane that is an empty room 30 feet in each dimension, made of wood or stone (your choice).

When the spell ends, the door disappears, and any creatures or objects inside the demiplane remain trapped there. Each time you cast this spell, you can create a new demiplane or connect the shadowy door to a demiplane you created with a previous casting of this spell. Additionally, if you know the nature and contents of a demiplane created by a casting of this spell by another creature, you can connect the shadowy door to that demiplane instead.
