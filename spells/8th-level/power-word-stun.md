# Power Word Stun

**Level:** 8th
**School:** Enchantment
**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** 60 feet
**Components:** V
**Duration:** Instantaneous
**Concentration:** No
**Ritual:** No

## Description

You speak a word of power that overwhelms the mind of one creature you can see within range. If the target has 150 Hit Points or fewer, it has the Stunned condition. Otherwise, this spell has no effect on it.

The Stunned target makes a Constitution saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the condition on a success.

Not applicable.
