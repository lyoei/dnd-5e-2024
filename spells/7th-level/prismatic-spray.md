# Prismatic Spray

*7th-level Evocation*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Self (60-foot cone)
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

Eight multicolored rays of light flash from your hand. Each ray is a different color and has a different power and purpose. Each creature in a 60-foot Cone must make a Dexterity saving throw. For each target, roll a d8 to determine which color ray affects it: 1-Red (12d6 Fire damage on fail, half on success), 2-Orange (12d6 Acid damage on fail, half on success), 3-Yellow (12d6 Lightning damage on fail, half on success), 4-Green (12d6 Poison damage on fail, half on success), 5-Blue (12d6 Cold damage on fail, half on success), 6-Indigo (Restrained, then Constitution save or Petrified), 7-Violet (Blinded, then Wisdom save or transported to another plane), 8-Special (struck by two rays, roll twice).

## At Higher Levels

None.
