# Symbol

*7th-level Abjuration*

- **Casting Time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (mercury, phosphorus, and powdered diamond and opal with a total value of at least 1,000 GP, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Until dispelled or triggered
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

When you cast this spell, you inscribe a harmful glyph either on a surface (such as a section of floor, a wall, or a table) or within an object that can be closed to conceal the glyph (such as a book, a scroll, or a treasure chest). When triggered, the glyph erupts with magical energy in a 60-foot-radius Sphere centered on the glyph. Each creature in the Sphere must make a saving throw, the type of which depends on the option chosen: Death (10d10 Necrotic, Con save), Discord (creatures argue, Cha save), Fear (Frightened, Wis save), Hopelessness (Overwhelmed with despair, Cha save), Insanity (Driven insane, Int save), Pain (Incapacitated, Con save), Sleep (Unconscious, Wis save), or Stunning (Stunned, Wis save).
