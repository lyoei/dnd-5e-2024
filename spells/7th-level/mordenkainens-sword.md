# Mordenkainen's Sword

*7th-level Evocation*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 90 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a miniature platinum sword with a grip and pommel of copper and zinc, worth 250 GP)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You create a sword-shaped plane of force that hovers within range. It lasts for the duration. When the sword appears, you make a melee spell attack against a target of your choice within 5 feet of the sword. On a hit, the target takes 4d12 Force damage. On each of your turns, you can use a Bonus Action to move the sword up to 30 feet to a spot you can see and repeat the attack against the same target or a different one.

## At Higher Levels

No additional effect at higher levels.

## Spell Lists

Bard, Wizard

## 2024 Changes

Damage significantly increased (was 3d10). Uses Bonus Action to move and attack. Force damage type.
