# Forcecage

*7th-level Evocation*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 100 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (ruby dust worth 1,500 GP, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

An immobile, invisible, cube-shaped prison composed of magical force springs into existence around an area you choose within range. The prison can be a cage or a solid box, as you choose. A prison in the shape of a cage can be up to 20 feet on a side and is made from 1/2-inch diameter bars spaced 1/2 inch apart. A prison in the shape of a box can be up to 10 feet on a side, creating a solid barrier. When the cage appears, any creature that is completely inside the cage's area is trapped. A creature inside the cage can't leave it by nonmagical means. Dispel Magic has no effect on it. When a creature inside the cage tries to use teleportation or interplanar travel, it must make a Charisma saving throw. On success, it can use that magic. On failure, it can't and wastes the use.

## At Higher Levels

No additional effect at higher levels.

## Spell Lists

Bard, Warlock, Wizard

## 2024 Changes

Now requires Concentration. Adds Charisma save for teleportation escape. Material component consumed.
