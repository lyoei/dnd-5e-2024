# Power Word Fortify

*7th-level Enchantment*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You speak a word of power that fortifies up to six creatures you can see within range. Each target gains 120 Temporary Hit Points divided among them as you choose.

## At Higher Levels

None.

## Spell Lists

Bard, Cleric

## 2024 Changes

NEW SPELL in the 2024 PHB. Provides a large pool of Temporary Hit Points distributed among allies.
