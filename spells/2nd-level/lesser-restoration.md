# Lesser Restoration

*2nd-Level Abjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Bonus Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You touch a creature and end one condition on it: Blinded, Deafened, Paralyzed, or Poisoned.

## Spell Lists

Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger

## 2024 Changes

Casting time changed from Action to Bonus Action. No longer removes diseases. Conditions list updated: now removes Blinded, Deafened, Paralyzed, or Poisoned (previously also included disease and one condition from Blinded, Deafened, Paralyzed, Poisoned).
