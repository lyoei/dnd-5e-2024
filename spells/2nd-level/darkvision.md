# Darkvision

*2nd-Level Transmutation*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a dried carrot)
- **Duration:** 8 hours
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

For the duration, a willing creature you touch has Darkvision with a range of 150 feet.

## Spell Lists

- **Classes:** Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard
- **Spell Lists:** Arcane, Primal

## 2024 Changes

Darkvision range increased from 60 feet to 150 feet. Major buff making this much more useful.
