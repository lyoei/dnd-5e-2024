# Barkskin

*2nd-Level Transmutation*

- **Casting Time:** Bonus Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a handful of bark)
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You touch a willing creature. Until the spell ends, the target's skin takes on a rough, bark-like appearance, and the target has an Armor Class of no less than 17.

## Spell Lists

- **Classes:** Druid, Ranger
- **Spell Lists:** Primal

## 2024 Changes

Significantly improved. Casting time changed from Action to Bonus Action. No longer requires Concentration. AC floor increased from 16 to 17. School changed from Abjuration to Transmutation. One of the most buffed spells in the 2024 revision.
