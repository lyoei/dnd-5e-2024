# Alter Self

*2nd-Level Transmutation*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You alter your physical form. Choose one of the following options, the effects of which last for the duration. While the spell lasts, you can take a Magic action to change your choice.

**Aquatic Adaptation.** You grow gills and gain a Swim Speed equal to your Speed, and you can breathe underwater.

**Change Appearance.** You change your appearance. You decide what you look like, including height, weight, facial features, voice, hair length, coloring, and distinguishing characteristics. You can appear as a member of another species, though none of your statistics change.

**Natural Weapons.** You grow claws, fangs, spines, horns, or a different natural weapon. The weapon counts as a Simple Melee weapon with which you have proficiency, deals 1d6 Slashing, Piercing, or Bludgeoning damage (your choice), has the Light property, and you have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls with it.
