# Arcane Vigor

*2nd-Level Abjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You tap into your life force to heal yourself. Roll a number of d8s equal to half the spell slot used to cast this spell (round up). You regain a number of Hit Points equal to the total rolled.

## At Higher Levels

The healing scales automatically — you roll d8s equal to half the slot level used (rounded up).

## Spell Lists

Sorcerer, Wizard

## 2024 Changes

New spell introduced in the 2024 PHB. Did not exist in the 2014 rules. Gives Sorcerers and Wizards a self-healing Bonus Action option.
