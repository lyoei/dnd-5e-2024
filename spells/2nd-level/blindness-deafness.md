# Blindness/Deafness

*2nd-Level Transmutation*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** 1 minute
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

One creature that you can see within range must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the target is Blinded or Deafened (your choice) for the duration. At the end of each of its turns, the target repeats the saving throw, ending the spell on itself on a success.

## At Higher Levels

You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 2.

## Spell Lists

- **Classes:** Bard, Cleric, Sorcerer, Wizard
- **Spell Lists:** Arcane, Divine

## 2024 Changes

School changed from Necromancy to Transmutation. Otherwise functions similarly to the 2014 version.
