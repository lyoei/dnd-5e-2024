# Invisibility

*2nd-Level Illusion*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (an eyelash in gum arabic)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

A creature you touch has the Invisible condition until the spell ends. The spell ends early immediately after the target makes an attack roll, deals damage, or casts a spell.

## At Higher Levels

You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 2.

## Spell Lists

- **Classes:** Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
- **Spell Lists:** Arcane

## 2024 Changes

Now uses Invisible condition terminology. Ends on dealing damage (not just attacking) or casting a spell (simplified from 'casts a spell or attacks').
