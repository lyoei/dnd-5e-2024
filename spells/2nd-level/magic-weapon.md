# Magic Weapon

*2nd-Level Transmutation*

- **Casting Time:** Bonus Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You touch a nonmagical weapon. Until the spell ends, that weapon becomes a magic weapon with a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls. The spell ends early if you cast it again.

## At Higher Levels

The bonus increases to +2 with a level 3-5 spell slot. The bonus increases to +3 with a level 6+ spell slot.

## Spell Lists

Paladin, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard

## 2024 Changes

No longer requires Concentration. Casting time changed from Bonus Action (unchanged). Duration changed to 1 hour (from Concentration, up to 1 hour). Added Ranger and Sorcerer to class list. Major buff by removing Concentration.
