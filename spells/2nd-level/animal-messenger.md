# Animal Messenger

*2nd-Level Enchantment*

- **Casting Time:** Action or Ritual
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a morsel of food)
- **Duration:** 24 hours
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** Yes

## Description

A Tiny Beast of your choice that you can see within range must succeed on a Charisma saving throw, or it is charmed by you for the duration. You specify a location and give a description of one or more creatures there, then speak a message of up to 25 words. The Beast travels toward the location, covering about 50 miles per 24 hours (25 miles if flying).

When the messenger arrives, it delivers your message to a matching creature, replicating your voice. If the messenger doesn't reach its destination before the spell ends, the message is lost and the Beast returns to where you cast the spell.

## At Higher Levels

The duration increases by 48 hours for each spell slot level above 2.
