# Enthrall

*2nd-Level Enchantment*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You weave a distracting string of words, causing creatures of your choice that you can see within range to make a Wisdom saving throw. Any creature you or your companions are fighting automatically succeeds on this save. On a failed save, a target has a -10 penalty to Wisdom (Perception) checks and Passive Perception until the spell ends.

## Spell Lists

- **Classes:** Bard, Warlock
- **Spell Lists:** Arcane

## 2024 Changes

Reworked significantly. Now imposes -10 penalty to Perception checks and Passive Perception instead of imposing Disadvantage on Perception checks. Fighting creatures auto-succeed. Duration changed to Concentration, up to 1 minute.
