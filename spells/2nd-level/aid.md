# Aid

*2nd-Level Abjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a strip of white cloth)
- **Duration:** 8 hours
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

Choose up to three creatures within range. Each target gains 5 Temporary Hit Points.

## At Higher Levels

The number of Temporary Hit Points increases by 5 for each spell slot level above 2.

## Spell Lists

Bard, Cleric, Druid, Ranger

## 2024 Changes

Completely reworked from 2014. No longer increases maximum HP — now grants Temporary Hit Points instead. Added to more class lists (Bard, Druid, Ranger).
