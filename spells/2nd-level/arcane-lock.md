# Arcane Lock

*2nd-Level Abjuration*

- **Casting Time:** Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (gold dust worth 25+ GP, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Until dispelled
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** No

## Description

You touch a closed door, window, gate, container, or other entryway, and it becomes locked for the duration. You and creatures you designate when you cast this spell can open the object normally. You can also set a password that, when spoken within 5 feet of the object, suppresses this spell for 1 minute. Otherwise, the object is impassable until broken or the spell is dispelled or suppressed. Casting Knock on the object suppresses Arcane Lock for 10 minutes.

While affected by this spell, the object is more difficult to break or force open; the DC to break it or pick any locks on it increases by 10.
