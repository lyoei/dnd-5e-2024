# Beast Sense

*2nd-Level Divination*

- **Casting Time:** Action or Ritual
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour
- **Concentration:** Yes
- **Ritual:** Yes

## Description

You touch a willing Beast. For the duration, you can take a Magic action to see and hear through the Beast's senses. While perceiving through the Beast's senses, you benefit from any special senses the Beast has, but you are Blinded and Deafened with regard to your own senses.
