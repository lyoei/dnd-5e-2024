# Augury

*2nd-Level Divination*

- **Casting Time:** 1 minute or Ritual
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (specially marked sticks, bones, cards, or other divinatory tokens worth 25+ GP)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Concentration:** No
- **Ritual:** Yes

## Description

You receive an omen from an otherworldly entity about the results of a course of action you plan to take within the next 30 minutes. The DM chooses from:

- **Weal** for good results
- **Woe** for bad results
- **Weal and Woe** for both
- **Indifference** for results that aren't especially good or bad

The spell doesn't account for possible circumstances that might change the outcome. If you cast the spell 2+ times before finishing a Long Rest, there is a cumulative 25% chance for each casting after the first that you get no answer.

- **Classes:** Cleric, Druid, Wizard
- **Spell Lists:** Divine, Arcane
