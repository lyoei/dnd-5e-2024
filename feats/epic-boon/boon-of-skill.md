# Boon of Skill

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** Epic Boon Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 19+  

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**All-Around Adept.** You gain proficiency in all skills.

**Expertise.** Choose one skill in which you lack Expertise. You gain Expertise in that skill.

## 2024 Changes

This is a new feat introduced in the 2024 PHB. No direct equivalent existed in the 2014 DMG's epic boon system. This is an exceptionally powerful skill feat, granting all skill proficiencies and one Expertise.
