# Boon of Truesight

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** Epic Boon Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 19+  

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**Truesight.** You have Truesight with a range of 60 feet.

## 2024 Changes

This is a new feat introduced in the 2024 PHB. The 2014 DMG had "Boon of Truesight" which granted Truesight 60 ft.; the 2024 version has identical core mechanics but is now a proper feat with an ability score increase.
