# Boon of Speed

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** Epic Boon Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 19+  

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**Escape Artist.** As a Bonus Action, you can take the Disengage action, which also ends the Grappled condition on you.

**Quickness.** Your Speed increases by 30 feet.

## 2024 Changes

This is a new feat introduced in the 2024 PHB. The 2014 DMG had "Boon of Speed" which granted +30 ft. speed; the 2024 version keeps that and adds the Bonus Action Disengage that also ends Grappled.
