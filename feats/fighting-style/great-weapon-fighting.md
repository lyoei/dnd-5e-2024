# Great Weapon Fighting

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** Fighting Style Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Fighting Style feature  

## Description

When you roll damage for an attack you make with a Melee weapon that you are holding with two hands, you can treat any 1 or 2 on a damage die as a 3. The weapon must have the Two-Handed or Versatile property to gain this benefit.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Great Weapon Fighting was a class feature option that let you reroll 1s and 2s on damage dice (and use the new roll). The 2024 version changes the mechanic: instead of rerolling, you simply treat any 1 or 2 as a 3, which is a slight buff since there's no chance of rerolling into another low number.
