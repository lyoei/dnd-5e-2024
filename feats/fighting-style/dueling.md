# Dueling

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** Fighting Style Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Fighting Style feature  

## Description

When you are holding a Melee weapon in one hand and no other weapons, you gain a +2 bonus to damage rolls with that weapon.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Dueling was a class feature option. The 2024 version converts it into a feat. The mechanical effect (+2 damage with a one-handed melee weapon and no other weapons) is unchanged.
