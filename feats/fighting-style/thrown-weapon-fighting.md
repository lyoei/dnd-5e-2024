# Thrown Weapon Fighting

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** Fighting Style Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Fighting Style feature  

## Description

When you hit with a ranged attack roll using a weapon that has the Thrown property, you gain a +2 bonus to the damage roll.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Thrown Weapon Fighting was introduced in Tasha's Cauldron of Everything and also allowed drawing a thrown weapon as part of the attack. The 2024 version converts it into a feat. The +2 damage bonus remains; the free draw benefit was absorbed into general weapon rules.
