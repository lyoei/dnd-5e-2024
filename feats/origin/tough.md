# Tough

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** Origin Feat  
**Prerequisite:** None  

## Description

You are exceptionally hardy, granting you the following benefit:

**Hard to Kill.** Your Hit Point maximum increases by an amount equal to twice your character level when you gain this feat. Whenever you gain a level thereafter, your Hit Point maximum increases by an additional 2 Hit Points.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Tough was a General Feat with identical mechanics. The 2024 version is now an Origin Feat with no mechanical changes beyond reclassification.
