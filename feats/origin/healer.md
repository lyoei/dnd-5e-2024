# Healer

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** Origin Feat  
**Prerequisite:** None  

## Description

You have the training and intuition to administer first aid and other care effectively, granting you the following benefits:

**Battle Medic.** If you have a Healer's Kit, you can expend one use of it and tend to a creature within 5 feet of yourself as a Utilize action. That creature can expend one of its Hit Dice, and you then roll that die. The creature regains a number of Hit Points equal to the roll plus your Proficiency Bonus.

**Healing Rerolls.** Whenever you roll a die to determine the number of Hit Points you restore with a spell or with this feat's Battle Medic benefit, you can reroll the die if it rolls a 1, and you must use the new roll.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Healer was a General Feat that used a Healer's Kit to restore 1d6+4+creature's max Hit Dice in HP (once per rest per creature), and stabilize and grant 1 HP. The 2024 version is now an Origin Feat with a revamped mechanic that lets an ally spend their own Hit Die, plus rerolling 1s on healing dice.
