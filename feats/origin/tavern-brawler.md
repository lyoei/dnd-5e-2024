# Tavern Brawler

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** Origin Feat  
**Prerequisite:** None  

## Description

Accustomed to brawling, you gain the following benefits:

**Enhanced Unarmed Strike.** When you hit with your Unarmed Strike and deal damage, you can deal Bludgeoning damage equal to 1d4 plus your Strength modifier instead of the normal damage of an Unarmed Strike. When you roll a 1 on this die, you can reroll the die and must use the new roll.

**Damage to Objects.** Your Unarmed Strike deals double damage to objects and structures.

**Push.** When you hit a creature with an Unarmed Strike as part of the Attack action on your turn, you can deal damage to the target and push it 5 feet away from you. You can use this benefit only once per turn.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Tavern Brawler was a General Feat that granted +1 Str or Con, proficiency with improvised weapons, d4 Unarmed Strike damage, and a bonus action to attempt a Grapple after hitting with an Unarmed Strike or improvised weapon. The 2024 version is now an Origin Feat, drops the ability score increase, adds rerolling 1s, adds double damage to objects, and replaces the bonus grapple with a push mechanic.
