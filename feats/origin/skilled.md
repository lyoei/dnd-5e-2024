# Skilled

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** Origin Feat  
**Prerequisite:** None  
**Repeatable:** Yes

## Description

You have exceptionally broad learning, granting you the following benefit:

**Skill Proficiencies.** You gain proficiency in any combination of three skills or tools of your choice.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Skilled was a General Feat with identical mechanics. The 2024 version is now an Origin Feat and is explicitly Repeatable (the 2014 version was not marked as repeatable).
