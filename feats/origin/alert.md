# Alert

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** Origin Feat  
**Prerequisite:** None  

## Description

You are always on the lookout for danger, granting you the following benefits:

**Initiative Proficiency.** When you roll Initiative, you can add your Proficiency Bonus to the roll.

**Initiative Swap.** Immediately after you roll Initiative, you can swap your Initiative with the Initiative of one willing ally in the same combat. You can't make this swap if you or the ally has the Incapacitated condition.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Alert was a General Feat (no level requirement) that granted a flat +5 to Initiative, prevented being surprised, and prevented hidden attackers from gaining Advantage on attack rolls against you. The 2024 version replaces the flat +5 with a scaling Proficiency Bonus to Initiative, removes surprise immunity, and adds the Initiative Swap mechanic. It is now an Origin Feat instead of a General Feat.
