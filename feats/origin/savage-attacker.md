# Savage Attacker

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** Origin Feat  
**Prerequisite:** None  

## Description

You have trained to deal particularly damaging strikes, granting you the following benefit:

**Savage Strike.** Once per turn when you hit a target with a weapon, you can roll the weapon's damage dice twice and use either roll against the target.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Savage Attacker was a General Feat with identical mechanics (roll weapon damage twice, use either). The 2024 version is now an Origin Feat with no mechanical changes beyond reclassification.
