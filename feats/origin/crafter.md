# Crafter

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** Origin Feat  
**Prerequisite:** None  

## Description

You are adept at crafting things and bargaining with merchants, granting you the following benefits:

**Tool Proficiency.** You gain proficiency with three different Artisan's Tools of your choice from the Fast Crafting table.

**Discount.** Whenever you buy a nonmagical item, you receive a 20 percent discount on it.

**Craft Temporary Item.** At the end of a Long Rest, you can craft one piece of temporary gear from the Fast Crafting table, provided you have the Artisan's Tools required by the item's entry. The item is made with materials you have on hand, lasts until the end of your next Long Rest, and then falls apart.

## 2024 Changes

This is a new feat introduced in the 2024 PHB. It did not exist in the 2014 edition.
