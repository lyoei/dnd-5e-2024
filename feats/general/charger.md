# Charger

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Strength or Dexterity 13+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Improved Dash.** When you take the Dash action, your Speed increases by 10 feet for that action.

**Charge Attack.** If you move at least 10 feet in a straight line toward a target immediately before hitting it with a melee attack as part of the Attack action, choose one of the following effects: the target takes an extra 1d8 damage from the attack, or the target is pushed up to 10 feet away from you. You can use this benefit only once on each of your turns.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Charger was a feat that let you use a bonus action to make one melee attack or shove after a Dash, dealing +5 damage. The 2024 version adds an ability score increase, Improved Dash (+10 ft.), and changes the charge bonus to 1d8 damage or a 10 ft. push, no longer consuming a bonus action.
