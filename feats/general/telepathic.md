# Telepathic

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Telepathic Communication.** You can speak telepathically to any creature you can see within 60 feet of you. Your telepathic utterances are in a language you know, and the creature understands you only if it knows that language. Your communication doesn't give the creature the ability to respond to you telepathically.

**Detect Thoughts.** You always have the Detect Thoughts spell prepared. You can cast it without a spell slot or spell components, and you must finish a Long Rest before you can cast it in this way again. You can also cast it using spell slots you have of the appropriate level. Your spellcasting ability for this spell is the ability increased by this feat.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 rules (from Tasha's Cauldron of Everything), Telepathic had identical mechanics. The 2024 version adds a Level 4+ requirement and is now in the PHB, but is otherwise unchanged.
