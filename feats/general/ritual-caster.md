# Ritual Caster

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma 13+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Ritual Spells.** You have learned a number of level 1 spells that have the Ritual tag. The number of spells equals your Proficiency Bonus. You always have these spells prepared. You can change which spells you have prepared when you finish a Long Rest by studying your written notes for the new spells (at least 1 minute per spell level for each spell you change).

**Quick Ritual.** With this benefit, you can cast any of your Ritual spells with their normal casting time rather than adding 10 minutes. Once you cast a spell in this way, you can't use this benefit again until you finish a Long Rest.

**Spellcasting Ability.** The spellcasting ability for these spells is the ability increased by this feat.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Ritual Caster required Int or Wis 13+, granted two level 1 Ritual spells from a class list, and let you add other ritual spells you find to your book. The 2024 version completely redesigns it: you get PB number of Ritual spells (scaling), can swap on Long Rest, and gain the Quick Ritual ability to cast one without the extra 10 minutes.
