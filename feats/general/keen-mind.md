# Keen Mind

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Intelligence 13+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Intelligence score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Lore Knowledge.** Choose one of the following skills: Arcana, History, Investigation, Nature, or Religion. If you lack proficiency in the chosen skill, you gain proficiency in it, and if you already have proficiency in it, you gain Expertise in it.

**Quick Study.** You can take the Study action as a Bonus Action.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Keen Mind granted +1 Int, always knowing which way is north, knowing hours until sunrise/sunset, and perfect recall of the last month. The 2024 version completely redesigns it: it now grants skill proficiency/Expertise and Bonus Action Study, making it far more mechanically useful.
