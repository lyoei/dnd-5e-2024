# Observant

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Intelligence or Wisdom 13+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Intelligence or Wisdom score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Keen Observer.** Choose one of the following skills: Insight, Investigation, or Perception. If you lack proficiency in the chosen skill, you gain proficiency in it, and if you already have proficiency in it, you gain Expertise in it.

**Quick Search.** You can take the Search action as a Bonus Action.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Observant granted +1 Int or Wis, the ability to read lips, and +5 to passive Perception and passive Investigation. The 2024 version completely redesigns it: it now grants skill proficiency/Expertise and Bonus Action Search, replacing the passive score bonuses.
