# Lightly Armored

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Armor Training.** You gain proficiency with Light armor and Shields.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Lightly Armored granted +1 Str or Dex and Light armor proficiency only. The 2024 version adds Shield proficiency as part of the feat.
