# Shield Master

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Shield Training

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Shield Bash.** If you attack a creature within 5 feet of you as part of the Attack action and hit with a Melee weapon, you can immediately bash the target with your Shield if you're holding one, forcing the target to make a Strength saving throw (DC 8 + your Strength modifier + your Proficiency Bonus). On a failed save, you either push the target 5 feet away from you or cause it to have the Prone condition. You can use this benefit only once on each of your turns.

**Interpose Shield.** If you are subjected to an effect that allows you to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, you can take a Reaction to take no damage if you succeed on the saving throw, interposing your Shield between yourself and the source of the effect.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Shield Master let you bonus action shove after attacking, add Shield's AC bonus to Dex saves vs. single-target effects, and take no damage on a successful Dex save (instead of half). The 2024 version removes the bonus action requirement for the shove (integrating it into the Attack action), adds the Push or Prone choice, adds an ability score increase, and simplifies the Dex save benefit.
