# Chef

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Constitution or Wisdom score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Cook's Utensils.** You gain proficiency with Cook's Utensils if you don't already have it.

**Replenishing Meal.** As part of a Short Rest, you can cook special food for up to a number of creatures equal to 4 + your Proficiency Bonus. Each creature that eats the food and spends one or more Hit Dice to regain Hit Points regains an extra 1d8 Hit Points.

**Bolstering Treats.** With 1 hour of work or when you finish a Long Rest, you can cook a number of treats equal to your Proficiency Bonus. These special treats last 8 hours after being made. A creature can use a Bonus Action to eat one of those treats to gain Temporary Hit Points equal to your Proficiency Bonus.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 rules (from Tasha's Cauldron of Everything), Chef granted +1 Con or Wis, Cook's Utensils proficiency, feeding up to 4+PB creatures during a Short Rest for +1d8 HP, and creating PB treats for PB Temporary HP. The 2024 version is essentially unchanged mechanically, now formally in the PHB.
