# Sentinel

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Strength or Dexterity 13+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Guardian.** Immediately after a creature within 5 feet of you takes the Disengage action or hits a target other than you with an attack, you can make an Opportunity Attack against that creature.

**Halt.** When you hit a creature with an Opportunity Attack, the creature's Speed becomes 0 for the rest of the current turn.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Sentinel stopped enemy movement on OA hit, let you OA creatures that Disengage, and gave a reaction attack when an adjacent creature attacked someone else. The 2024 version consolidates: Guardian combines the Disengage and ally-defense triggers into one feature, and Halt reduces speed to 0. It adds an ability score increase and prerequisites.
