# Heavily Armored

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Medium Armor Training

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Constitution score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Armor Training.** You gain proficiency with Heavy armor.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Heavily Armored required Medium Armor proficiency, granted +1 Str, and Heavy armor proficiency. The 2024 version adds Level 4+ requirement and lets you choose +1 Str or Con (instead of Str only).
