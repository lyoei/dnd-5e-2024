# Weapon Master

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Weapon Mastery.** You gain the use of the Mastery property of one kind of Simple or Martial weapon of your choice, provided you have proficiency with that weapon. Whenever you finish a Long Rest, you can change the weapon you chose to another eligible weapon.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Weapon Master granted +1 Str or Dex and proficiency with four weapons of your choice. The 2024 version completely redesigns it: instead of granting proficiencies, it now unlocks the new Mastery property system for one weapon, letting non-martial classes access weapon Masteries. The weapon choice can be changed on Long Rest.
