# Heavy Armor Master

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Heavy Armor Training

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Constitution score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Damage Reduction.** While you are wearing Heavy armor, Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage that you take is reduced by an amount equal to your Proficiency Bonus.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Heavy Armor Master required Heavy armor proficiency, granted +1 Str, and reduced nonmagical Bludgeoning/Piercing/Slashing by 3 (flat). The 2024 version changes the reduction to equal Proficiency Bonus (scaling from 2 to 6), applies to ALL BPS damage (not just nonmagical), and lets you choose +1 Str or Con.
