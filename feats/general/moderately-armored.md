# Moderately Armored

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Light Armor Training

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Armor Training.** You gain proficiency with Medium armor.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Moderately Armored required Light armor proficiency, granted +1 Str or Dex, and Medium armor and Shield proficiency. The 2024 version removes Shield proficiency from this feat (Shields are now part of Lightly Armored) and adds a Level 4+ requirement.
