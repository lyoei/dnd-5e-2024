# Skill Expert

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Skill Proficiency.** You gain proficiency in one skill of your choice.

**Expertise.** Choose one skill in which you have proficiency but lack Expertise. You gain Expertise in that skill.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 rules (from Tasha's Cauldron of Everything), Skill Expert had identical mechanics. The 2024 version adds a Level 4+ requirement and is now in the PHB, but is otherwise unchanged.
