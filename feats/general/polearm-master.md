# Polearm Master

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Strength or Dexterity 13+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Butt End.** When you take the Attack action and attack with a Quarterstaff, a Spear, or a weapon that has the Heavy and Reach properties, you can use a Bonus Action to make a melee attack with the opposite end of the weapon. The weapon deals 1d4 Bludgeoning damage on a hit.

**Reactive Strike.** While you are holding a Quarterstaff, a Spear, or a weapon that has the Heavy and Reach properties, you can take a Reaction to make one melee attack against a creature that enters the reach you have with that weapon.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Polearm Master had similar mechanics but applied to glaive, halberd, pike, quarterstaff, and spear specifically. The 2024 version generalizes to "Heavy and Reach" weapons plus Quarterstaff and Spear, adds an ability score increase, and adds a Str/Dex 13+ prerequisite.
