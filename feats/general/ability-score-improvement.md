# Ability Score Improvement

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+  
**Repeatable:** Yes

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 2, or increase two ability scores of your choice by 1. You can't increase an ability score above 20 using this feat.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Ability Score Improvement was a class feature (not a feat). The 2024 version converts it into a General Feat that is always available as an option when you gain a feat at certain class levels. The mechanics (+2 to one score or +1 to two scores, max 20) are unchanged.
