# Dual Wielder

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Strength or Dexterity 13+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Enhanced Dual Wielding.** When you take the Attack action and attack with a weapon that has the Light property, you can make one extra attack as part of the same action with a different weapon, which must be a Melee weapon that lacks the Two-Handed property. You don't add your ability modifier to the extra attack's damage unless that modifier is negative.

**Quick Draw.** You can draw or stow two weapons that lack the Two-Handed property when you would normally be able to draw or stow only one.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Dual Wielder granted +1 AC while dual wielding, let you dual wield non-Light weapons, and allowed drawing two weapons at once. The 2024 version removes the +1 AC, adds an ability score increase, and redesigns the extra attack to work with any non-Two-Handed melee weapon (not just Light weapons).
