# Durable

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Constitution score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Defy Death.** You have Advantage on Death Saving Throws.

**Speedy Recovery.** As a Bonus Action, you can expend one of your Hit Dice, roll the die, and regain a number of Hit Points equal to the roll.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Durable granted +1 Con and set a minimum on Hit Dice healing (minimum of 2× Con modifier). The 2024 version replaces the minimum healing mechanic with Advantage on Death Saves and a Bonus Action to spend a Hit Die in combat, making it significantly more useful.
