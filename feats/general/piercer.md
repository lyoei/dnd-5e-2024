# Piercer

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Puncture.** Once per turn, when you hit a creature with an attack that deals Piercing damage, you can reroll one of the attack's Piercing damage dice and use the higher roll.

**Enhanced Critical.** When you score a Critical Hit that deals Piercing damage to a creature, you can roll one additional Piercing damage die when determining the extra Piercing damage the target takes.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 rules (from Tasha's Cauldron of Everything), Piercer had identical mechanics. The 2024 version adds a Level 4+ requirement and is now in the PHB, but is otherwise unchanged.
