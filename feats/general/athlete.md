# Athlete

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Strength or Dexterity 13+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Climb Speed.** You gain a Climb Speed equal to your Speed.

**Hop Up.** When you have the Prone condition, you can stand up by spending 5 feet of movement rather than half your movement.

**Jumping.** You can make a running Long or High Jump after moving only 5 feet rather than 10 feet.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Athlete granted +1 Str or Dex, standing from prone cost 5 feet, advantage on running jumps, and climbing didn't cost extra movement. The 2024 version adds a full Climb Speed and ability score prerequisite, and removes the advantage on running jumps in favor of reducing the approach distance.
