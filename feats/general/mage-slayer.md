# Mage Slayer

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Concentration Breaker.** When you damage a creature that is concentrating, it has Disadvantage on the saving throw it makes to maintain Concentration.

**Saving Throw Advantage.** When a creature you can see within 60 feet of you casts a spell that forces a saving throw, you can take a Reaction to grant yourself (and allies within 5 feet of you, if any) Advantage on the saving throw. You can use this Reaction a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Mage Slayer gave Advantage on saves vs. spells cast within 5 feet, a reaction melee attack on adjacent spellcasters, and imposed Disadvantage on Concentration saves when you hit a caster. The 2024 version extends the save advantage range to 60 feet (but limited uses), removes the reaction attack, adds an ability score increase, and the Concentration Disadvantage now applies to all damage you deal.
