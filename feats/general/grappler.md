# Grappler

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Strength or Dexterity 13+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Punch and Grab.** When you hit a creature with an Unarmed Strike as part of the Attack action on your turn, you can use both the Damage and the Grapple option. You can use this benefit only once per turn.

**Attack Advantage.** You have Advantage on attack rolls against a creature Grappled by you.

**Fast Wrestler.** You aren't slowed when you move a creature Grappled by you if the creature is your size or smaller.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Grappler required Str 13+ and gave Advantage on attacks against creatures you grapple, plus the ability to pin (restrain both you and the target). The 2024 version removes pinning, adds an ability score increase, adds the Punch and Grab combo, and adds unhindered movement with grappled creatures.
