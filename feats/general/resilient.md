# Resilient

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20. You must choose an ability score in which you lack saving throw proficiency.

**Saving Throw Proficiency.** You gain Saving Throw Proficiency with the chosen ability.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Resilient had identical mechanics (+1 to a chosen ability, gain saving throw proficiency in it). The 2024 version adds a Level 4+ requirement and explicitly requires you to choose an ability where you lack saving throw proficiency, but is otherwise unchanged.
