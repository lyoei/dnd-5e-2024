# Sharpshooter

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Dexterity 13+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Bypass Cover.** Your ranged attacks with weapons ignore Half Cover and Three-Quarters Cover.

**Firing in Melee.** Being within 5 feet of an enemy doesn't impose Disadvantage on your ranged attack rolls with weapons.

**Long-Range Shooting.** Attacking at long range doesn't impose Disadvantage on your ranged attack rolls with weapons.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Sharpshooter ignored cover, ignored long range disadvantage, and offered -5 to hit for +10 damage. The 2024 version removes the -5/+10 mechanic entirely, adds an ability score increase, adds "no melee disadvantage for ranged" (previously unique to Crossbow Expert), and adds Dex 13+ prerequisite.
