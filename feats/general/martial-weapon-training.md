# Martial Weapon Training

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Weapon Proficiency.** You gain proficiency with Martial weapons.

## 2024 Changes

This is a new feat introduced in the 2024 PHB. It replaces the 2014 Weapon Master feat's function of granting weapon proficiencies and focuses specifically on granting full Martial weapon proficiency.
