# Elemental Adept

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Spellcasting or Pact Magic feature  
**Repeatable:** Yes (choose a different damage type each time)

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Energy Mastery.** Choose one of the following damage types: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder. Spells you cast ignore Resistance to damage of the chosen type. In addition, when you roll damage for a spell you cast that deals damage of that type, you can treat any 1 on a damage die as a 2.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Elemental Adept required spellcasting, let you choose a damage type (same list), and had the same mechanics (ignore resistance, treat 1s as 2s). The 2024 version adds an ability score increase and a Level 4+ requirement but is otherwise mechanically identical.
