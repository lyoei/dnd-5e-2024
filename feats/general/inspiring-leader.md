# Inspiring Leader

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Wisdom or Charisma 13+

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Wisdom or Charisma score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Encouraging Performance.** At the end of a Short or Long Rest, you can give an inspiring performance (a speech, a song, or the like). When you do so, choose up to six friendly creatures (which can include yourself) within 30 feet of you who witness the performance. The chosen creatures each gain Temporary Hit Points equal to your character level plus the modifier of the ability score increased by this feat.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Inspiring Leader required Cha 13+ and gave temp HP equal to level + Cha modifier after a 10-minute speech to up to 6 creatures. The 2024 version ties it to a rest instead of a 10-minute speech, lets you choose Wisdom or Charisma, and adds an ability score increase.
