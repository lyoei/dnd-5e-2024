# Medium Armor Master

**Source:** Player's Handbook 2024  
**Category:** General Feat  
**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Medium Armor Training

## Description

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Dexterous Wearer.** While you are wearing Medium armor, you can add 3, rather than 2, to your AC if your Dexterity is 16 or higher.

## 2024 Changes

In the 2014 PHB, Medium Armor Master required Medium armor proficiency, let you add up to +3 Dex to AC (no prerequisite score), and removed Stealth disadvantage from Medium armor. The 2024 version adds a Level 4+ requirement, adds an ability score increase, keeps the +3 Dex cap but now requires Dex 16+, and no longer removes Stealth disadvantage.
