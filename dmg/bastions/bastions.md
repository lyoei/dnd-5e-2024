# Bastions

> *Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024), Chapter 8*

---

## RAW Summary — home, stronghold, and place of power developed over a campaign. Characters acquire Bastions at **level 5**.

## Bastion Turns

A Bastion turn occurs every **7 days** of in-game time. On a Bastion turn, a character issues orders to special facilities or issues the **Maintain** order to the whole Bastion.

If the character isn't present and can't communicate (e.g., via *Sending*), the Bastion defaults to **Maintain**.

## Facility Space

| Space | Maximum Area |
|---|---|
| Cramped | 4 squares (5 ft each) |
| Roomy | 16 squares |
| Vast | 36 squares |

## Basic Facilities

Start with 2 free basic facilities (1 Cramped, 1 Roomy). Options: **Bedroom, Dining Room, Parlor, Courtyard, Kitchen, Storage**.

No game effects — purely for roleplay and verisimilitude.

### Adding Basic Facilities

| Space | Cost | Time |
|---|---|---|
| Cramped | 500 GP | 20 days |
| Roomy | 1,000 GP | 45 days |
| Vast | 3,000 GP | 125 days |

### Enlarging Basic Facilities

| Upgrade | Cost | Time |
|---|---|---|
| Cramped → Roomy | 500 GP | 25 days |
| Roomy → Vast | 2,000 GP | 80 days |

## Special Facilities

Gained through level advancement; **cannot be bought**.

| Level | Total Special Facilities |
|---|---|
| 5 | 2 |
| 9 | 4 |
| 13 | 5 |
| 17 | 6 |

Each level-up: can replace one special facility with another you qualify for.

## Orders

| Order | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Craft** | Hirelings craft an item in the facility |
| **Empower** | Facility confers a temporary empowerment |
| **Harvest** | Hirelings gather a resource |
| **Maintain** | All hirelings focus on maintenance (triggers Bastion Event roll) |
| **Recruit** | Hirelings recruit creatures (including Bastion Defenders) |
| **Research** | Hirelings gather information |
| **Trade** | Hirelings buy/sell goods or services |

## Key Facility Highlights

### Arcane Study (Lv 5)
- **Charm:** After Long Rest in Bastion, cast *Identify* without slot/components (7 days)
- **Craft:** Arcane Focus (7 days, free) / Book (7 days, 10 GP) / Magic Item (Lv 9+, Common/Uncommon from Arcana tables)

### Barrack (Lv 5)
- **Recruit:** Up to 4 Bastion Defenders per order (free, max 12 per Roomy Barrack, 25 if Vast)

### Garden (Lv 5)
- Types: Decorative (bouquets/perfume/candles), Food (100 rations), Herb (10 Healer's Kits or 1 Potion of Healing), Poison (2 Antitoxin or 1 Basic Poison)

### Sanctuary (Lv 5)
- **Charm:** Cast *Healing Word* once without slot (7 days)

### Workshop (Lv 5)
- **Source of Inspiration:** After Short Rest in Workshop, gain Heroic Inspiration (1/Long Rest)
- Comes with 6 types of Artisan's Tools

### Greenhouse (Lv 9)
- **Fruit of Restoration:** 3 magical fruits daily (each = *Lesser Restoration*). Picked fruits expire in 24h.
- **Harvest:** Potion of Healing (greater) or poison (Assassin's Blood, Malice, Pale Tincture, Truth Serum)

### Sacristy (Lv 9)
- **Spell Refreshment:** Regain 1 expended spell slot (level 5 or lower) after Short Rest in Bastion (1/Long Rest)

### Scriptorium (Lv 9)
- **Craft Spell Scroll:** Cleric or Wizard spells of level 3 or lower

### Training Area (Lv 9)
- Expert Trainers (7-day training, benefit lasts 7 days):
  - **Battle Expert:** Reduce attack damage by 1d4 (Reaction)
  - **Skills Expert:** Proficiency in Acrobatics/Athletics/Performance/Sleight of Hand/Stealth
  - **Tools Expert:** Proficiency with one tool
  - **Unarmed Combat Expert:** +1d4 Bludgeoning on Unarmed Strike
  - **Weapon Expert:** Proficiency with a weapon, or use its mastery property

### Gaming Hall (Lv 9)
- Trade → gambling den (7 days). Roll d100: 01–50: 1d6×10 GP / 51–85: 2d6×10 GP / 86–95: 4d6×10 GP / 96–00: 10d6×10 GP

### Archive (Lv 13)
- **Research:** Equivalent of *Legend Lore* spell (7 days)
- **Reference Book:** Advantage on specific Intelligence checks in Bastion

### Pub (Lv 13)
- **Spy Network:** Info on events within 10 mi; locate creatures within 50 mi (7 days)
- **Pub Special** (magical beverages, 24h effect): Enlarge / Spider Climb / Darkvision 60 ft / Necrotic Resistance / Auto-succeed Frightened saves

### Menagerie (Lv 13)
- Houses up to 4 Large creatures; creatures count as Bastion Defenders
- Costs: CR 0–1/8: 50 GP / CR 1/4: 250 GP / CR 1/2: 500 GP / CR 1: 1,000 GP / CR 2: 2,000 GP / CR 3: 3,500 GP

### Reliquary (Lv 13)
- **Charm:** Cast *Greater Restoration* once without slot/components (7 days)
- **Talisman:** Replaces Material components ≤ 1,000 GP for one spell (not consumed)

### Demiplane (Lv 17)
- Extradimensional stone room; only you and hirelings can open door
- **Empower:** Temp HP = 5 × level after Long Rest in Demiplane (7 days)
- **Fabrication:** Create nonmagical object ≤ 5 ft, ≤ 5 GP value (1/Long Rest)

### Sanctum (Lv 17)
- **Charm:** Cast *Heal* once without slot (7 days)
- **Empower:** Target gains Temp HP = your level after each Long Rest (7 days)
- **Sanctum Recall:** *Word of Recall* can target Sanctum; one arrival gets *Heal*

### War Room (Lv 17)
- Up to 10 lieutenants (Warrior Veterans)
- **Recruit: Soldiers** — each lieutenant musters 100 Guards (or 20 mounted) in 7 days; 1 GP/day per guard + horse

### Guildhall (Lv 17)
- ~50 guild members; various guild assignments (Adventurers', Bakers', Brewers', Masons', Shipbuilders', Thieves')

## Defensive Walls

20 ft high; each 5-ft square: 250 GP, 10 days to build. Fully enclosed = reduce Attack event defender losses by 2 dice.

## Bastion Events (on Maintain order)

| d100 | Event |
|---|---|
| 01–50 | All Is Well |
| 51–55 | Attack (6d6; each 1 = 1 Defender dies) |
| 56–58 | Criminal Hireling (bribe 1d6×100 GP or lose hireling) |
| 59–63 | Extraordinary Opportunity (pay 500 GP → reroll event) |
| 64–72 | Friendly Visitors (1d6×100 GP for facility use) |
| 73–76 | Guest (renown letter / sanctuary / mercenary / friendly monster) |
| 77–79 | Lost Hirelings (facility offline 1 turn) |
| 80–83 | Magical Discovery (free Uncommon Potion or Scroll) |
| 84–91 | Refugees (2d4 refugees, 1d6×100 GP payment) |
| 92–98 | Request for Aid (send Defenders, 1d6 each, need 10+ total) |
| 99–00 | Treasure (art object or magic item) |

## Fall of a Bastion

- **Divestiture:** Voluntarily give up; hirelings released, Bastion looted
- **Neglect:** No orders for [character level] consecutive turns → hirelings abandon
- **Ruination:** *Deck of Many Things* — Ruin card

New Bastion can be established; retains special facility count per level.
